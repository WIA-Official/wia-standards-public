💨 Key Technologies

  • 💦 High-Pressure Misting - Ultra-fine nutrient droplets (5-50 microns) for maximum absorption
  • 🌱 Suspended Root Systems - Roots exposed to air for optimal oxygen access
  • ⏱️ Precision Mist Cycles - Programmable spray intervals (every 2-5 minutes)
  • 📊 Real-Time Monitoring - IoT sensors for EC, pH, temperature, and humidity
  • 🔬 Nutrient Precision - Direct root absorption with minimal waste
  • 🚀 NASA Heritage - Space agriculture technology adapted for Earth

🌍 Applications

  • 🏢 Commercial Farms - High-value crops (herbs, leafy greens, strawberries)
  • 🏠 Home Gardens - Compact systems for residential growing
  • 🚀 Space Agriculture - Zero-gravity food production for spacecraft
  • 🏜️ Desert Farming - Extreme water efficiency in arid regions
