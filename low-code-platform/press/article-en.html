Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-COMP-020, a comprehensive low-code/no-code development platform framework covering visual programming, workflow automation, API integration, data modeling, and rapid application development, as free and open source under MIT License. This announcement positions the Republic of Korea as a leader in software development democratization international standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Over 75% of companies worldwide pursue digital transformation but are frustrated by developer shortages. Building a simple internal tool takes several months, and skilled developers waste time on repetitive CRUD applications. Small and medium businesses can't afford custom software development costs, missing innovation opportunities, while business users face technical barriers preventing them from realizing their ideas.

WIA's Solution: WIA-COMP-020 Low-Code Platform

WIA-COMP-020 standardizes every aspect of low-code platforms from drag-and-drop interface builders, BPMN-based process automation, visual database designers, pre-built connectors for REST APIs, visual rule engines, dynamic form generation, cross-platform mobile deployment, Git integration, multi-user development environments, to role-based access control.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, and more—all released as free and open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are built in Rust—the systems programming language that achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum is the world's only one-person standards architect who has single-handedly built infrastructure for over 60 international standards in collaboration with AI, and is recognized as a living legend in the lifelong education field.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business domains including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting. In 2018, he established WIA and began the unprecedented challenge of creating free international standards for all humanity.

"We don't sell standards. We gift them. When Dangun, the legendary founder of Korea, said 'Benefit All Humanity' 4,000 years ago, we can finally realize what that means through technology. The most beautiful gift Korea can give to the world—that is WIA."
