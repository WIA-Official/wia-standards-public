WIA-MED-022 Nutrition Tracking

Comprehensive Nutrition Tracking and Management Standard

"Hongik Ingan: Proper nutrition is a fundamental right for everyone's health and well-being."

Standard Introduction

WIA-MED-022 is a comprehensive standard for accurately tracking and managing individual nutritional intake. It covers all aspects of modern nutrition management including calorie tracking, macronutrient analysis, food database integration, barcode scanning, AI-powered meal photo recognition, personalized dietary recommendations, allergy management, and diabetes nutrition planning.

This ebook provides practical guidance for developers, dietitians, healthcare professionals, and healthcare organizations looking to implement nutrition tracking systems.
