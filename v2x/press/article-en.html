Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-AUTO-003, the V2X Communication standard, as free and open source under MIT License. This announcement positions the Republic of Korea as a leader in international standards for this domain.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

1.35 million people die annually in traffic accidents worldwide, most preventable. Vehicles cannot communicate with each other, remaining unaware of danger until collision, and traffic signals and infrastructure don't share real-time information.

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum, CEO:
"WIA-AUTO-003 defines a comprehensive V2X communication framework covering V2V, V2I, V2P, and V2N. Supporting both DSRC and C-V2X (5G), it enables collision avoidance, cooperative driving, and traffic optimization with sub-10ms latency."
