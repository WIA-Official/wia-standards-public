WIA Announces Ticketing System Standard - The Future of Digital Tickets

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the WIA-IND-019 Ticketing System standard. This standard revolutionizes the ticketing industry through universal digital ticket formats, QR code standards, dynamic pricing, blockchain-based fraud prevention, and multi-platform integration.

The Digital Revolution in Ticketing

The Republic of Korea's entertainment and sports industries are rapidly growing. WIA-IND-019 integrates tickets for all sectors - concerts, sports events, performances, exhibitions, and transportation - into a standardized digital format. Blockchain verification, biometric authentication, real-time seat management, and AI-based dynamic pricing maximize user experience and eliminate fraud at its source.

Key Features:
  • Universal Ticket Format: Standardized digital tickets compatible across all platforms
  • Blockchain Verification: Eliminate scalping with immutable ownership records
  • Dynamic Pricing: AI calculates optimal prices based on demand and time
  • Secure Secondary Market: Official resale marketplace prevents excessive markups
  • Accessibility Support: Comprehensive ticketing options for people with disabilities

Fair Ticketing Without Fraud

Ticket scalping and fraud have long been chronic industry problems. WIA-IND-019 fundamentally blocks ticket counterfeiting and duplicate sales through blockchain immutability, time-based QR code encryption, location-based verification, and NFC integration. Only legitimate purchasers can use tickets, and all transactions are transparently recorded.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The Ticketing System standard enables everyone to enjoy culture and sports at fair prices." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this technology will eradicate scalpers, benefit both organizers and audiences, and increase accessibility so more people can enjoy cultural benefits."

"Culture and sports belong to everyone. The WIA-IND-019 standard ensures fair and transparent ticketing, enabling everyone to enjoy entertainment at legitimate prices and providing equal access opportunities for people with disabilities and underserved communities."
