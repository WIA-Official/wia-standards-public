Key Features

Advanced technology for cultural heritage preservation

📸

3D Photogrammetry

High-resolution 3D models created from photographs using advanced photogrammetry algorithms. Capture artifacts with sub-millimeter accuracy for detailed analysis and virtual preservation.

Material Analysis

AI-powered material identification and composition analysis. Determine age, origin, and conservation requirements through spectroscopic and microscopic imaging.

🎨

AI Restoration

Machine learning algorithms for digital restoration of damaged artifacts. Reconstruct missing parts, restore faded colors, and remove deterioration digitally while preserving the original.

Metadata Standards

Comprehensive metadata schema for cultural artifacts including provenance, historical context, condition reports, and conservation history. Compatible with international standards.

Virtual Exhibitions

Create immersive virtual museum experiences with VR/AR support. Enable global access to cultural heritage through web-based 3D viewers and interactive storytelling.

🔒

Blockchain Provenance

Immutable provenance tracking using blockchain technology. Record ownership history, authenticity verification, and conservation records in a tamper-proof distributed ledger.
