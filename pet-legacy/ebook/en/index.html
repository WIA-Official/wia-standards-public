About This Standard

The WIA-PET-LEGACY standard defines protocols for preserving pet memories, creating digital memorials, and managing legacy data. This ensures that the bond between pets and their families is honored and preserved beyond the pet's lifetime.

Key Features

  • [i] Digital memorial pages with customizable themes
  • [i] Life timeline and biography management
  • [i] AI-powered photo selection and curation
  • [i] Tribute and virtual offering systems
  • [i] Printable memory book generation
  • [i] Long-term archival with 100+ year preservation

Legacy Status Types

Status Description
PLANNING Legacy being prepared while pet is living
DORMANT Ready but not activated, pet still living
ACTIVE Memorial activated and accessible
ARCHIVED Long-term preservation storage
