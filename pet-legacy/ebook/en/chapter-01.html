1.1 The Importance of Pet Legacy

Pets are family members who enrich our lives with unconditional love, companionship, and countless precious moments. When they pass, we often struggle to preserve and honor their memory in a meaningful way.

Why Legacy Planning Matters

  • [OK] Preserves precious memories before they fade
  • [OK] Provides structured way to process grief
  • [OK] Creates lasting tribute for future generations
  • [OK] Ensures important records are maintained
  • [OK] Facilitates healing through remembrance

1.2 What is Pet Legacy Management?

Pet legacy management encompasses all activities related to documenting, preserving, and honoring a pet's life. This includes biographical information, photos, videos, health records, and memorable moments.

Legacy Components

Component Description Purpose
Biography Pet's life story and personality Preserve their unique character
Timeline Important events and milestones Document life journey
Media Gallery Photos, videos, audio recordings Visual memory preservation
Memorial Page Public or private tribute Share and remember
Tributes Messages from loved ones Community remembrance
Archive Long-term data preservation Future access guarantee

1.3 Legacy Lifecycle

PET LEGACY LIFECYCLE
====================

Phase 1: PLANNING
  While pet is living, begin collecting:
  - Photos and videos
  - Favorite memories
  - Personality traits
  - Important dates

Phase 2: DORMANT
  Legacy prepared but not activated:
  - All content uploaded
  - Memorial configured
  - Access settings defined
  - Ready when needed

Phase 3: ACTIVE
  Memorial goes live after passing:
  - Tributes accepted
  - Visitors can view
  - Candles can be lit
  - Updates possible

Phase 4: ARCHIVED
  Long-term preservation:
  - Secured for 100+ years
  - Inheritable by family
  - Export available
  - Permanent storage

1.5 Privacy and Access Control

Visibility Levels

ACCESS CONTROL LEVELS
=====================

PRIVATE:
  - Only the creator can view
  - Not discoverable by others
  - Maximum privacy protection

FAMILY:
  - Shared with designated family members
  - Requires invitation or link
  - Collaborative editing possible

MEMORIAL (SHARED):
  - Visible to invited guests
  - Tributes can be submitted
  - Moderation available

PUBLIC:
  - Open to anyone with link
  - Discoverable in search
  - Maximum reach for tributes
