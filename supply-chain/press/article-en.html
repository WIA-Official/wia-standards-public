WIA Announces Supply Chain Standard - Blockchain-based Transparency Revolution

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the WIA-IND-023 Supply Chain Management standard. This standard realizes complete digital transformation of global supply chains by integrating blockchain-based traceability, AI risk management, real-time visibility, and sustainability tracking.

Complete Supply Chain Transparency

The Republic of Korea's manufacturing and logistics industries are central to global supply chains. WIA-IND-023 records every stage from raw materials to end consumers on blockchain, verifying product authenticity and movement paths. Supplier management, order tracking, inventory optimization, and ESG compliance monitoring are automated, dramatically improving efficiency and reliability.

Key Features:
  • Blockchain Traceability: Ensure product authenticity with immutable records
  • AI Risk Management: Predict and respond to supply chain risks proactively
  • Real-time Visibility: Monitor entire supply chain in real-time
  • Sustainability Tracking: Manage carbon footprint and ESG scores
  • Automated Procurement: Automate orders and payments with smart contracts

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The Supply Chain standard enables transparent and sustainable global commerce." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this technology will benefit manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, and consumers alike, contributing to building ethical and environmentally friendly supply chains."

"Supply chains are the circulatory system of the modern economy. The WIA-IND-023 standard makes this system transparent and efficient, enabling all participants to conduct trustworthy transactions and realize responsible consumption and production that protects our planet."
