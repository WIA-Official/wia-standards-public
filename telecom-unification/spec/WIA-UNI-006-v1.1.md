## What's New in v1.1

### Major Features

1. **VoLTE Enhancement** - Improved voice quality and reliability
2. **Wi-Fi Calling** - Make calls over Wi-Fi when cellular coverage is weak
3. **eSIM Support** - Digital SIM cards for easier device activation
4. **Enhanced Translation** - AI-powered translation with 98% accuracy
5. **Video Conferencing** - Multi-party video calls (up to 50 participants)

---

## 1. VoLTE Enhancement

### 1.1 Improved Codec

- **New Codec**: EVS Full Band (FB) mode
- **Bitrate**: Up to 128 kbps for HD+ voice quality
- **Bandwidth**: 20 Hz - 20 kHz (full audio spectrum)
- **Quality**: MOS 4.5+ (out of 5.0)
- **Noise Cancellation**: AI-powered background noise reduction

### 1.2 Handover Improvements

- **Seamless Handover**: Zero packet loss during DMZ crossing
- **Faster Setup**: Call setup time reduced to &lt;1 second
- **Better Reliability**: 99.9% call success rate (up from 99.5%)

---

## 2. Wi-Fi Calling

### 2.1 Specification

- **Technology**: Voice over Wi-Fi (VoWiFi) / IMS
- **Quality**: Same as VoLTE when on Wi-Fi
- **Handover**: Seamless transition between Wi-Fi and cellular
- **Emergency**: 112/119 works over Wi-Fi with approximate location
- **Cross-Border**: Wi-Fi calling works across DMZ

### 2.2 Use Cases

- Indoor coverage improvement (offices, homes, basements)
- International travelers using Korean Wi-Fi
- Remote areas with Wi-Fi but weak cellular signal
- Emergency backup when cellular network is congested

---

## 3. eSIM Support

### 3.1 Digital SIM Cards

- **Technology**: GSMA eSIM specification
- **Activation**: Remote provisioning over-the-air
- **Multi-Profile**: Support multiple carriers on one device
- **Cross-Border**: Single eSIM works in both North and South Korea
- **Quick Switch**: Change carrier without physical SIM swap

### 3.2 Implementation

- **Device Support**: All 5G phones manufactured after 2027
- **Provisioning**: QR code or UKT app activation
- **Security**: PKI-based secure download
- **Backup**: Physical SIM option still available

---

## 4. Enhanced Translation

### 4.1 AI-Powered Translation

- **Technology**: Transformer neural networks
- **Accuracy**: 98% (up from 95% in v1.0)
- **Languages**: Korean (North/South dialects) + 50 international languages
- **Latency**: &lt;100ms (down from &lt;200ms)
- **Context-Aware**: Understands formal vs. casual speech

### 4.2 New Features

- **Emotion Preservation**: Maintains speaker's emotional tone
- **Idiom Translation**: Handles regional idioms and expressions
- **Multi-Speaker**: Supports group conversations
- **Learning**: Adapts to individual speaking patterns over time

---

## 5. Video Conferencing

### 5.1 Multi-Party Video

- **Participants**: Up to 50 simultaneous participants
- **Quality**: Adaptive based on network conditions
  - HD (720p) for 2-10 participants
  - SD (480p) for 11-50 participants
- **Layout**: Grid view, speaker view, gallery view
- **Recording**: Cloud recording with 30-day retention (opt-in)

### 5.2 Features

- **Screen Sharing**: Share screens across DMZ
- **Virtual Backgrounds**: Custom backgrounds for privacy
- **Chat**: Text chat alongside video
- **Whiteboard**: Collaborative drawing board
- **Translation**: Real-time subtitle translation for all participants
