About This Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Vision AI, based on the WIA-AI-021 standard. This book will take you on a journey from the fundamentals of image processing to advanced topics like 3D vision and production deployment.

Whether you're a beginner looking to understand computer vision or an experienced developer seeking to implement production-ready systems, this book provides practical knowledge, code examples, and real-world insights.
