7.2 Cross-Standard Integration

WIA-CLIMATE Integration

Link reef restoration efforts to carbon credits. Healthy coral reefs contribute to blue carbon sequestration, and WIA-REEF restoration data can be verified through WIA-CLIMATE protocols.

WIA-BLOCKCHAIN Integration

Use blockchain verification for immutable reef health records. Critical assessments and restoration milestones can be anchored to blockchain for permanent, tamper-proof documentation.

WIA-ACCESS-CONTROL Integration

Manage access to marine protected areas. Control who can conduct research, tourism, or restoration activities at certified reef sites.
