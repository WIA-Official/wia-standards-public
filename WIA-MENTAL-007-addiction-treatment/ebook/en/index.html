About This Ebook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Digital Addiction Treatment and Recovery Systems. This ebook explores cutting-edge technologies and evidence-based approaches for addressing substance use disorders, behavioral addictions, and supporting long-term recovery through digital interventions.

Addiction is a chronic, relapsing condition that affects millions worldwide. Traditional treatment approaches, while effective, face significant barriers including accessibility, stigma, cost, and limited availability of specialized care. Digital health technologies offer transformative potential to overcome these barriers and extend the reach of evidence-based treatments to those who need them most.

This standard (WIA-MENTAL-007) provides a comprehensive framework for developing, implementing, and evaluating digital addiction treatment systems that are clinically validated, privacy-preserving, and designed to support recovery at every stage of the journey.
