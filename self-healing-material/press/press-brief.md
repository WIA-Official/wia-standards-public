# WIA-MAT-SH-001 — Self Healing Material (Press Brief)

**WIA Standards releases canonical Self Healing Material envelope shape**

The WIA Standards Committee has published the WIA-MAT-SH-001 standard covering the canonical envelope shape, API surface, protocol exchanges, and ecosystem integration for self healing material hosts. The standard composes with the wider WIA Standards family for cross-standard audit transport, identity, and federation.
