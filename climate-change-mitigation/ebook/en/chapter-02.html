⚠️ The Greenwashing Problem

Without standardized measurement and verification, organizations can make misleading climate claims. Studies show that up to 40% of corporate climate pledges lack credible implementation plans, highlighting the need for robust standards like WIA-CLIMATE-CHANGE-MITIGATION.

2.6 Chapter Summary

Key Takeaways

  • Scope 3 emissions remain the biggest measurement challenge
  • Critical technologies need continued R&D and scale-up
  • Carbon pricing coverage and levels are insufficient
  • $3 trillion annual investment gap must be closed
  • Just transition requires attention to workers and communities
