7.1 Third-Party Verification

Third-party verification ensures the credibility and accuracy of climate claims. WIA-CLIMATE-CHANGE-MITIGATION requires independent verification at MAL-2 and above.

7.1.1 Verification Levels

Level Assurance Scope
Limited Assurance Moderate confidence Analytical procedures, inquiry
Reasonable Assurance High confidence Detailed testing, site visits
Full Audit Highest confidence Comprehensive verification
