8.2 Certification Process

Step Activity Duration
1. Application Submit intent and scope 1 week
2. Gap Assessment Identify compliance gaps 2-4 weeks
3. Implementation Close gaps, build systems 3-24 months
4. Documentation Prepare evidence package 2-4 weeks
5. Verification Third-party audit 2-6 weeks
6. Certification Certificate issued 1-2 weeks

8.9 Conclusion

WIA-CLIMATE-CHANGE-MITIGATION provides a comprehensive framework for organizations to measure, reduce, and verify their greenhouse gas emissions. By following the four-phase architecture—Measurement, Reduction, Technology, and Verification—organizations can achieve meaningful climate impact while demonstrating credible progress to stakeholders.

Climate change is humanity's greatest challenge, but it is also our greatest opportunity for transformation. Through standardized approaches like WIA-CLIMATE-CHANGE-MITIGATION, we can accelerate the transition to a low-carbon economy while ensuring no one is left behind.
