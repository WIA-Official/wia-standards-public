5.1 Reduction Hierarchy

Effective climate mitigation follows a clear hierarchy: avoid emissions first, reduce where unavoidable, and offset only residual emissions.

5.2 Energy Efficiency

Sector Efficiency Measures Typical Savings
Buildings LED lighting, HVAC optimization, insulation 20-40%
Industry Motor drives, heat recovery, process control 15-30%
Transportation Route optimization, fleet modernization 10-25%
Data Centers Cooling optimization, server virtualization 30-50%

5.6 Chapter Summary

Key Takeaways

  • Follow the avoid-reduce-substitute-offset hierarchy
  • Energy efficiency is the most cost-effective first step
  • Multiple renewable procurement options available
  • Electrification enables deep decarbonization
  • Science-based targets require 4.2% annual reduction for 1.5°C
