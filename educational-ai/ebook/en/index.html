About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the Educational AI Standard (WIA-EDU-005). This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about implementing AI-powered educational systems, from intelligent tutoring to automated grading and personalized learning experiences.

Educational AI is transforming how we teach and learn, making education more accessible, personalized, and effective. This standard provides a framework for building ethical, effective, and scalable AI educational systems that benefit all learners.

弘益人間 (홍익인간) - Our mission is to broadly benefit humanity through accessible, intelligent education technology.
