About This Standard

This standard is developed by WIA (World Certification Industry Association) as an international standard for digital funeral services, virtual memorials, and digital legacy preservation. It provides comprehensive guidelines to modernize end-of-life ceremonies while preserving dignity, cultural sensitivity, and meaningful connections in the digital age.

As society increasingly embraces digital technologies, the funeral industry faces unique opportunities to expand accessibility, preserve memories eternally, and connect global communities during times of loss. This standard addresses the technical, ethical, and cultural dimensions of digital funeral services.

WIA's digital funeral standards integrate best practices from grief counseling, memorial service design, livestreaming technology, digital archiving, and privacy protection. This enables funeral homes, memorial service providers, and families to offer compassionate, technologically-enhanced services that honor the deceased while supporting the bereaved.
