4.5 Conclusion

This chapter has provided comprehensive coverage of thermal energy storage, including theoretical foundations, practical implementations, industry best practices, and future developments. Understanding these concepts is essential for anyone involved in designing, deploying, or operating energy storage systems.

The WIA-ENE-010 standard provides the technical framework to ensure thermal energy storage meet the highest standards of performance, safety, and reliability. As the energy storage industry continues its rapid evolution, staying informed about the latest developments and maintaining adherence to established standards becomes increasingly important.
