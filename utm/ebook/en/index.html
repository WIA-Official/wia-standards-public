About This E-Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Unmanned Traffic Management (UTM) systems. This e-book represents the culmination of years of research, development, and real-world implementation experience from leading aviation authorities, technology companies, and regulatory bodies worldwide.

UTM is revolutionizing how we manage low-altitude airspace, enabling safe and efficient operations of drones and other unmanned aircraft systems (UAS). From package delivery and infrastructure inspection to emergency response and agricultural monitoring, UTM provides the critical infrastructure needed to support the rapidly growing drone industry while ensuring safety for all airspace users.

This guide covers everything from fundamental concepts to advanced implementation strategies, making it valuable for regulators, operators, technology developers, and anyone interested in the future of aviation.

  • FAA UTM Framework: U.S. regulatory approach to unmanned traffic management
  • ASTM F3411: Remote ID and tracking standard
  • ASTM F3548: USS network and data exchange specification
  • RTCA SC-228: Detect and Avoid performance standards

  • EASA U-Space: European Union's UTM framework
  • JARUS: Joint Authorities for Rulemaking on Unmanned Systems

Terminology Note

Throughout this book, we use "UTM" (Unmanned Traffic Management) which is the U.S. terminology. In Europe, the equivalent concept is called "U-Space." While there are some implementation differences, the core principles and technologies are largely aligned, and this book covers both perspectives where relevant.
