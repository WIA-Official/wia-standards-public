3.1 SWIFT Network Integration

SWIFT (Society for Worldwide Interbank Financial Telecommunication) remains the dominant network for international payments, connecting over 11,000 financial institutions across 200+ countries. WIA-FIN-014 provides comprehensive SWIFT integration.

SWIFT GPI (Global Payments Innovation)

SWIFT GPI enhances traditional SWIFT with:

  • Real-Time Tracking: End-to-end payment tracking with unique UETR (Unique End-to-End Transaction Reference)
  • Same-Day Settlement: Most GPI payments settle within hours, many within minutes
  • Fee Transparency: Upfront disclosure of all fees and charges
  • Confirmation of Credit: Confirmation that funds have been credited to beneficiary account

3.2 SEPA Instant Payments

SEPA (Single Euro Payments Area) Instant Credit Transfer enables real-time EUR payments across 36 European countries. Payments are settled in less than 10 seconds, 24/7/365.

SEPA Instant Key Features

  • Speed: Maximum 10 seconds from initiation to credit
  • Availability: 24/7/365 operations including weekends and holidays
  • Amount Limit: Up to €100,000 per transaction (expanding to €200,000)
  • Irrevocability: Once sent, payments cannot be recalled
  • Certainty: Immediate confirmation of funds availability

3.4 Real-Time Payment Systems

Many countries have deployed real-time payment systems for instant domestic transfers. WIA-FIN-014 enables cross-border connectivity between these systems.

Major RTP Systems

  • FedNow (USA): Federal Reserve's instant payment service
  • RTP (USA): The Clearing House real-time payments
  • Faster Payments (UK): UK's instant payment service
  • UPI (India): Unified Payments Interface
  • PIX (Brazil): Brazilian instant payment system
  • PayNow (Singapore): Real-time fund transfer service
  • PromptPay (Thailand): Thai national e-payment system
