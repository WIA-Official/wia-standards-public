Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the WIA-TIME-008 standard under MIT License for free. WIA-TIME-008 defines temporal reactor design, chrono-dynamo systems, time crystal cells, entropy harvesting, and CTC loop generators. Achieves 60-85% efficiency for multi-stage reactors, 80-95% for time crystals, and 95-99.9% for CTC loops
