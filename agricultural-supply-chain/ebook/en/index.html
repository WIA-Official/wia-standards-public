About This Ebook

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA-AGRI-014 Agricultural Supply Chain Standard, providing everything you need to implement transparent, efficient, and blockchain-verified farm-to-fork traceability systems.

Total Chapters: 8 | Estimated Reading Time: 4-6 hours

Table of Contents

  • Chapter 1: Introduction to Agricultural Supply Chains
    The evolution of food traceability, current challenges, and the need for digital transformation in agricultural supply chains
  • Chapter 2: Current Challenges & Pain Points
    Food safety crises, lack of transparency, cold chain failures, and the economic impact of supply chain inefficiencies
  • Chapter 3: WIA-AGRI-014 Standard Overview
    Architecture, core components, and the four-phase implementation approach
  • Chapter 4: Phase 1 - Data Format
    Shipment data structures, cold chain monitoring formats, provenance records, and quality control data
  • Chapter 5: Phase 2 - API Interface
    REST APIs for shipment management, cold chain monitoring, provenance verification, and quality control
  • Chapter 6: Phase 3 - Protocol
    IoT sensor protocols (MQTT, CoAP), blockchain integration, real-time streaming, and security protocols
  • Chapter 7: Phase 4 - Integration
    WIA ecosystem integration, ERP systems, warehouse management, retailer POS, and regulatory compliance
  • Chapter 8: Implementation & Certification
    Implementation roadmap, best practices, case studies, and WIA certification process

  • Deploy IoT sensors for real-time cold chain monitoring
  • Integrate with major ERP, WMS, and POS systems
