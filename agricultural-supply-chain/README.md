# WIA-AGRI-014: Agricultural Supply Chain Standard

📦 **Farm-to-Fork Digital Traceability**

## Overview

The WIA-AGRI-014 Agricultural Supply Chain Standard provides a comprehensive framework for transparent, efficient, and blockchain-verified farm-to-fork traceability systems.

## Key Features

### 📊 Shipment Tracking
- Real-time location tracking from farm to retail
- Estimated arrival times
- Complete journey timeline

### ❄️ Cold Chain Monitoring
- Continuous temperature/humidity tracking
- Automated alerts for violations
- Historical data for quality prediction

### 🔗 Blockchain Provenance
- Immutable audit trails
- Verifiable credentials (W3C VC)
- Multi-blockchain support (Hyperledger Fabric, Ethereum)

### 📱 Quality Assurance
- Digital quality control records
- Laboratory test results
- Automated grade assignment

### 🎯 Consumer Verification
- QR codes for product scanning
- Blockchain-verified provenance
- Complete farm-to-fork journey

## Use Cases

### Small Farms (50-500kg production)
- QR code product labeling
- Organic certification proof
- Premium market access

### Regional Distributors (500-5000 tons/year)
- Cold chain monitoring
- Multi-farm aggregation
- Retail integration

### National Chains (5000+ tons/year)
- End-to-end traceability
- Regulatory compliance (FDA FSMA, EU Farm to Fork)
- Consumer apps

## Korean Agricultural Context

- **가락시장 (Garak Market):** Korea's largest wholesale market (2.4M tons/year)
- **농산물유통공사 (Korea Agro-Fisheries & Food Trade):** Key integration partner
- **도매시장 (Wholesale Markets):** 32 major markets nationwide
- **유통기한 (Expiration Date):** Critical for consumer trust
- **원산지 (Origin):** Mandatory labeling requirement

- Empowering small farmers
- Protecting consumers
- Promoting food safety
- Enabling sustainable agriculture
