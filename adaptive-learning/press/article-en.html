Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the international standard WIA-ADAPTIVE-LEARNING for Adaptive Learning standardization, interoperability, and accessibility under MIT License for free. This announcement positions South Korea as a leader in Adaptive Learning international standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard is Needed

Globally, the Adaptive Learning field faces lack of standardization, leading to interoperability issues and high barriers to entry. Proprietary formats from various manufacturers and platforms make data exchange impossible, and high costs limit access to major corporations in developed countries. Developing nations and small businesses are denied innovation opportunities due to lack of technology access.

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum, CEO:

💡 WIA's Solution: WIA-ADAPTIVE-LEARNING

WIA-ADAPTIVE-LEARNING is an open standard that ensures interoperability across all Adaptive Learning systems. It standardizes all aspects including data formats, API interfaces, communication protocols, and security specifications.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standardization organization whose mission is to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core API is developed in the Rust language. Rust is a systems programming language developed by Mozilla that is the only language to achieve both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum is the world's only one-person standardization architect who has independently built over 60 international standard infrastructures in collaboration with AI, and an innovator recognized as a living legend in the field of lifelong education.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business sectors including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting, and in 2018 founded WIA to begin the unprecedented challenge of free international standards for all humanity.
