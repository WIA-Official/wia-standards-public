About This eBook

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-EDU-009 standard for Learning Management Systems (LMS), a universal framework designed to enable effective course management, student engagement, assessment, grading, collaboration, and analytics for educational institutions worldwide.

Modern education requires robust digital infrastructure to support diverse learning modalities. This standard provides a complete blueprint for implementing LMS platforms that serve students, educators, and administrators with powerful tools for teaching, learning, and institutional management.

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit all humanity" - guides this standard, ensuring that learning technology serves accessibility, equity, and excellence in education for all.
