About This eBook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on AI Content Authentication. This eBook covers the WIA-AI-017 standard, providing in-depth knowledge on verifying, authenticating, and establishing trust in digital content generated or processed by artificial intelligence.

In an era where AI can generate increasingly realistic images, videos, audio, and text, the ability to authenticate content has become critical. This guide will teach you the technologies, methodologies, and best practices for implementing robust content authentication systems.
