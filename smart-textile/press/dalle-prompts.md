# DALL-E Prompts for WIA-IND-002 Press Materials

## Prompt 1: Hero Image - Smart Textile Innovation
A macro close-up of futuristic smart fabric showing woven conductive silver fibers glowing with subtle indigo (#6366F1) light integrated seamlessly into soft textile. The fabric is draped elegantly showing embedded sensors as tiny circuits woven into the material. Holographic overlays display real-time biometric data: heart rate waveform, temperature readings, and ECG signals. In the background, blurred images of athletes and medical professionals wearing smart clothing. High-tech materials science photography, clean and sophisticated aesthetic.

## Prompt 2: Problem Scenario - Traditional Medical Monitoring Limitations
A hospital patient restricted by bulky traditional medical monitoring equipment: multiple wired sensors attached with tape, uncomfortable chest straps, thick cables limiting movement, and a patient looking uncomfortable and constrained. Medical staff struggling with tangled wires. Separate scene shows an athlete with rigid, non-flexible sensors affecting performance. The scene emphasizes discomfort, restriction, and inconvenience of current monitoring technology. Clinical documentary photography style with emphasis on limitations and patient discomfort.

## Prompt 3: Solution Visualization - Seamless Health Monitoring
A serene scene showing diverse people (athlete, elderly person, office worker) comfortably wearing smart textiles that look like normal clothing. The fabric displays subtle visualization of integrated sensors monitoring vital signs. Split-screen displays show: ECG readings with 99% accuracy, continuous temperature monitoring, respiration tracking, all from comfortable, washable smart clothing. Person moving freely while exercising, their smart shirt wirelessly transmitting health data to a smartphone dashboard. Clean, comfortable, liberating health technology. Modern lifestyle photography with health-tech integration.
