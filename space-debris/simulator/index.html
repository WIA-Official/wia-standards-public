🎯 About This Simulator

This interactive simulator visualizes space debris in Earth orbit based on real data from ESA, NASA, and IADC. Explore the distribution of debris across different orbital regimes, see collision risks in action, and understand why debris mitigation is critical for sustainable space operations.

Following the WIA principle of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this tool helps educate about the space debris challenge and promotes awareness of responsible space stewardship.
