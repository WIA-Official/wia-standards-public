8.1.2 Core App Features

All WIA-PET-007 compliant apps must provide:

  • Device Pairing: Simple BLE pairing process with visual guidance (< 60 seconds)
  • Real-Time Dashboard: At-a-glance view of health, activity, location, battery
  • Historical Data: Graphs and charts for trends (day, week, month, year views)
  • Alerts Management: Push notifications for health alerts, geofence violations, low battery
  • Pet Profile: Name, photo, species, breed, age, weight, medical conditions
  • Goals and Recommendations: Personalized activity goals with progress tracking
  • Data Export: Export health and activity data in standard formats
  • Settings and Configuration: Customize geofences, alerts, sync frequency, power modes

8.2 Cloud Services and Backend Integration

8.2.1 Cloud Architecture Requirements

Scalable, secure cloud infrastructure is essential for multi-user, multi-device support:

  • Redundancy: Multi-region deployment for 99.9% uptime SLA
  • Scalability: Auto-scaling to handle traffic spikes (lost pet alerts, promotions)
  • Data Residency: Comply with regional data sovereignty laws (GDPR, etc.)
  • Backup: Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery
  • Disaster Recovery: RPO < 1 hour, RTO < 4 hours

8.3 Veterinary System Integration

8.3.1 FHIR for Veterinary Data Exchange

Adapting HL7 FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) for veterinary use:

  • Patient Resource: Modified to represent pets (species, breed, microchip ID)
  • Observation Resource: Vital signs, activity data, weight measurements
  • Condition Resource: Diagnoses and health conditions
  • MedicationStatement: Medications and supplements
  • Procedure: Surgeries, treatments, vaccinations

8.10 Conclusion: The Future of Pet Wellness

The WIA-PET-007 Pet Wearable Standard represents a significant step forward in leveraging technology to improve the lives of companion animals. By establishing common data formats, interoperability protocols, safety standards, and certification processes, this standard enables:

  • Innovation: Manufacturers can focus on unique features rather than reinventing basic infrastructure
  • Interoperability: Devices from different brands work together, data flows seamlessly between apps and services
  • Trust: Pet owners know certified devices meet rigorous safety, quality, and privacy standards
  • Veterinary Care: Objective, longitudinal data empowers veterinarians to provide better diagnoses and treatment
  • Research: Aggregated data advances veterinary science, benefiting all animals

As technology continues to evolve, the WIA-PET-007 standard will evolve with it, incorporating new sensors, communication technologies, and AI capabilities while maintaining its core mission: benefiting all humanity, including our beloved animal companions.
