## 6. Mechanical Protection (Enhanced)

### 6.1 G-Force Limits
[Same as v1.1]

### 6.2 Monitoring (Enhanced)
- 6-axis accelerometer required
- Sampling rate: minimum **1000 Hz** (increased from 100Hz)
- Data storage: minimum 7 days rolling buffer
- Alert on events >2g with automatic high-resolution capture

## 7. Tracking and Location (Enhanced)

### 7.1 GNSS Requirements (Enhanced)
- Position accuracy: ±10 meters (Level 1), ±5 meters (Level 2), ±2 meters (Level 3)
- Update interval: 5 minutes (L1), 1 minute (L2), 30 seconds (L3)
- Multi-constellation support: GPS + GLONASS + Galileo + BeiDou for Level 3

### 7.2 Geofencing
- Route corridor geofences with deviation alerts
- Facility boundary geofences
- Speed-based alerts
- Time-based zone restrictions

## 8-11. [Same as v1.1]

## 12. Maritime Transport (New)

### 12.1 Container Requirements
- Minimum 250L capacity for voyages >14 days
- Active cryocooler backup recommended for >30 day voyages
- Satellite communication required (cellular coverage insufficient)

### 12.2 Vessel Placement
- Reefer container preferred for ambient temperature control
- Secure mounting to prevent movement in heavy seas
- Avoid cargo holds with excessive vibration (near engines)

### 12.3 Weather Routing
- Route planning to avoid severe weather
- Maximum sea state: Beaufort 7 for standard containers
- Emergency port arrangements pre-identified
