### 8.2 Blockchain Option
Organizations may use blockchain technology for immutable custody records:
- Each transfer recorded as cryptographically signed transaction
- Smart contracts can automate alert escalation
- Public or private blockchain supported
- Must maintain traditional documentation as backup
