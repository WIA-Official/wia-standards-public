About This E-Book

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of cryo transport according to the WIA-CRYO-009 standard. From fundamental principles to advanced protocols, this e-book provides the knowledge needed to safely transport cryopreserved bodies across any distance.

Whether you're a cryonics facility operator, transport coordinator, or medical professional, this guide will help you understand and implement best practices for maintaining the integrity of cryopreserved patients during transport.
