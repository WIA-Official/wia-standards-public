About This eBook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Data Lineage. This eBook covers everything you need to know about tracking data provenance, understanding data flows, performing impact analysis, and implementing lineage solutions in your organization.

Whether you're a data engineer, analyst, architect, or governance professional, this guide will help you understand and implement data lineage best practices.
