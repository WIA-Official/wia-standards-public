About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of the WIA-TRADITIONAL-MEDICINE standard for integrating world traditional medicine systems with modern healthcare. From ancient diagnostic methods to cutting-edge systems biology, this guide bridges millennia of healing wisdom with contemporary precision medicine.

The WIA-TRADITIONAL-MEDICINE standard aims to unify fragmented traditional medicine systems by providing common data formats, APIs, protocols, and integration guidelines that respect cultural diversity while enabling global interoperability.
