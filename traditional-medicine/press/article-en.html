Traditional Medicine

WIA Standards announces the publication of the Traditional Medicine standard, the latest entry in the World Certification Industry Association open standards catalogue.

The Traditional Medicine standard captures the data formats, client-facing API surface, real-time communication protocol, and ecosystem integration patterns required for interoperable implementations across industry, research, and public-sector deployments. Reference implementations under the WIA-Official GitHub organization ship under the MIT license.

WIA evaluates conformance across three tiers — self-declared, third-party assessed, and accredited — with audit cadences scaled to deployment risk. Tier 3 evaluations are aligned with ISO/IEC 17065:2012 conformity assessment requirements and rely on documented evidence retention.

For more information, visit wiastandards.com.
