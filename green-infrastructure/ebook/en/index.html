🌲 WIA Green Infrastructure Standard

Official Ebook - Complete Guide

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

"Benefit All Humanity"

Cities face increasing challenges from climate change, stormwater flooding, and urban heat islands. The WIA Green Infrastructure Standard provides a comprehensive framework for designing, monitoring, and managing green roofs, permeable surfaces, urban vegetation, and stormwater systems. Together, we can build sustainable, resilient cities.

About This Ebook

This official ebook provides a comprehensive guide to the WIA Green Infrastructure Standard. It covers the complete specification for planning, installing, monitoring, and maintaining green infrastructure systems that create sustainable urban environments.
