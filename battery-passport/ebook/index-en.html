WIA Standard v1.0

WIA-BATTERY_PASSPORT: Battery Passport System

Standard Overview: The WIA Battery Passport System provides a comprehensive framework for digital tracking, documentation, and transparency of battery products throughout their entire lifecycle. This standard aligns with EU regulations and global sustainability initiatives for electric vehicle and energy storage batteries.

Introduction to Battery Passports

A battery passport is a digital document that records and tracks essential information about a battery from its production through use, second-life applications, and eventual recycling. This innovative approach addresses the growing need for transparency in the rapidly expanding electric vehicle and energy storage markets.

The WIA-BATTERY_PASSPORT standard establishes a unified framework that enables manufacturers, regulators, consumers, and recyclers to access accurate, standardized information about battery products. This transparency is crucial for ensuring sustainable practices, regulatory compliance, and consumer protection.

Core Objectives and Benefits

The Battery Passport System serves multiple stakeholders with distinct advantages for each participant in the battery value chain.

Environmental Sustainability

By tracking material composition and carbon footprint throughout the production process, the battery passport enables accurate environmental impact assessment. This data supports circular economy initiatives by facilitating proper recycling and material recovery, reducing the demand for virgin materials extraction.

Key Environmental Metrics: Carbon footprint per kWh, recycled content percentage, water usage in production, and end-of-life recyclability score are all tracked and documented within the passport system.

Regulatory Compliance

The European Union Battery Regulation (EU 2023/1542) mandates digital battery passports for electric vehicle batteries, industrial batteries, and light means of transport batteries starting from 2027. The WIA standard ensures full compliance with these requirements while providing flexibility for additional regional regulations.

Data Structure and Standards

The battery passport contains comprehensive information organized into standardized categories. Each category follows strict schema definitions to ensure interoperability across systems and jurisdictions.

Guiding Philosophy

The WIA-BATTERY_PASSPORT standard embodies the principle of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "benefit all humanity." By promoting transparency and sustainability in battery production and usage, we contribute to a cleaner environment and more responsible resource management for future generations.

Our Mission: Enable complete battery lifecycle transparency that serves manufacturers, consumers, regulators, and the environment equally. Through standardized data exchange and immutable records, we build trust and accountability across the entire battery value chain.
