Key Features

🆔

Digital Identity

Universal digital identity system with biometric verification, multi-factor authentication, and blockchain-based credential management for secure online citizenship.

⚖️

Rights Management

Comprehensive digital rights framework including privacy rights, freedom of expression, data ownership, and protection against digital discrimination.

✅

Global Recognition

Cross-border identity verification system recognized by 193 countries with support for multi-jurisdiction citizenship and international digital services.

🔐

Privacy Protection

Zero-knowledge proof authentication, selective disclosure, and GDPR/CCPA compliance ensuring maximum privacy and data protection.

🌍

Universal Access

Accessible to all regardless of location, income, or status. Supporting refugees, stateless individuals, and marginalized communities with digital citizenship.

⚡

Instant Verification

Real-time identity verification with sub-second response times, supporting e-government services, banking, healthcare, and education.

Our Philosophy

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

Benefit All Humanity

Digital citizenship is a fundamental human right in the 21st century. Our standard ensures that every person on Earth can participate in the digital economy and society with dignity, security, and equal rights.
