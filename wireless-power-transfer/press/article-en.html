WIA Wireless Power Transfer International Standard Released for Free

Standardizing Power Transfer from Inductive to Laser Technologies

Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released international standard WIA-COMM-009 covering Inductive (5cm, 90%), resonant (2m, 80%), microwave (1km, 50%), laser (1000km, 40%), under MIT license for free.

🚨 Current Situation

Drones, robots, space applications need long-range wireless power, but lack microwave/laser transmission standards and safety criteria.

Sam-Heum Yeon, CEO:
"We created standards transmitting power from 1cm to 1000km for a cable-free future."

WIA's Solution

Inductive (5cm, 90%), resonant (2m, 80%), microwave (1km, 50%), laser (1000km, 40%)

Sam-Heum Yeon, CEO:
"2.45GHz microwave transmits 100W at 100m distance with SAR <2 W/kg ensuring safety."
