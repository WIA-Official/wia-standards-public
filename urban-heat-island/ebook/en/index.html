"Benefit All Humanity"

Urban heat islands threaten public health, increase energy consumption, and reduce quality of life in cities worldwide. The WIA Urban Heat Island Response Standard provides a comprehensive framework for monitoring, analyzing, and mitigating urban heat through data-driven strategies and coordinated action.

About This Ebook

This official ebook provides a comprehensive guide to the WIA Urban Heat Island Response Standard. It covers the complete specification for monitoring urban temperatures, analyzing heat island effects, implementing cooling strategies, and integrating with smart city systems.
