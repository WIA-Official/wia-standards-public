## Technical Specifications for All Images
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (1792×1024)
- Style: Modern tech illustration with movement and energy, professional yet collaborative
- Color Palette:
  - Primary: #3B82F6 (Blue)
  - Background: #0f172a (Dark Navy) or #e3f2fd (Light Blue)
  - Accents: Pipeline greens (success), automation blues, collaboration colors
  - Problem image: Reds, grays (conflict and stress)
- Mood: Collaborative, automated, continuous, reliable, innovative

## Brand Consistency Notes
- Emphasize collaboration between Dev and Ops
- Show automation and continuous delivery prominently
- Highlight cultural transformation, not just tools
- Demonstrate speed and reliability together
- Include monitoring and feedback loops
- Show the infinity loop concept visually
- Maintain enterprise credibility while showing agility
