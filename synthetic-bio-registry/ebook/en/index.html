Market Overview 2025

Brain-computer interfaces and sensory enhancement technologies have reached a critical inflection point in 2025. With approximately 25 clinical trials of BCI implants currently underway worldwide, participation has grown from single digits to dozens of patients, marking unprecedented progress in the field.

The global BCI market has reached $12 billion in 2025, with a projected annual growth rate of 32%. Leading companies including Neuralink, Synchron, and Paradromics are expanding international trials, while the BISC (Biological Interface System to Cortex) ultra-thin neural implant announced in December 2025 represents a breakthrough with tens of thousands of electrodes creating high-bandwidth wireless links between brain and computers.

Key Technologies Covered

Intracortical Neural Interfaces
High-resolution electrode arrays implanted directly into cortical tissue, providing single-neuron resolution for precise control of external devices. Applications include paralysis treatment, speech restoration, and motor function recovery.
Electrocorticography (ECoG)
Minimally invasive arrays placed on the cortical surface, offering excellent signal quality without penetrating brain tissue. Ideal for epilepsy monitoring and motor control applications.
Non-Invasive Wearable Sensors
Self-powered flexible sensors that conform to body contours, enabling comfortable 24/7 brain monitoring without surgery. Applications in cognitive enhancement, meditation, and wellness.
AI-Powered Signal Decoding
Advanced machine learning models for real-time interpretation of neural signals, enabling accurate intent classification, speech synthesis, and movement control.
