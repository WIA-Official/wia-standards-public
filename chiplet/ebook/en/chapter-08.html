Chapter 8: Future Directions

This final chapter explores emerging technologies and trends that will shape the future of chiplet architecture. From advanced packaging innovations to photonic integration and AI-optimized chiplets, the trajectory of chiplet technology promises continued advancement in semiconductor capabilities.

Advanced Packaging Roadmap

Packaging technology continues to evolve rapidly, with each generation enabling higher interconnect density, lower power consumption, and improved thermal management. The roadmap points toward increasingly sophisticated 3D integration and novel materials.

Beyond Current Limits

While current advanced packaging achieves 25-55 μm bump pitch, the roadmap targets sub-10 μm pitch through hybrid bonding and ultimately sub-1 μm direct bonding, enabling true monolithic 3D integration density with chiplet flexibility.
