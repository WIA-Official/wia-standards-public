Chapter 6: Data Portability and Transfer

6.1 The Portability Revolution

Data portability is perhaps the most transformative feature of WIA-EDU-010. Students no longer need to start from scratch when transferring institutions or applying to new programs.

Key Benefits

  • Instant transcript sharing without waiting for mail
  • Reduced administrative burden on registrar offices
  • Elimination of transcript fees for students
  • Automatic credit evaluation at receiving institutions
  • Preservation of complete academic history

6.2 Export Formats

Students can export their data in multiple formats:

  • WIA-EDU-010 JSON: Complete structured data with full fidelity
  • PDF Transcript: Official, human-readable document
  • XML: For legacy system integration
  • CSV: For analysis in spreadsheet applications

6.3 Transfer Protocol

The secure transfer protocol ensures data integrity and authenticity:

Transfer Steps

  1. Student initiates transfer request
  2. Source institution packages data with digital signature
  3. Transfer authorization code generated
  4. Destination institution receives encrypted package
  5. Cryptographic verification performed
  6. Data imported into destination system
  7. Student and both institutions notified

6.5 Credit Transfer Evaluation

With standardized course data, receiving institutions can automatically evaluate transfer credits:

  • Course equivalency mapping
  • Credit hour conversion
  • GPA recalculation with institutional policy
  • Degree requirement matching

6.6 Blockchain Integration

For long-term integrity, WIA-EDU-010 supports blockchain anchoring:

  • Immutable record of degree conferral
  • Lifetime verification without institutional access
  • Protection against institutional closure
  • International recognition and verification
