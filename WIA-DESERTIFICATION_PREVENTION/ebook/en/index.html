Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the WIA Desertification Prevention Standard.

This e-book provides a complete framework for combating land degradation, monitoring desertification processes, and implementing effective restoration strategies. Drawing on international best practices, satellite technology, and the UN Convention to Combat Desertification (UNCCD), this standard offers practical tools for environmental professionals, policymakers, and land managers worldwide.

Desertification affects over 2 billion people globally and threatens 40% of Earth's land surface. Through this standard, we provide actionable solutions grounded in science and powered by technology to reverse land degradation and restore ecosystem health.
