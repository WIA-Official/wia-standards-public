Key Features

🔍

Complete Traceability

Track every product from farm to table with comprehensive data capture at every stage. Know exactly where your food comes from and every step it takes.

🚛

Supply Chain Transparency

Real-time visibility into your entire supply chain network. Monitor transportation, storage conditions, and handling processes to ensure quality and safety.

⚠️

Rapid Recall Management

Execute product recalls in hours, not days. Precisely identify affected batches and notify all stakeholders instantly with automated alert systems.

📋

Regulatory Compliance

Automatically comply with global food safety regulations including FDA, FSMA, EU regulations, and local standards. Generate audit reports effortlessly.

🔗

Blockchain Integration

Leverage blockchain technology for immutable record-keeping. Ensure data integrity and build trust with tamper-proof audit trails.

👥

Consumer Safety

Empower consumers with QR code access to complete product histories. Build trust through transparency and protect public health proactively.

4-Phase Architecture

Phase 1

Data Format

Standardized schemas for product identification, batch tracking, origin data, temperature logs, and handling records.

Phase 2

API Interface

RESTful APIs for traceability queries, recall initiation, supply chain integration, and real-time data synchronization.

Phase 3

Protocol

Communication protocols for data exchange between stakeholders, alert propagation, and contamination event notifications.

Phase 4

Integration

Seamless integration with ERP systems, blockchain networks, regulatory reporting, and IoT sensor networks.

Real-World Use Cases

🥬 Fresh Produce Tracking

Monitor lettuce from farm harvest through distribution centers to retail stores, tracking temperature and handling at every step.

🥩 Meat Processing Safety

Trace beef products from ranch to butcher, ensuring cold chain integrity and rapid recall capabilities for food safety incidents.

🐟 Seafood Authenticity

Verify sustainable fishing practices and prevent seafood fraud with blockchain-verified catch data and processing records.

⚠️ Allergen Management

Track allergen information throughout production and prevent cross-contamination with detailed ingredient traceability.

🌱 Organic Certification

Maintain verifiable organic chain-of-custody from certified farms through processing to final packaging and sale.

🍽️ Restaurant Supply Chains

Enable restaurants to verify ingredient sources and provide diners with complete transparency about meal components.
