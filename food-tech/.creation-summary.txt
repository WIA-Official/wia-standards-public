EXAMPLE USAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
$ wia-ind-007 calc-protein cultured-beef 2.5 1.2
  → FCR: 2.08 (90% more efficient than beef)
  → Water saved: 18,039 liters per kg protein
  → GHG reduced: 55.2 kg CO2e per kg protein

$ wia-ind-007 analyze-nutrition spinach 100
  → NDS: 1300 (SUPERFOOD ⭐⭐⭐)

$ wia-ind-007 calc-macros 35 male 75 175 moderate
  → BMR: 1745 kcal/day
  → TDEE: 2705 kcal/day
  → Protein: 203g, Carbs: 271g, Fat: 90g
