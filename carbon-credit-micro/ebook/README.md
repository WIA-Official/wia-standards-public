# WIA Micro Carbon Credit Trading Standard - Ebook

Complete ebook for the WIA Micro Carbon Credit Trading Standard with comprehensive documentation in English and Korean.

## 📚 Contents

### English Version (`/en/`)
- **index.html** - Table of contents and introduction
- **chapter1.html** - Introduction & Overview: Democratizing carbon markets, individual participation
- **chapter2.html** - Data Schema: Carbon credit units, activity records, verification data
- **chapter3.html** - API Endpoints: Credit issuance, trading, verification, wallet management
- **chapter4.html** - Protocol Specification: Credit tokenization, verification protocols
- **chapter5.html** - Integration Guide: IoT sensors, mobile apps, smart meters
- **chapter6.html** - Security & Privacy: Transaction privacy, anti-fraud measures
- **chapter7.html** - Implementation Examples: Python/TypeScript/Bash for carbon trading
- **chapter8.html** - Certification & Compliance: Carbon verification standards, regulatory compliance

### Korean Version (`/ko/`)
- **index.html** - 목차 및 소개
- **chapter1.html** - 소개 및 개요
- **chapter2.html** - 데이터 스키마
- **chapter3.html** - API 엔드포인트
- **chapter4.html** - 프로토콜 사양
- **chapter5.html** - 통합 가이드
- **chapter6.html** - 보안 및 개인정보 보호
- **chapter7.html** - 구현 예제
- **chapter8.html** - 인증 및 규정 준수

1. **Comprehensive Coverage**: 8 chapters covering all aspects of micro carbon credit trading
