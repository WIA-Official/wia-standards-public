About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on AI Bias Detection. This ebook provides in-depth knowledge about identifying, measuring, and mitigating bias in artificial intelligence systems. Whether you're a data scientist, machine learning engineer, product manager, or policymaker, this guide will equip you with the tools and understanding needed to build fair and ethical AI systems.

Bias in AI systems can lead to discriminatory outcomes that harm individuals and communities. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into critical decision-making processes—from hiring and lending to healthcare and criminal justice—ensuring fairness is not just an ethical imperative but a practical necessity for building trustworthy systems.

This guide follows the 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) philosophy, emphasizing that AI technology should serve all people fairly and equitably, regardless of their background or characteristics.
