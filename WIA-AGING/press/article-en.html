WIA Aging — Healthy Aging and Geriatric Care

WIA Standards Editorial · 2026-04-25 · 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity)

An open standard for healthy aging biomarkers, longevity research data, and geriatric-care interoperability.

Why this standard exists

The WIA Aging — Healthy Aging and Geriatric Care standard codifies the data formats, APIs, and protocols required for interoperable implementations across vendors, regions, and operational scales. By aligning on a single contract, organizations adopting this standard can:

  • Exchange records with any other compliant system without bespoke integration work.
  • Audit conformance through a stable, machine-checkable test suite.
  • Access certification credentials issued under the WIA conformance program.

What is in scope

The standard covers four phases:

  1. Data formats — canonical JSON Schema, YAML, and binary encodings.
  2. API interface — OpenAPI 3.0 REST contract with X-API-Key and OAuth 2.0 auth.
  3. Protocol — request/response semantics, idempotency, error envelopes, rate limiting.
  4. Integration — how this standard composes with other WIA standards.

Adoption pathway

Implementations are encouraged to begin with the open-source SDKs in WIA-AGING/api/sdk/typescript/ and WIA-AGING/api/sdk/python/, then run the validation CLI in WIA-AGING/cli/ to confirm conformance. Once both pass, organizations may apply for certification at cert.wiastandards.com.

Standards that are open, verifiable, and machine-checkable are the only ones that survive long enough to outlive the organization that wrote them. — WIA Editorial Board
