Chapter 7: Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence

The true power of soil sensor data emerges through advanced analytics and AI. Machine learning models predict irrigation needs, forecast yields, and detect anomalies before they impact crops.

Predictive Analytics

AI algorithms analyze historical soil data patterns to forecast future conditions. Predictive models recommend optimal irrigation schedules considering weather forecasts, ET rates, and crop water requirements.

Anomaly Detection

Machine learning identifies unusual soil conditions indicating problems like sensor malfunction, disease onset, or nutrient imbalances. Early detection enables proactive intervention.

Yield Prediction

Correlating soil health data with historical yields builds predictive models forecasting crop production. Farmers can optimize inputs and plan harvests based on data-driven yield estimates.
