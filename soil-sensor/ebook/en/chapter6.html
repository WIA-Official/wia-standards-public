Chapter 6: Integrating Soil Sensors with Farm Systems

Soil sensors deliver maximum value when integrated with irrigation controllers, fertilizer systems, and farm management platforms. This chapter explores integration strategies and best practices.

Irrigation Automation

Direct integration with irrigation systems enables automatic watering based on real-time soil moisture. Smart controllers activate irrigation only when moisture drops below crop-specific thresholds, saving water and labor.

Fertilizer Management

NPK sensor data drives variable-rate fertilization, applying nutrients precisely where needed. Integration with fertilizer dispensers or VRA equipment optimizes application rates across fields.

Farm Management Software

Centralized dashboards aggregate data from multiple sensors, providing holistic field health views. Integration with FMS platforms enables data-driven decision making across all farm operations.
