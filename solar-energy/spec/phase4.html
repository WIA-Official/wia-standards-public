5. Advanced Grid Services

5.1 Fast Frequency Response (FFR)

Sub-second response to grid frequency deviations:

  • Detection time: <100ms
  • Response time: <200ms to 50% power change
  • Sustained response: 30+ minutes
  • Accuracy: ±5% of committed capacity
  • Availability: >95% during committed periods

5.2 Synthetic Inertia

Emulation of generator inertia using battery-inverter systems:

  • Virtual inertia constant: 2-10 seconds (configurable)
  • Rate of change of frequency (RoCoF) response
  • Coordination with other inverter-based resources
  • Contribution to grid stability metrics
