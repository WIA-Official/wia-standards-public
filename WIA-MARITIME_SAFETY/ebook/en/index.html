🌍 International Standards Coverage

IMO Conventions

  • SOLAS (Safety of Life at Sea) - Chapters I-XIV comprehensive coverage
  • MARPOL (Marine Pollution) - Annexes I-VI environmental compliance
  • STCW (Training & Certification) - Officer competency requirements
  • COLREGS (Collision Regulations) - International rules of the road

Technical Standards

  • IEC 61162 - Maritime navigation and communication equipment standards
  • ITU-R M.1371 - AIS technical characteristics and operational procedures
  • ISO 19847 - Ships and marine technology specifications
  • IHO S-57/S-63 - Electronic navigational chart standards

Regional Compliance

  • U.S. Coast Guard - 33 CFR & 46 CFR navigation safety regulations
  • EU Maritime - Vessel traffic monitoring and port state control directives
  • Asia-Pacific - Tokyo MOU port state control requirements
  • Flag State - Specific national maritime authority regulations
