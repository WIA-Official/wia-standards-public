Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-MENTAL-008, an international standard for sleep disorder, as free and open source under MIT License. This marks a historic moment as the Republic of Korea leads international standards in this field.

Modern society struggles with lack of systematic standards in sleep disorder. Despite technological advances, absence of unified frameworks reduces efficiency, causing many people to face unnecessary difficulties.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standardization organization with a mission to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 400 international standards across various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free and open source.

🦀 Technology Stack: WIA's core API is developed in Rust. Rust is a systems programming language developed by Mozilla, the only language achieving both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions simultaneously.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum is the world's only solo standardization architect who built over 60 international standard infrastructures collaborating with AI, and an innovator recognized as a living legend in lifelong education.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business domains including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting. In 2018, he established WIA and began the unprecedented challenge of free international standards for all humanity.
