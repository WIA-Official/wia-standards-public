Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-TIME-028, an international temporal medical care standard protecting time travelers' health, as free and open source under MIT License. The standard comprehensively addresses Temporal Sickness Syndrome (TSS) diagnosis, cellular age synchronization, and memory disorder treatment.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Time travel presents unprecedented medical challenges to the human body. Cellular damage from temporal displacement, biological age misalignment with chronological time, temporal amnesia, and chronic temporal stress syndrome— none of these conditions can even be diagnosed with conventional medicine. Without standardized treatment protocols, time travelers are left defenseless against life-threatening health risks.
