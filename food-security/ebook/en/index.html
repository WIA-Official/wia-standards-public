About This Ebook

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA Food Security Standard (WIA-AGRI-029), a global framework for managing food security through strategic reserves, supply chain resilience, climate adaptation, distribution equity, and emergency response. You'll learn how to implement this standard to protect populations from food insecurity and build resilient food systems.

Who Should Read This

  • Government Food Authorities: Implement national food security systems
  • Humanitarian Organizations: Coordinate emergency food aid
  • Agricultural Planners: Integrate production with security planning
  • Supply Chain Managers: Build resilient food distribution networks
  • Climate Adaptation Specialists: Develop climate-resilient strategies
  • Software Developers: Build food security applications and integrations
  • Policy Makers: Design effective food security policies
