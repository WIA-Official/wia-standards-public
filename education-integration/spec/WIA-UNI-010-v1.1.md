# WIA-UNI-010: Education Integration Standard v1.1

## Change Summary
This minor update adds teacher certification requirements and enhanced privacy protections based on pilot program feedback.

## Version Information
- **Version**: 1.1
- **Published**: 2025-06-01
- **Status**: Active
- **Supersedes**: v1.0

## Major Changes from v1.0

### 1. Teacher Certification Program
New requirement for educators participating in integration programs:

**Certification Levels:**
- Level 1: Basic Integration Training (20 hours)
- Level 2: Advanced Collaborative Teaching (40 hours)
- Level 3: Integration Leadership (60 hours + practicum)

**Requirements:**
- Cultural sensitivity training
- Technology proficiency assessment
- Collaborative teaching demonstration
- Annual renewal with 10 hours professional development

### 2. Enhanced Privacy Protections

**Student Data:**
- Explicit parental consent required for all cross-border exchanges
- Data retention limited to 7 years post-graduation
- Enhanced anonymization for research data
- Right to be forgotten with 30-day processing

**Session Recording:**
- Clear notification of recording status
- Option for students to disable video (audio-only participation)
- Encrypted storage with access logging
- Automatic deletion after 90 days (unless flagged)

### 3. Accessibility Enhancements

**Platform Requirements:**
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance
- Screen reader support (JAWS, NVDA compatibility)
- Keyboard-only navigation
- Adjustable text size (100%-200%)
- High contrast mode
- Closed captioning for all videos

### 4. Quality Assurance Updates

**Annual Review Process:**
- Peer review by international education experts
- Student and parent feedback surveys
- Learning outcomes assessment
- Platform security audit
- Content quality evaluation

## Backward Compatibility

All v1.0 implementations remain compliant with v1.1. Migration requires:
1. Teacher certification registration within 6 months
2. Privacy policy updates within 30 days
3. Accessibility upgrades within 90 days

## Implementation Timeline

- Q2 2025: Teacher certification program launch
- Q3 2025: Privacy enhancements deployment
- Q4 2025: Accessibility compliance deadline
- Q1 2026: Full v1.1 compliance required
