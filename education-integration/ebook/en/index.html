About This eBook

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-UNI-010 standard for inter-Korean education integration, a visionary framework designed to harmonize curricula, facilitate student exchanges, enable teacher collaboration, and build unified online learning platforms across the Korean peninsula.

Education is the foundation of peaceful reunification. By bringing together students, teachers, and educational systems from North and South Korea, this standard aims to create understanding, build bridges, and prepare future generations for a unified Korea.

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit all humanity" - guides every aspect of this standard, ensuring that education serves peace, unity, and human dignity.
