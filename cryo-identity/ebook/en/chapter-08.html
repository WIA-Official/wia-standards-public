8.1 REVIVAL PREPARATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When revival becomes technologically possible, the identity restoration
process ensures the preserved individual returns with their complete identity.

[ID] PREPARATION STAGES:

    1. VAULT VERIFICATION
       - Verify all vault locations accessible
       - Confirm integrity of all encrypted payloads
       - Decrypt and validate biometric data

    2. BIOMETRIC PACKAGE PREPARATION
       - Extract DNA profile for comparison
       - Prepare fingerprint templates
       - Ready facial geometry for verification

    3. LEGAL PACKAGE PREPARATION
       - Compile all legal documents
       - Verify current legal status
       - Prepare reissuance requests

    4. DIGITAL PACKAGE PREPARATION
       - Verify DID status and blockchain records
       - Prepare wallet recovery credentials
       - Compile account recovery information


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.2 IDENTITY VERIFICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

First step of revival: confirm the revived subject IS the preserved individual.

[ID] VERIFICATION WORKFLOW:

    +------------------+
    | COLLECT SAMPLE   |
    | - DNA sample     |
    | - Fingerprints   |
    | - Facial scan    |
    +--------+---------+
             |
             v
    +------------------+
    | COMPARE TO VAULT |
    | - DNA match?     |
    | - Biometric match|
    +--------+---------+
             |
    +--------v---------+
    | IDENTITY         |
    | CONFIRMED?       |
    +--------+---------+
        |         |
       YES        NO
        |         |
        v         v
    [PROCEED]  [INVESTIGATE]


[ID] VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:

    - DNA match: REQUIRED (>99.99% confidence)
    - At least 1 additional biometric match
    - Combined confidence score >80%

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.3 LEGAL IDENTITY RESTORATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] RESTORATION STEPS:

    Step 1: GOVERNMENT NOTIFICATION
            - Notify relevant government agencies
            - Submit revival verification documents
            - Request legal status restoration

    Step 2: DOCUMENT REISSUANCE
            - New passport application
            - National ID reissuance
            - Driver's license (if applicable)

    Step 3: RECORD UPDATES
            - Update vital records (living status)
            - Restore citizenship rights
            - Update social security/tax records

    Step 4: LEGAL STATUS VERIFICATION
            - Confirm legal identity restored
            - Issue identity restoration certificate
            - Update all linked systems


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.4 DIGITAL IDENTITY RECOVERY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] DID RECOVERY:

    1. Verify blockchain records for DID
    2. Re-establish control of primary DID
    3. Update DID document with new keys
    4. Publish recovery proof

[ID] WALLET RECOVERY:

    1. Guardian approval for wallet access
    2. Recover credential wallet
    3. Verify all credentials still valid
    4. Revoke and reissue expired credentials

[ID] ACCOUNT RECOVERY:

    For each documented account:
    1. Contact platform with identity proof
    2. Complete account recovery process
    3. Update credentials
    4. Verify account access


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.5 SOCIAL REINTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] REINTEGRATION SUPPORT:

    IMMEDIATE (Days 1-7):
    - Basic orientation to current date/time
    - Introduction to technological changes
    - Medical and psychological support

    SHORT-TERM (Weeks 1-4):
    - Family reunification (if applicable)
    - Financial situation assessment
    - Housing and basic needs

    MEDIUM-TERM (Months 1-6):
    - Education on societal changes
    - Career assessment and guidance
    - Full integration support

    Pre-recorded messages from the preserved individual to themselves
