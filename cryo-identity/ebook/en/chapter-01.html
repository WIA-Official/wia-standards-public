1.1 THE IDENTITY CONTINUITY CHALLENGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cryopreservation extends beyond physical preservation - it requires maintaining
the complete identity of the preserved individual across potentially centuries
of storage.

[ID] IDENTITY DOMAINS:

    +------------------------------------------------------------------------+
    | Domain              | Components                                       |
    +------------------------------------------------------------------------+
    | Legal Identity      | Name, birth, citizenship, IDs, relationships    |
    | Personal Identity   | Memories, personality, values, skills           |
    | Digital Identity    | DIDs, wallets, accounts, digital assets         |
    | Biometric Identity  | DNA, fingerprints, facial, voice                |
    +------------------------------------------------------------------------+

The Challenge:
  - Identity must persist across decades or centuries
  - Legal status must be maintained during preservation
  - Technology formats must survive technological evolution
  - Privacy requires long-term cryptographic security
  - Revival requires seamless identity restoration


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.2 WHY CREDENTIALS FOR CRYOPRESERVED IDENTITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Verifiable Credentials provide the foundation for long-term identity management.

[ID] CREDENTIAL REQUIREMENTS:

    1. IMMUTABILITY
       - Records remain unchanged during storage
       - Cryptographic signatures prevent tampering
       - Blockchain anchoring for timestamps

    2. PORTABILITY
       - Credentials travel with preserved subject
       - Any compliant facility can verify
       - Cross-border recognition

    3. PRIVACY PRESERVATION
       - Encrypted storage for sensitive data
       - Selective disclosure capabilities
       - Guardian-controlled access

    4. TEMPORAL RESILIENCE
       - Algorithm agility for future-proofing
       - Format migration strategies
       - Long-term archive standards
