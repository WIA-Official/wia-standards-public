WIA-MENTAL-006

PTSD Support Standard: Complete Guide

Version 1.0 Status: Official 4 Phases HIPAA/GDPR Compliant

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the WIA-MENTAL-006 standard, a complete framework for Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) assessment, treatment, and recovery support using digital technologies. This ebook covers evidence-based assessment tools, therapeutic protocols including Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT), Prolonged Exposure (PE), and Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), along with comprehensive security, privacy, and integration requirements for clinical deployment.

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of digital PTSD support as defined by the WIA-MENTAL-006 standard. The standard is organized into four phases:

  • Phase 1: Data Formats - JSON schemas for assessments, treatment sessions, progress tracking, and clinical documentation
  • Phase 2: APIs - RESTful endpoints for assessment administration, treatment delivery, crisis intervention, and outcome monitoring
  • Phase 3: Protocols - Clinical workflows, security requirements, ethical guidelines, and emergency response procedures
  • Phase 4: Integration - EHR integration, provider systems, telehealth platforms, and compliance validation

Whether you're a mental health clinician implementing evidence-based PTSD treatments, a software developer building clinical tools, or a healthcare administrator ensuring regulatory compliance, this guide provides the technical depth and clinical insights you need for safe and effective PTSD support services.
