Chapter 7: Phase 4 - WIA Ecosystem Integration

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

No standard exists in isolation. Phase 4 integrates food safety with the broader WIA ecosystem, regulatory systems, supply chains, and consumer interfaces.

7.1 WIA Standards Integration

WIA StandardIntegrationBenefit
WIA-INTENT Voice queries: "Is this product safe?" Consumer accessibility
WIA-OMNI-API Unified gateway for all WIA services Simplified integration
WIA-BLOCKCHAIN Immutable traceability records Transparency, trust
WIA-DPKI Decentralized identity (DIDs) Secure authentication
WIA-VC Verifiable credentials for certifications Digital HACCP certificates

Summary

Phase 4 creates a unified ecosystem where food safety data flows seamlessly between manufacturers, regulators, retailers, laboratories, and consumers. Through WIA standard integration and regulatory APIs, this phase delivers the promise of global food safety transparency.
