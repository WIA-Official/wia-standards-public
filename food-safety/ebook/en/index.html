About This Ebook

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA Food Safety Standard (WIA-AGRI-015), a global framework for digital food safety management. You'll learn about HACCP compliance, blockchain traceability, contamination detection, recall management, and consumer protection systems.

Who Should Read This

  • Food Manufacturers: Implement digital safety systems
  • Regulatory Agencies: Understand compliance frameworks
  • Retailers & Distributors: Ensure supply chain safety
  • Laboratory Technicians: Integrate testing systems
  • Software Developers: Build food safety applications
  • Consumers: Understand food safety verification
