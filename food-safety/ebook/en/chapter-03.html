Chapter 3: WIA Food Safety Standard Overview

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

The WIA Food Safety Standard (WIA-AGRI-015) provides a comprehensive, vendor-neutral framework for digital food safety management. Built on four phases—Data Format, API Interface, Protocol, and Integration—this standard enables interoperability, transparency, and real-time safety monitoring across the entire food supply chain.

3.1 Vision and Mission

3.1.1 Core Vision

"Safe food for all humanity, verified by technology, trusted by blockchain."

The WIA Food Safety Standard envisions a world where:

  • Every food product has a complete, verifiable history from farm to table
  • Contamination is detected instantly and affected products recalled in seconds
  • Consumers can scan a QR code and see full safety information
  • Food safety systems from different vendors work together seamlessly
  • Global regulatory compliance is automated, not manual

3.2 Four-Phase Architecture

Phase Focus Key Technologies Deliverables
Phase 1 Data Format JSON schemas, validation rules Product data, safety records, traceability chains
Phase 2 API Interface REST APIs, GraphQL, WebSockets Endpoints for CRUD, monitoring, recalls
Phase 3 Protocol MQTT, blockchain, real-time streaming Alert protocols, IoT integration, messaging
Phase 4 Integration WIA ecosystem, regulatory APIs FDA/USDA/MFDS integration, WIA-VC certificates

3.4 Benefits

3.4.1 For Manufacturers

  • Faster Recalls: 2.2 seconds vs. 42 days
  • Reduced Liability: Demonstrable due diligence
  • Brand Protection: Transparency builds trust
  • Insurance Savings: 20% premium reduction
  • Market Access: Meet global regulatory requirements

3.4.2 For Retailers

  • Instant product verification before stocking
  • Automated compliance checking
  • Rapid response to safety alerts
  • Consumer trust through transparency

3.4.3 For Consumers

  • Scan QR code for complete safety information
  • See farm origin, test results, certifications
  • Receive instant recall notifications
  • Verify allergen information digitally

3.4.4 For Regulators

  • Real-time compliance monitoring
  • Automated incident reporting
  • Faster outbreak investigation
  • Harmonized global standards

Summary

The WIA Food Safety Standard provides a comprehensive framework for modernizing food safety through four phases: standardized data formats, open APIs, real-time protocols, and ecosystem integration. By combining blockchain traceability, HACCP digital compliance, and consumer transparency, this standard addresses the critical challenges facing global food safety today.
