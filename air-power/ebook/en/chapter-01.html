1.1 The Problem: Charging as Modern Stress

In our connected world, the anxiety of low battery has become a universal human experience. We constantly worry about finding outlets, carrying cables, remembering charging pads, and the eternal question: "What's your battery percentage?"

Current Reality: Despite our technological advances, we remain tethered to power outlets and charging cables. Even "wireless" charging pads require precise placement and are effectively wired solutions in disguise.

The Modern Charging Pain Points

Imagine a world where power is as ubiquitous and invisible as WiFi. Where walking into a room automatically begins charging all your devices. Where the very concept of "charging" becomes obsolete because your devices are always at full power.

1.6 Key Capabilities

Multi-Device Support

Unlike traditional charging that requires one cable per device, a single AIR-POWER transmitter can charge unlimited devices simultaneously. The system intelligently allocates power based on need and priority.

Device Tracking & Beamforming

Advanced transmitters track device positions in real-time and focus power beams toward them, maximizing efficiency. As you move around a room, power follows you automatically.

Smart Power Negotiation

Devices communicate their power needs, battery levels, and priorities. The system automatically optimizes power distribution - critical devices get priority, fully charged devices receive trickle power.

Safety First

Comprehensive safety systems including human detection, SAR monitoring, automatic shutoff, and foreign object detection ensure that wireless power is as safe as WiFi.
