About This Standard

The WIA-FIN-007 Smart Contract Standard provides a comprehensive framework for developing, deploying, and managing smart contracts across multiple blockchain platforms. This standard ensures security, interoperability, and best practices for decentralized applications.

Philosophy: 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) - Empowering developers to create secure, reliable, and accessible smart contract solutions for everyone.
