Benefit All Humanity - The WIA Automated Trading Standard establishes universal frameworks for algorithmic trading systems, risk management protocols, and regulatory compliance across global financial markets.

About This Standard

This comprehensive standard addresses the critical needs of automated trading systems in modern financial markets. From high-frequency trading algorithms to portfolio rebalancing bots, from market-making strategies to arbitrage detection, this standard provides the technical specifications, API interfaces, risk management protocols, and regulatory compliance frameworks necessary for safe and effective automated trading.

Automated trading now accounts for over 70% of equity market volume in developed markets. This standard ensures that these systems operate with transparency, safety, and interoperability while protecting market integrity and investor interests.
