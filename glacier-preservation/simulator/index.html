Glacier Analysis Algorithms

Calculate melt predictions, sea level contribution, and water supply impact.

Melt Prediction

Predict future ice loss based on current trends and climate scenarios

meltLoss = meltRate × years × (1 + tempIncrease × sensitivity)

Sea Level Contribution

Calculate glacier contribution to global sea level rise

seaLevel = (glacierMass × meltPercent) / oceanArea

빙하 분석 알고리즘

융해 예측, 해수면 기여도 및 수자원 영향을 계산합니다.

융해 예측

현재 추세와 기후 시나리오를 기반으로 미래 얼음 손실 예측

융해손실 = 융해율 × 연수 × (1 + 온도증가 × 민감도)

해수면 기여도

전 지구적 해수면 상승에 대한 빙하 기여도 계산

해수면 = (빙하질량 × 융해비율) / 해양면적

Glacier Monitoring Protocol

Test API endpoints for glacier data retrieval and analysis.

Available Endpoints

GET /api/v1/glaciers - List all monitored glaciers

GET /api/v1/glaciers/{id} - Get specific glacier data

POST /api/v1/glaciers/{id}/measure - Submit new measurements

GET /api/v1/glaciers/{id}/trends - Get historical trends

System Integration

Connect glacier monitoring with satellite systems, climate models, and water resource management.

🛰️ Satellite Systems

Integration with:

  • Landsat, Sentinel-2 (optical imaging)
  • SAR satellites (ice thickness)
  • GRACE (gravity anomalies)
  • ICESat-2 (elevation changes)

🌍 Climate Models

Integration with:

  • CMIP6 climate projections
  • Regional climate models
  • Temperature and precipitation data
  • Glacier mass balance models
