# WIA-ENE-013: Distributed Energy Standard 🔌

## Overview

The WIA-ENE-013 Distributed Energy Standard provides comprehensive specifications for distributed energy resources (DER), enabling seamless integration of renewable energy sources, energy storage systems, microgrids, virtual power plants, and peer-to-peer energy trading into modern smart grids.

## Philosophy

**홍익인간 (弘益人間) (홍익인간)** - "Benefit All Humanity"

This standard embodies the principle of creating clean, affordable, reliable energy systems that benefit all people, addressing climate change while expanding energy access and improving quality of life globally.

## Key Features

- **DER Integration:** Solar PV, wind, batteries, EV charging, CHP systems
- **Microgrids:** Islanding capability, black start, grid synchronization
- **Virtual Power Plants:** Resource aggregation, demand response, grid services
- **P2P Trading:** Blockchain settlement, smart contracts, prosumer markets
- **Advanced Control:** MPC, machine learning, distributed optimization
- **Standards Compliance:** IEEE 1547, IEC 61850, Modbus, OpenADR, IEEE 2030.5

## Technical Specifications

### Phase 1: Foundation
Core protocols, data models, and device integration standards for DER components.

### Phase 2: Microgrids
Islanding capabilities, black start procedures, grid synchronization, and resilience features.

### Phase 3: Markets
P2P energy trading, VPP aggregation, blockchain settlement mechanisms.

### Phase 4: AI & Future
Machine learning, predictive control algorithms, autonomous grid operation.

## Use Cases

- **Residential Communities:** Solar + battery microgrids with P2P trading
- **Industrial Campuses:** VPP aggregation for demand response and frequency regulation
- **Agricultural Networks:** Distributed renewable resources with islanding capability
- **Urban Districts:** Integrated energy management with EV charging and V2G

## Technologies Covered

- Solar photovoltaics (monocrystalline, polycrystalline, thin-film, perovskite)
- Wind energy systems (small-scale to community wind)
- Energy storage (Li-ion, flow batteries, thermal, mechanical)
- Combined heat and power (CHP/cogeneration)
- Electric vehicle charging and vehicle-to-grid (V2G)
- Demand response and flexible loads

## Standards and Protocols

- **IEEE 1547-2018:** Interconnection standard for DER
- **IEC 61850:** Substation automation and DER communication
- **Modbus/SunSpec:** Industry-standard device communication
- **DNP3:** SCADA system protocols
- **OpenADR 2.0b:** Automated demand response
- **IEEE 2030.5:** Smart energy profile for DER
