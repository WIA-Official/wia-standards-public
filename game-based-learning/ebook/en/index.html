About This Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on the WIA Game-Based Learning Standard (WIA-EDU-014). This book covers everything you need to know about creating, implementing, and managing educational games and gamified learning experiences that engage students and enhance learning outcomes.

Game-based learning represents a transformative approach to education, combining the motivational power of games with rigorous learning objectives. This standard ensures that educational games are pedagogically sound, accessible, and aligned with curriculum standards while maintaining the engagement and fun that make games effective learning tools.
