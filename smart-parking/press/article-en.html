WIA Announces Smart Parking Standard - Revolutionary Solution to Urban Parking

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the WIA-AUTO-010 Smart Parking standard to solve urban parking problems. This standard completely revolutionizes the parking experience through real-time parking space detection, AI-based reservation systems, automatic payment, and autonomous vehicle integration.

The Smartification of Parking

Parking space shortage is a serious problem in major cities worldwide, including the Republic of Korea. WIA-AUTO-010 combines IoT sensors, computer vision, and AI algorithms to find available parking spaces in real-time, guide optimal routes, and handle payments automatically. Drivers no longer need to wander searching for parking spaces.

Key Features:
  • Real-time Space Detection: Identify available parking spaces in real-time using IoT sensors and cameras
  • AI Reservation System: Learn usage patterns to reserve optimal parking times and locations
  • Automatic Payment: Calculate and process fees automatically upon exit
  • Route Guidance: Real-time shortest route guidance within parking facilities
  • Autonomous Vehicle Integration: Support automatic parking for autonomous vehicles

Environment and Urban Efficiency

Vehicles circling urban areas searching for parking are a major cause of traffic congestion and air pollution. Smart parking systems reduce this unnecessary driving by up to 30%, reducing carbon emissions and improving traffic flow. Dynamic pricing also optimizes parking space utilization.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The Smart Parking standard goes beyond mere convenience to improve urban sustainability and citizens' quality of life." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), all drivers will be free from parking stress, and cities will operate more cleanly and efficiently."

"Parking is one of the biggest stress factors in urban life. The WIA-AUTO-010 standard fundamentally solves this problem, enabling everyone to enjoy more comfortable and relaxed city living."

Global Expansion

Major cities and parking operators are adopting WIA-AUTO-010 to improve services. Integration with existing parking systems is easy through TypeScript SDK and RESTful API, and a smarter mobility ecosystem is being built through linkages with WIA-INTENT and WIA-OMNI-API.
