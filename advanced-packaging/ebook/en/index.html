WIA-ADVANCED_PACKAGING v1.0

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Advanced Semiconductor Packaging standards defined by WIA (World Certification Industry Association). This ebook covers the complete spectrum of modern packaging technologies that enable the continued advancement of semiconductor performance beyond traditional Moore's Law scaling.

Executive Overview

Advanced semiconductor packaging has emerged as the critical enabler of continued performance improvements in integrated circuits. As traditional transistor scaling faces fundamental physical limits, packaging innovations such as 2.5D/3D integration, chiplet architectures, and heterogeneous integration provide pathways to increased performance, reduced power consumption, and enhanced functionality.

Standard Identifier: WIA-ADVANCED_PACKAGING
Version: 1.0
Domain: Semiconductor Manufacturing and Integration
Status: Active Standard

Scope and Objectives

This standard establishes comprehensive guidelines for advanced semiconductor packaging technologies, addressing the full lifecycle from design through manufacturing, testing, and reliability assurance. The primary objectives include:

  • Define interoperability standards for chiplet-based designs
  • Establish thermal and electrical specifications for 2.5D/3D packages
  • Specify testing methodologies for advanced interconnects
  • Provide reliability criteria for heterogeneous integration
  • Enable ecosystem-wide compatibility across packaging technologies

Prerequisites and Audience

This ebook is designed for semiconductor engineers, packaging specialists, system architects, and technical managers involved in advanced packaging development and deployment. Readers should have foundational knowledge of:

  • Basic semiconductor physics and device operation
  • Integrated circuit design fundamentals
  • Electronic packaging concepts
  • Manufacturing process familiarity
Note: This standard is continuously updated to reflect the rapidly evolving landscape of advanced packaging technologies. Users are encouraged to verify compliance with the latest version of WIA-ADVANCED_PACKAGING specifications.
