WIA Announces Ecosystem Restoration International Standard

WIA-ECOSYSTEM_RESTORATION v1.0 | Climate ${CATEGORY_EN} Environment Sector | December 27, 2025
The World Certification Industry Association (WIA), under SmileStory Inc. of the Republic of Korea, has officially announced the international standard 'WIA-ECOSYSTEM_RESTORATION' for Ecosystem Restoration.

WIA has established a comprehensive standard to ensure global interoperability and security for Ecosystem Restoration technology. This standard aims to accelerate innovation in the Climate ${CATEGORY_EN} Environment sector and provide consistent quality and reliability worldwide.

The WIA-ECOSYSTEM_RESTORATION standard provides comprehensive technical specifications including data structures, API interfaces, security protocols, and certification procedures. This enables businesses and developers to build Ecosystem Restoration solutions that are recognized in the global market.

"The WIA-ECOSYSTEM_RESTORATION standard marks a crucial milestone in defining the future of Ecosystem Restoration technology. Through this standard, companies worldwide will be able to provide safe and efficient Ecosystem Restoration services."

- Yeon Sam-heum, CEO, SmileStory Inc. / WIA

Key features of the standard include:

  • Open Architecture: Seamless integration across various platforms and systems
  • Enhanced Security: Latest encryption and authentication mechanisms
  • Scalability: Flexible scaling from small to large enterprise deployments
  • Global Compatibility: Perfect compatibility with international standards
