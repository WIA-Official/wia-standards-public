Core Features

📋

Order Management

Comprehensive order lifecycle management from placement to delivery completion.

  • Real-time order status tracking
  • Multi-restaurant ordering
  • Order modification & cancellation
  • Order history & analytics
  • Scheduled orders
📍

Real-time Tracking

Live GPS tracking and ETAs for transparent delivery experience.

  • Driver location tracking
  • Accurate ETA calculation
  • Route optimization
  • Push notifications
  • Geofencing capabilities
🚴

Driver Dispatch

Intelligent driver assignment and fleet management system.

  • Auto-assignment algorithms
  • Driver availability tracking
  • Performance metrics
  • Multi-delivery batching
  • Driver ratings & feedback
🍽️

Restaurant Integration

Seamless POS and kitchen management system integration.

  • Menu synchronization
  • Inventory management
  • Kitchen display integration
  • Operating hours control
  • Special instructions handling
💳

Payment Processing

Secure, multi-method payment processing with split payments.

  • Multiple payment methods
  • Digital wallets
  • Split payments
  • Tip processing
  • Refund management
⭐

Customer Experience

Enhanced user experience with personalization and support.

  • Personalized recommendations
  • Favorite orders & addresses
  • Ratings & reviews
  • Loyalty programs
  • Customer support chat

Use Cases

🏢 Restaurant Chains

Multi-location management with centralized menu control and analytics across all branches.

🍕 Independent Restaurants

Easy integration without complex infrastructure, enabling small businesses to compete globally.

Delivery Fleets

Optimize routes, manage drivers, and increase delivery efficiency with intelligent algorithms.

👻 Ghost Kitchens

Delivery-only operations with multiple virtual brands from a single kitchen location.

🏬 Corporate Catering

Large-scale orders with advanced scheduling and group payment splitting capabilities.

🛒 Grocery Delivery

Extend beyond prepared food to include grocery and retail item delivery services.
