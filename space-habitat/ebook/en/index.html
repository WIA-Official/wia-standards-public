Standard Introduction

The WIA Space Habitat Standard (WIA-SPACE-007) provides a comprehensive framework for designing, building, and operating sustainable human habitats in space and on planetary surfaces. This standard covers all aspects of space habitation including radiation shielding, pressure maintenance, artificial gravity, closed ecosystems, life support systems, construction technology, energy management, and psychological factors.

Applicable to diverse space habitats including space stations, lunar bases, and Mars settlements, this standard integrates the latest technologies and research including 3D printing construction, ISRU (In-Situ Resource Utilization), regenerative life support systems, and psychological support.
