## 📋 Overview

The **WIA-SPACE-007 Space Habitat Standard** provides complete specifications for creating long-term human settlements in space environments including:

- 🛡️ **Radiation Shielding** - Multi-layer protection from cosmic rays and solar particles
- 🔄 **Artificial Gravity** - Rotating structures to maintain crew health
- 🌱 **Closed Ecosystems** - Self-sustaining life support with BLSS
- 🏗️ **Construction** - 3D printing, inflatable modules, ISRU
- ⚡ **Energy Systems** - Solar, nuclear, and energy storage
- 🧠 **Human Factors** - Psychological support and community building
- 🌍 **Planetary Bases** - Lunar and Mars settlement planning

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Comprehensive Coverage**: All aspects of space habitation from structure to psychology
- **Science-Based**: Built on 25+ years of ISS experience and latest research
- **Practical**: Ready-to-implement specifications and guidelines
- **Future-Proof**: Scalable from small stations to large planetary bases
- **Ethical**: Respects potential extraterrestrial life and planetary protection

## 🌟 Chapters

The standard is organized into 8 comprehensive chapters:

| # | Chapter | Key Topics |
|---|---------|------------|
| 1 | **Space Habitat Overview** | History, current projects, future vision, necessity |
| 2 | **Design Principles** | Radiation, pressure, micrometeorites, thermal management |
| 3 | **Artificial Gravity** | Rotating structures, centrifugal force, Coriolis effect |
| 4 | **Closed Ecosystems** | BLSS, hydroponics, water/air recycling, waste processing |
| 5 | **Construction** | 3D printing, inflatable modules, robotic assembly, ISRU |
| 6 | **Energy & Resources** | Solar, nuclear, batteries, water mining, mineral extraction |
| 7 | **Psychological Factors** | Isolation, crew selection, mental health, community |
| 8 | **Planetary Bases** | Lunar base, Mars settlement, self-sufficiency, terraforming |
