PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

    "Hongik Ingan" - Benefit All Humanity

    Legal frameworks must evolve to accommodate the possibility of life
    extension through cryopreservation. These standards ensure that preserved
    individuals retain appropriate legal protections and can be fully
    restored to legal personhood upon successful revival.

    The fundamental principle underlying CRYO-LEGAL is that temporary
    biological suspension should not permanently extinguish legal rights.
    Every individual deserves the protection of law, regardless of their
    current biological state.
