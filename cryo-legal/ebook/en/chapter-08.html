Conclusion
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

CRYO-LEGAL establishes a comprehensive framework for managing the legal
status of cryopreserved individuals. By defining clear status categories,
transition rules, and multi-jurisdiction protocols, this standard ensures
that preserved individuals receive appropriate legal protection and can be
fully restored to legal personhood upon successful revival.

The framework embodies the principle of "Hongik Ingan" - benefiting all
humanity by ensuring that legal systems evolve to protect all individuals,
regardless of their current biological state. As cryopreservation technology
advances, CRYO-LEGAL will continue to evolve, maintaining its commitment
to human dignity and legal clarity.
