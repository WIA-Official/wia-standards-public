MAIL

IN-PERSON

Formats:

  Initial review: 24 hours

LOW:
  Initial review: 7 days

5.3 Investigation Process

Investigation Stages
====================

STAGE 1: Initial Assessment
===========================
Duration: 1-3 days

Actions:
  - Complaint logged and categorized
  - Priority assigned
  - Complainant contacted (if not anonymous)
  - Entity's certification status checked
  - Previous complaints reviewed
  - Initial evidence assessment

Outcomes:
  - Proceed to full investigation
  - Request additional information
  - Refer to appropriate authority
  - Close (insufficient basis)


STAGE 2: Evidence Gathering
===========================
Duration: 3-14 days

Actions:
  - Collect all available evidence
  - Interview complainant (detailed)
  - Interview witnesses
  - Obtain documentation
  - Review historical records
  - Prepare for entity contact

Evidence Types:
  - Photographs/videos
  - Documents/records
  - Witness statements
  - Expert opinions
  - Historical data


STAGE 3: Entity Response
========================
Duration: 7-14 days

Actions:
  - Entity notified of complaint
  - Details shared (protect complainant)
  - Response requested
  - Documentation requested
  - Self-assessment requested

Entity Rights:
  - Know the nature of complaint
  - Provide response and evidence
  - Identify relevant witnesses
  - Request investigation timeline


STAGE 4: On-Site Investigation
==============================
Duration: 1-3 days (when warranted)

Triggers:
  - Evidence suggests serious violations
  - Contradictory information
  - Complainant credibility established
  - Entity response inadequate

Actions:
  - Unannounced or short-notice visit
  - Full facility inspection
  - Animal welfare assessment
  - Staff interviews
  - Document verification
  - Evidence collection


STAGE 5: Analysis and Decision
==============================
Duration: 7-14 days

Actions:
  - All evidence weighed
  - Entity response considered
  - Standards compliance assessed
  - Expert consultation (if needed)
  - Decision reached
  - Report prepared

Decision Options:
  - Complaint substantiated (action required)
  - Complaint partially substantiated
  - Complaint not substantiated
  - Insufficient evidence
  - Referred to authority

5.4 Whistleblower Protection

Protection Principles
=====================

CONFIDENTIALITY
  - Identity protected throughout
  - Need-to-know basis only
  - No sharing with entity
  - Secure data storage

ANTI-RETALIATION
  - Retaliation prohibited
  - Legal protections apply
  - Monitoring for retaliation
  - Support if retaliation occurs

GOOD FAITH PROTECTION
  - Good faith complaints protected
  - Even if ultimately unsubstantiated
  - Honest belief sufficient
  - Process followed is key


Employee Whistleblowers
=======================

Special Protections:
  - Employment protection
  - Confidential channels
  - Anonymous reporting option
  - Legal support information

Employer Obligations:
  - No termination for reporting
  - No demotion or penalty
  - No hostile work environment
  - Investigate reported retaliation


Anonymous Reporting
===================

Available:
  - Online portal (anonymous option)
  - Anonymous tip line
  - Third-party reporting services

Limitations:
  - Cannot receive updates directly
  - Must check status via reference number
  - Some investigations limited without contact
  - Encouraged to provide contact if comfortable

Protections:
  - IP address not logged
  - No registration required
  - Evidence accepted without identity

5.5 Outcomes and Sanctions

Possible Outcomes
=================

IF COMPLAINT SUBSTANTIATED:

Level 1: Warning
  - Formal written warning
  - Corrective action required
  - Timeline for resolution
  - Recorded in certification file

Level 2: Enhanced Surveillance
  - Increased audit frequency
  - Unannounced inspections
  - Progress monitoring
  - Duration: 6-12 months

Level 3: Conditions on Certification
  - Specific improvements required
  - Deadline for compliance
  - Follow-up verification
  - Potential suspension if unmet

Level 4: Tier Downgrade
  - Certification level reduced
  - Public notification
  - Re-assessment required for upgrade
  - Minimum period before upgrade

Level 5: Suspension
  - Certification suspended
  - Cannot claim certified status
  - Public notification
  - Corrective action required
  - Reinstatement audit needed

Level 6: Revocation
  - Certification cancelled
  - Public notification
  - Cooling-off period for reapplication
  - May be permanent for severe cases


IF COMPLAINT NOT SUBSTANTIATED:

  - Entity notified of closure
  - Complainant notified (if not anonymous)
  - No action against entity
  - File retained for pattern analysis


IF REFERRED TO AUTHORITIES:

  - Law enforcement notified
  - Animal protection agencies notified
  - Evidence shared appropriately
  - Certification action pending outcome

5.6 Public Transparency

Public Information
==================

Always Public:
  - Entity certification status
  - Certification tier
  - Suspension or revocation
  - Sanction summary (after final)

Public Registry Entry:
  Entity: [Name]
  Status: [Active/Suspended/Revoked]
  Tier: [BRONZE/SILVER/GOLD/PLATINUM]
  Last Audit: [Date]
  Sanctions: [Yes/No - Details if Yes]


Sanctions Registry
==================

Information Published:
  - Entity name and location
  - Nature of violation (summary)
  - Sanction applied
  - Date of sanction
  - Current status

Retention:
  - Warnings: 1 year
  - Enhanced surveillance: 2 years
  - Conditions: 2 years
  - Downgrades: 3 years
  - Suspensions: 5 years
  - Revocations: Permanent


Complainant Communication
=========================

Progress Updates:
  - Acknowledgment: within 24-48 hours
  - Initial assessment: within 7 days
  - Investigation update: monthly
  - Final outcome: within 7 days of decision

Information Shared:
  - Complaint received and categorized
  - Investigation underway
  - Outcome of investigation
  - Action taken (summary)

Not Shared:
  - Detailed entity response
  - Internal deliberations
  - Other complainant details
  - Confidential evidence
