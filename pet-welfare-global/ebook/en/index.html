WIA-PET-WELFARE-GLOBAL

Global Animal Welfare Certification Standard

"Hongik Ingan: True welfare recognizes that every creature deserves dignity and care."

About This Standard

WIA-PET-WELFARE-GLOBAL establishes a comprehensive framework for animal welfare certification that transcends borders. Built on the internationally recognized Five Freedoms, this standard provides measurable criteria for assessing and certifying facilities, organizations, and individuals who work with companion animals.

This ebook explains the standard through practical examples, implementation guidance, and real-world scenarios that demonstrate how welfare certification creates better outcomes for animals, guardians, and the industry.
