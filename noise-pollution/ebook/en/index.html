📖 About This Standard

Welcome to the WIA-ENE-028 Noise Pollution Standards, a comprehensive framework for understanding, measuring, monitoring, and mitigating noise pollution in urban and industrial environments. This standard represents the convergence of acoustic science, environmental health, smart city technology, and regulatory compliance.

Noise pollution affects billions of people worldwide, contributing to hearing loss, cardiovascular disease, sleep disturbance, cognitive impairment in children, and reduced quality of life. With urbanization accelerating globally, the need for robust noise management systems has never been more critical.

This ebook provides eight comprehensive chapters covering the full spectrum of noise pollution science and practice—from fundamental acoustics to cutting-edge AI-powered prediction systems. Whether you're an environmental engineer, urban planner, public health official, or concerned citizen, you'll find actionable knowledge and practical tools throughout.

Built on the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)—broadly benefiting humanity—this standard aims to create quieter, healthier cities where all people can thrive.
