Standard Overview

WIA-CITY-012 - Smart Water Management is a comprehensive standard for Urban Resource Management. This ebook covers the latest technology, implementation strategies, best practices, and real-world case studies.

As of 2025, the field has reached unprecedented sophistication with AI-powered automation, advanced sensor technologies, cloud-edge hybrid architectures, and predictive analytics. This standard ensures systems meet rigorous requirements for efficiency, quality, interoperability, and security.

Key Benefits

  • Energy Efficiency: Achieve 60-85% resource savings in typical deployments
  • Operational Excellence: >99% system uptime with predictive maintenance
  • User Experience: Enhanced satisfaction through personalized control and automatic optimization
  • Cost Savings: 2-4 year payback periods in commercial applications
  • Sustainability: Reduce carbon emissions 60-80% and support environmental goals
  • Compliance: Alignment with ISO, IEC, IEEE, and green building certifications
