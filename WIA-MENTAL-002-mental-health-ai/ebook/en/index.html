Welcome to the WIA-MENTAL-002 Mental Health AI Standard.

This comprehensive guide explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and mental healthcare, providing evidence-based protocols, implementation strategies, and ethical frameworks for AI-powered mental health assessment, screening, and support systems.

Mental health affects over 1 billion people worldwide, yet access to quality care remains limited. AI systems offer the potential to democratize mental healthcare, providing accessible, affordable, and effective support to those in need while augmenting the capabilities of healthcare professionals.

This ebook covers cutting-edge research, practical implementations, and real-world case studies to help organizations build responsible, effective, and compassionate AI systems for mental health.
