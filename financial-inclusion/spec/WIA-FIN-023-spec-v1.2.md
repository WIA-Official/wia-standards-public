## [Sections 1-9: All v1.1 requirements plus additions below]

## 10. Open Banking & APIs

### 10.1 Open Banking Framework
MUST support:
- Account information services
- Payment initiation services
- Confirmation of funds
- User consent management
- Token-based authentication

### 10.2 Third-Party Integration
MUST provide APIs for:
- Balance checks
- Transaction history
- Payment initiation
- Account verification
- Consent management

## 11. Climate Finance

### 11.1 Green Products
SHOULD offer:
- Green loans (lower rates for sustainable practices)
- Parametric weather insurance
- Carbon credit financing
- Climate adaptation savings accounts

### 11.2 Environmental Impact
SHOULD track:
- Carbon footprint of operations
- Green loan disbursements
- Climate insurance coverage
- Sustainable agriculture financing

## 12. Accessibility

### 12.1 Voice Interfaces
SHOULD support:
- Voice commands in local languages
- IVR banking
- Voice biometric authentication
- Audio confirmations

### 12.2 Disability Access
MUST comply with:
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards
- Screen reader compatibility
- Large text options
- Color contrast requirements
- Simplified language options

## 13. Digital Identity

### 13.1 Identity Systems
MUST support:
- National digital ID integration
- Self-sovereign identity (where available)
- Blockchain-based identity
- Portable KYC

### 13.2 Privacy-Preserving KYC
SHOULD implement:
- Zero-knowledge proofs
- Selective disclosure
- Decentralized identifiers
- Verifiable credentials
