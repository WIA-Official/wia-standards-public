About This E-Book

Financial inclusion is one of the most pressing challenges of our time, with 1.7 billion adults worldwide still lacking access to basic financial services. This comprehensive guide explores the barriers to financial inclusion, innovative solutions being deployed around the world, and practical frameworks for implementing inclusive financial systems.

Whether you're a policymaker, financial institution, fintech entrepreneur, or development professional, this e-book provides the knowledge and tools needed to expand financial access to underserved populations.

Topics Covered: Mobile banking, microfinance, alternative credit scoring, regulatory frameworks, digital identity, agent networks, financial literacy, and real-world case studies from across the globe.
