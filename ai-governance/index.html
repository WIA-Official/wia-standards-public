Governance Framework

Three-Layer Architecture

Strategic Layer

  • AI Vision & Strategy
  • Governance Principles
  • Executive Oversight
  • Board Accountability
  • Stakeholder Alignment

Tactical Layer

  • Policy Development
  • Risk Assessment
  • Compliance Management
  • Ethics Review Board
  • Performance Metrics

Operational Layer

  • Development Guidelines
  • Testing Protocols
  • Deployment Controls
  • Monitoring Systems
  • Incident Response

Core Principles

  • Transparency: Clear documentation and explainability of AI decisions
  • Accountability: Defined roles and responsibilities across AI lifecycle
  • Fairness: Bias detection and mitigation in AI systems
  • Privacy: Data protection and user consent management
  • Safety: Risk assessment and continuous monitoring
