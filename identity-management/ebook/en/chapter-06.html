6.1.1 Implementation Considerations

  • Security: Implement appropriate controls to protect sensitive identity data and prevent unauthorized access
  • Scalability: Design systems to handle growing numbers of users and increasing complexity
  • Usability: Balance security requirements with user experience to maintain productivity
  • Compliance: Ensure adherence to relevant regulations and industry standards
  • Integration: Support seamless integration with existing systems and future technologies
  • Monitoring: Implement comprehensive logging and alerting for security and operational visibility

6.1.2 Best Practices

Organizations implementing identity governance overview should follow these industry best practices aligned with the WIA-SEC-009 standard:

  1. Conduct thorough requirements analysis before implementation
  2. Design with security as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought
  3. Implement defense-in-depth strategies with multiple layers of protection
  4. Automate processes wherever possible to reduce errors and improve efficiency
  5. Maintain comprehensive documentation of configurations and procedures
  6. Regularly review and update implementations to address evolving threats
  7. Provide training for administrators and end-users
  8. Monitor continuously and respond promptly to security events
