7.4 Blockchain & Distributed Ledger

Immutable record-keeping using blockchain technology ensures transparency and prevents data manipulation.

Blockchain Use Cases

  • Arms Inventory Declarations: Timestamped, signed records on distributed ledger
  • Verification Results: Inspection findings with multi-party signatures
  • Confidence-Building Measures: Agreements and outcomes recorded permanently
  • Incident Records: Complete audit trail for all significant events
  • Compliance Tracking: Automated smart contracts monitor adherence

Blockchain Architecture

  • Nodes: North Korea, South Korea, UN Command, IAEA, NNSC maintain nodes
  • Consensus: Proof-of-Authority with designated validators
  • Privacy: Zero-knowledge proofs allow verification without revealing sensitive details
  • Immutability: Cryptographic hashing prevents tampering
  • Audit Trail: Complete history of all changes and signatures
