About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of 3D image sensor technology as defined by the WIA-SEMI-013 standard. Whether you're an engineer implementing depth sensing systems, a product manager evaluating 3D camera options, or a researcher exploring cutting-edge imaging technologies, this guide provides the technical depth and practical insights you need.

The guide covers three major 3D sensing technologies: Time-of-Flight (ToF), Structured Light, and Stereo Vision. Each technology has unique strengths and optimal use cases, from Apple's Face ID using structured light for sub-millimeter facial mapping, to automotive ToF sensors providing long-range depth detection, to stereo cameras enabling passive 3D reconstruction for robotics.
