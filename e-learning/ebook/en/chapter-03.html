Chapter 3

Learning Management System Architecture

A robust LMS architecture is essential for managing courses, students, and educational content at scale.

Core LMS Components

1. Course Catalog

  • Browse and search courses by category, difficulty, instructor
  • Course previews with syllabus and sample lessons
  • Ratings and reviews from previous students
  • Prerequisites and learning paths

2. Enrollment System

  • Self-enrollment vs. admin-managed enrollment
  • Payment processing for paid courses
  • Waitlists and capacity management
  • Group enrollments for corporate training

3. User Management

  • Role-based access control (student, instructor, admin)
  • User profiles with learning history
  • Single Sign-On (SSO) integration
  • Privacy and GDPR compliance

4. Content Delivery

  • Progressive content unlocking
  • Adaptive learning paths based on performance
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Offline content for mobile apps

Database Schema

Key entities in an LMS:

  • Users: Students, instructors, administrators
  • Courses: Title, description, content, pricing
  • Enrollments: Links students to courses with progress data
  • Lessons: Individual learning units (video, quiz, assignment)
  • Assessments: Quizzes, exams, assignments
  • Grades: Scores and feedback
  • Certificates: Completion records

Scalability Considerations

  • Content Delivery Network (CDN): Distribute video globally
  • Database Sharding: Partition data by region or institution
  • Caching: Redis for session management and course metadata
  • Load Balancing: Handle millions of concurrent users
  • Microservices: Separate services for video, quizzes, analytics

Progress Tracking

Students must be able to resume where they left off:

  • Track completion of individual lessons
  • Save video playback position
  • Store quiz attempts and scores
  • Calculate overall course progress percentage
  • Sync progress across devices

Discussion Forums & Community

  • Course-specific discussion boards
  • Q&A threads with instructor responses
  • Peer-to-peer study groups
  • Upvoting and best answer selection
  • Moderation tools to prevent spam

Notifications & Reminders

  • Email notifications for new course announcements
  • Push notifications for assignment due dates
  • SMS reminders for live sessions
  • Daily/weekly study streak tracking
