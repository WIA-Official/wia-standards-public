📚 Digital Textbook Standard

WIA-EDU-008 Complete Guide

About This Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on the WIA Digital Textbook Standard (WIA-EDU-008). This book covers everything you need to know about creating, managing, and implementing digital textbooks that are accessible, interactive, and universally compatible.

Digital textbooks represent the future of education, combining the best of traditional learning materials with modern technology. This standard ensures that educational content is accessible to all learners, regardless of their device, location, or abilities.
