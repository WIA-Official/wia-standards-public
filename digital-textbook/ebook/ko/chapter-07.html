7.6 Institutional Integration

Schools and universities can manage all WIA-compliant content through a unified dashboard.

Institutional Dashboard Features

  • Bulk Licensing: Purchase and deploy textbooks at scale
  • Usage Analytics: Track adoption and engagement across courses
  • Compliance Monitoring: Verify ongoing certification status
  • Student Management: Provision and revoke access
  • Cost Tracking: Monitor expenditures and ROI
  • Content Updates: Centralized management of version updates

7.9 International Harmonization

WIA-EDU-008 aligns with international educational standards and frameworks.

Compatible Standards

  • ISO/IEC TS 30135: Information technology — Learning, education, and training (LET) — Modular and mobile digital textbooks
  • IMS Global Standards: LTI, OneRoster, Caliper Analytics
  • IEEE LOM: Learning Object Metadata
  • SCORM/xAPI: Content packaging and learning tracking
  • Dublin Core: Metadata standards

7.10 Future-Proofing Through Extensibility

The standard includes extension points for emerging technologies without breaking compatibility.

Extension Mechanisms

  • Custom Metadata: Publisher-specific fields in dedicated namespaces
  • Plugin Architecture: Support for AR/VR, AI tutors, adaptive learning
  • Backward Compatibility: New versions must support old content
  • Deprecation Policy: 3-year notice before removing features
