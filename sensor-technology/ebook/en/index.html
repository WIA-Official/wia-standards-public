🔬 Core Sensor Technologies

📡 MEMS Technology

Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems combine mechanical elements with electronics at microscale. Applications include accelerometers in smartphones, gyroscopes for navigation, and pressure sensors for altitude detection.

📸 CMOS Image Sensors

Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor sensors convert light to electrical signals. Sony IMX and Samsung ISOCELL lead the market with advanced pixel technologies for mobile and automotive imaging.

🔦 LiDAR Systems

Light Detection and Ranging uses laser pulses to measure distances with millimeter accuracy. Essential for autonomous vehicles, robotics, and 3D mapping applications.

📏 Time-of-Flight (ToF)

ToF sensors measure depth by calculating light travel time. Used in face recognition, gesture control, and AR/VR applications with real-time 3D scene understanding.

🌬️ Gas Sensors

Detect and measure gas concentrations using various principles: electrochemical, semiconductor, infrared absorption, and catalytic combustion for safety and environmental monitoring.

💚 Biosensors

Convert biological responses into electrical signals for health monitoring. PPG sensors for heart rate, ECG for cardiac activity, and SpO2 for blood oxygen levels.
