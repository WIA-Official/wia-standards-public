# WIA-SEMI-012: Testing Protocols

## Performance Testing

### Noise Measurement
- Conditions: Sensor at rest, stable temperature
- Duration: Minimum 60 seconds
- Sampling: Maximum sensor rate
- Analysis: Allan variance, power spectral density

### Frequency Response
- Input: Sinusoidal stimulus, varying frequency
- Measure: Amplitude ratio, phase shift
- Bandwidth: Frequency at -3dB attenuation

## Environmental Testing

### Temperature Cycling
- Range: -40°C to +85°C
- Ramp rate: 5°C/minute maximum
- Soak time: 30 minutes at each extreme
- Cycles: Minimum 10

### Humidity Testing
- Conditions: 85% RH, 85°C, 1000 hours
- Measurement: Drift in key parameters
