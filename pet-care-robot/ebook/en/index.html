About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA-ROB-014 Pet Care Robot Standard, a unified framework for automated pet care systems. From smart feeders and monitoring cameras to interactive toys and health tracking devices, this standard ensures interoperability, data security, and optimal care for our beloved pets.

Target Audience: Pet care technology developers, veterinarians, product designers, and pet wellness professionals.

Standard Version: 1.0
