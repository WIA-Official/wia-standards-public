Welcome to the comprehensive guide for WIA-MENTAL-004: Anxiety Management. This standard provides evidence-based digital tools and protocols for anxiety assessment, intervention, and management.

In an era where mental health challenges are increasingly prevalent, this standard offers clinicians, developers, and healthcare organizations a robust framework for implementing anxiety management systems that are clinically validated, technically sound, and ethically designed.

This ebook covers eight comprehensive chapters, from foundational understanding of anxiety disorders to cutting-edge digital interventions and future directions in the field.
