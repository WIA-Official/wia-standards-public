WIA-AGRI-027: Hydroponics Standard

Advanced Water-Based Cultivation Systems

Revolutionary Soilless Agriculture

WIA-AGRI-027 defines comprehensive standards for hydroponic farming systems, enabling efficient, sustainable, and scalable water-based crop cultivation. From nutrient management to automated monitoring, this standard covers the complete hydroponics ecosystem.

🌱

Nutrient Solutions

Standardized formulations for optimal plant growth with precise pH, EC, and nutrient ratios for different crop types and growth stages.

⚙️

System Design

Specifications for NFT, DWC, Ebb & Flow, Drip, Aeroponic, and other hydroponic system configurations with best practices.

📊

Monitoring & Control

IoT-enabled sensors and automation protocols for real-time monitoring of pH, EC, DO, temperature, and water levels.

🔬

Quality Assurance

Testing protocols and certification standards ensuring food safety, traceability, and compliance with agricultural regulations.

🌍

Sustainability

Water recycling systems, energy efficiency metrics, and environmental impact assessment frameworks for sustainable operations.

🤖

AI Integration

Machine learning models for crop prediction, disease detection, and optimization of growing conditions through data analytics.
