📖 About This Ebook

This ebook is the official guide to the WIA Blockchain Finance Standard for decentralized finance interoperability. It provides comprehensive information for blockchain developers, DeFi protocols, financial institutions, and Web3 builders to understand and implement standardized blockchain finance solutions.

Target Audience

  • Blockchain Developers: Smart contract developers and Web3 engineers
  • DeFi Protocols: Decentralized exchange (DEX) and lending platform teams
  • Financial Institutions: Banks and fintech companies exploring blockchain
  • Token Projects: Cryptocurrency and NFT project founders
  • Security Auditors: Smart contract security and audit teams
  • Regulators: Blockchain policy makers and compliance officers
