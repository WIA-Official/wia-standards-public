WIA Announces Tourism Data Standard - The Future of Smart Travel

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the WIA-IND-017 Tourism Data standard. This standard revolutionizes travel experiences and promotes sustainable tourism by integrating tourist attraction information, visitor statistics, real-time crowd density, cultural heritage data, and accessibility information.

Data-Driven Smart Tourism

The Republic of Korea is a world-class tourist destination with rich cultural heritage and modern tourism infrastructure. WIA-IND-017 provides nationwide tourist attractions, accommodation, dining, and transportation information in standardized formats. Real-time crowd density information helps visitors avoid busy times, and multi-language support gives foreign tourists easy access to information. Accessibility data enables people with disabilities to travel comfortably.

Key Features:
  • Tourist Attraction Data: Standardized information for landmarks, museums, cultural heritage
  • Real-time Crowd Density: Live visitor counts and wait times
  • Multi-language Support: All information available in 20+ languages
  • Accessibility Information: Detailed information for wheelchair users and visually/hearing impaired
  • Sustainability Metrics: Eco-friendly options and carbon footprint tracking

Cultural Heritage Preservation and Sharing

This standard digitizes the Republic of Korea's precious cultural heritage, including UNESCO World Heritage Sites, to share with the world. High-resolution images, historical context, and conservation status information enhance educational value and build digital archives for future generations.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The Tourism Data standard enables all travelers to enjoy richer and safer trips." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this technology will promote cross-cultural understanding, revitalize local economies, and protect the environment and cultural heritage through sustainable tourism."

"Travel is the best way to understand the world. The WIA-IND-017 standard helps everyone travel easily and safely while respecting and learning about different cultures."
