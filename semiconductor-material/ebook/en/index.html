About This eBook

Comprehensive Coverage

This eBook provides in-depth coverage of all critical semiconductor materials used in modern chip manufacturing, from substrate preparation to final metallization. We cover the entire materials ecosystem required for advanced logic and memory devices.

Latest Technology

Focused on cutting-edge 2nm and sub-2nm technology nodes, including EUV photoresists, metal oxide resists, molybdenum interconnects, and advanced high-k dielectrics. Stay current with the latest developments in semiconductor materials science.

Industry Standard

Based on the WIA-SEMI-MAT-001 specification, this eBook establishes standardized requirements for semiconductor materials qualification, testing methodologies, and quality assurance protocols used throughout the global semiconductor supply chain.
