Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-BIO-011, a comprehensive international standard for Biosensor technology, as free and open source under MIT License. This announcement positions the Republic of Korea as a leader in global technology standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

The Biosensor field currently suffers from fragmented approaches with inconsistent data formats and APIs across manufacturers. Developers must learn dozens of SDKs, and lack of standardization severely limits interoperability and innovation speed.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum, CEO:
"Biosensor technology should no longer be the privilege of a few corporations. Developers and researchers worldwide deserve the right to innovate with this technology. This isn't a technology problem—it's a matter of equal opportunity."

WIA's Solution: WIA-BIO-011

WIA-BIO-011 standardizes every aspect of Biosensor from data formats to APIs, protocols, and integration guidelines.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, and more—all released as free and open source.
