About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA-UNI-009 Healthcare Integration standard, which establishes unified inter-Korean healthcare systems. Learn how medical records, disease control, hospital networks, and pharmaceutical systems can be integrated across the Korean Peninsula to save lives and improve public health for all Korean people.

This standard represents a critical step toward reunification, prioritizing human health and wellbeing above political boundaries. Through advanced technology, careful privacy protection, and international cooperation, healthcare integration can become a reality.
