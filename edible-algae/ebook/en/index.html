About This Ebook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the WIA Edible Algae Standard (WIA-AGRI-033). This standard defines the global framework for edible algae cultivation, including spirulina, chlorella, seaweed farming, and microalgae nutrition for sustainable protein production.

Whether you're an algae farmer looking to optimize production, a nutritionist exploring sustainable protein sources, or a researcher developing aquaculture technology, this ebook provides everything you need to understand and implement the WIA edible algae standard.
