WIA-AGRI-023 Complete Ebook

About This Ebook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on the WIA Deep Sea Aquaculture Standard (WIA-AGRI-023). This ebook provides in-depth knowledge about sustainable deep-sea fish farming, offshore aquaculture technologies, environmental management, and the future of ocean-based protein production.

Through 8 detailed chapters, you'll learn about the technologies, protocols, best practices, and innovations driving the deep-sea aquaculture industry toward a sustainable future.

03
