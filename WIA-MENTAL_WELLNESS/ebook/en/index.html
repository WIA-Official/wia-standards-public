About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the WIA-MENTAL_WELLNESS standard. This ebook provides in-depth coverage of mental health assessment protocols, digital therapeutics, teletherapy standards, data privacy, crisis intervention, mindfulness practices, workplace wellness, AI-powered mental health support, and ethical guidelines.

Mental wellness is a fundamental human right, and this standard aims to make quality mental health care accessible, safe, and effective through digital technologies while maintaining the highest professional and ethical standards.
