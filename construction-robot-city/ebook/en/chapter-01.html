WIA-CITY-007 establishes standards for construction robots that protect workers, improve efficiency, and build better structures for all people.

1.1 The Construction Revolution

Construction is one of humanity's oldest industries, yet it remains one of the most dangerous and least automated. While manufacturing has seen productivity increase by 760% since 1947, construction productivity has remained nearly flat. This is changing with the advent of construction robotics.

1.1.1 Industry Challenges

Challenge Traditional Impact Robotic Solution
Worker Safety High injury/fatality rates Robots handle dangerous tasks
Labor Shortage Aging workforce, skill gaps Augment human capabilities
Quality Consistency Variable human performance Precision automation
Schedule Delays Weather, labor availability 24/7 operation capability
Cost Overruns Rework, inefficiency First-time-right execution

1.2 What is WIA-CITY-007?

WIA-CITY-007 is the international standard for construction robotics, defining safety requirements, performance specifications, and integration protocols for automated construction systems.
