7.1 Automated Inspection

Construction robots enable unprecedented quality verification through continuous, automated inspection. Every element can be measured, documented, and verified against specifications.

7.1.1 Inspection Technologies

Technology Capability Application
LiDAR Scanning ±2mm 3D accuracy Dimensional verification
Photogrammetry High-res surface mapping Visual defect detection
Thermal Imaging 0.1°C resolution Insulation, moisture, electrical
Ultrasonic Internal flaw detection Concrete integrity, welds
AI Vision Automated defect classification Crack, alignment, finish quality
