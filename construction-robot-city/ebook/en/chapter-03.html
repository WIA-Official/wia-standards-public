3.1.1 Phase Overview

Phase Purpose Key Components
Phase 1: Safety Ensure worker protection Safety zones, sensors, e-stops
Phase 2: Operations Execute construction tasks BIM integration, task automation
Phase 3: Collaboration Human-robot teamwork Role definition, communication
Phase 4: Quality Verify and document Inspection, as-built records

3.2 Construction Readiness Levels (CRL)

WIA-CITY-007 defines four Construction Readiness Levels to address different automation needs and capabilities.

Level Autonomy Supervision Application
CRL-1 (Basic) Manual control Constant operator Remote demolition
CRL-2 (Supervised) Semi-autonomous 1:1 supervision Bricklaying robots
CRL-3 (Collaborative) Collaborative autonomy 1:3 supervision Inspection drones
CRL-4 (Full) Full autonomy 1:10 supervision Autonomous excavators
