8.2 Certification Process

Step Activity Duration
1. Application Submit scope, robot types, CRL target 1 week
2. Gap Assessment Identify compliance gaps 2-4 weeks
3. Implementation Address gaps, deploy systems 1-24 months
4. Documentation Prepare evidence package 2-4 weeks
5. Site Audit Third-party verification 1-2 weeks
6. Certification Certificate issued 1-2 weeks

8.9 Conclusion

Construction robotics represents a transformative opportunity to make construction safer, more efficient, and more sustainable. WIA-CITY-007 provides the framework for responsible, standardized deployment of these technologies.

By following the four-phase architecture—Safety, Operations, Collaboration, and Quality—organizations can successfully integrate robots while protecting workers and delivering superior results. The future of construction is human-robot collaboration, building better for all humanity.

In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)—benefiting all humanity—construction robots protect workers, improve quality, and build structures that serve communities for generations.
