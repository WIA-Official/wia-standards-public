2.1 Technical Challenges

2.1.1 Unstructured Environment

Unlike factory floors, construction sites are dynamic, unpredictable environments. Robots must navigate changing terrain, weather conditions, and coexisting activities.

Challenge Description Impact
Terrain Variability Mud, slopes, debris, uneven surfaces Mobility and stability issues
Weather Exposure Rain, wind, temperature extremes Sensor degradation, material issues
Dynamic Changes Site evolves daily Requires real-time adaptation
Material Handling Varied sizes, weights, types Gripper and manipulation challenges

2.2 Safety Challenges

⚠️ Construction Site Hazards

Construction sites present unique safety challenges for robotic systems. Falling objects, moving equipment, confined spaces, and electrical hazards all require robust safety protocols. A robot operating near workers must detect and respond to human presence within milliseconds.

Hazard Type Risk Mitigation
Collision Robot-human impact Proximity sensors, speed limiting
Falling Objects Dropped materials Secure gripping, exclusion zones
Tip-over Robot instability Center of gravity monitoring
Electrical Power system faults Ground fault protection, isolation
