8.1 WIA-AIR-SHIELD Certification Levels

WIA-AIR-SHIELD certification validates that your implementation provides genuine wireless privacy protection. There are three certification levels:

8.1.1 Level 1: Basic Certification

🛡️ WIA-AIR-SHIELD CERTIFIED

Requirements:

  • Basic encryption (WPA3 minimum)
  • Evil twin detection
  • Traffic encryption
  • Threat logging
  • User notifications

Suitable for: Home networks, personal devices, small businesses

Compliance Level: 40% of full specification

8.2.4 Certification Decision

  • Passed: Certificate issued, valid for 12 months
  • Conditional Pass: Minor issues must be resolved within 30 days
  • Failed: Implementation does not meet requirements, must reapply

8.3 Compliance Standards

8.3.1 GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation (EU)

WIA-AIR-SHIELD implementations must comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Privacy by Design: Privacy built into the architecture
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary data
  • User Rights: Access, portability, erasure
  • Consent: Explicit, informed, withdrawable
  • Security: State-of-the-art protection
  • Breach Notification: Within 72 hours

8.3.2 CCPA Compliance

California Consumer Privacy Act (US)

Requirements for CCPA compliance:

  • Right to Know: Disclose data collection practices
  • Right to Delete: Delete personal information on request
  • Right to Opt-Out: Of data sale (N/A - we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

8.4 Certification Maintenance

8.4.1 Annual Renewal

Certifications are valid for 12 months and must be renewed annually:

  1. Renewal Application: Submit 60 days before expiration
  2. Updated Documentation: Describe any changes since last certification
  3. Re-testing: Run certification test suite on current version
  4. Audit: Undergo appropriate audit for certification level
  5. Fee Payment: Pay annual certification fee
