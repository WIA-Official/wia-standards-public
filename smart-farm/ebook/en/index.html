WIA Smart Farm Standard

Complete Implementation Guide

About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the WIA Smart Farm Standard (WIA-AGRI-001). This standard defines the global framework for intelligent agricultural automation, IoT sensor networks, precision farming, and sustainable food production.


This guide covers everything from basic concepts to advanced implementation, including real-world examples, best practices, and certification requirements.


Who is this for? Farmers, agricultural engineers, IoT developers, agtech entrepreneurs, policy makers, and anyone interested in the future of sustainable agriculture.
