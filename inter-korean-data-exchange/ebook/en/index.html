About This eBook

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-UNI-001 standard for inter-Korean data exchange, a groundbreaking framework designed to facilitate secure, transparent, and humanitarian communication between North and South Korea.

Written with hope and respect for the Korean people, this standard aims to bridge decades of separation through technology, enabling family reunification, cultural exchange, and ultimately contributing to peaceful reunification.

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit all humanity" - guides every aspect of this standard, ensuring that technology serves the greater good of peace and human dignity.
