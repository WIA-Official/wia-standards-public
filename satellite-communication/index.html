Satellite Communication

위성 통신
WIA-SPACE-003

弘益人間 (홍익인간)

Benefit All Humanity · 널리 인간을 이롭게 하라

Standard Overview

  • Global Connectivity: Communication infrastructure spanning the entire planet
  • Multiple Orbits: GEO, MEO, and LEO satellite systems for diverse applications
  • Frequency Bands: C, Ku, Ka-band spectrum allocation and management
  • Ground Infrastructure: VSAT terminals, gateways, and antenna systems
  • Link Analysis: Uplink/downlink budgets, noise, and propagation effects
  • Multiple Access: FDMA, TDMA, CDMA techniques for efficient spectrum use
  • Broadband Services: Next-generation satellite internet (Starlink, OneWeb)
  • Future Technologies: Optical and quantum satellite communications
