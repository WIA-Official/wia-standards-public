Certification Pathways

Organizations can pursue certification through multiple pathways depending on their maturity and resources:

Fast Track (6-9 months): For organizations with existing implementations seeking validation. Includes gap analysis, remediation, and testing phases.

Standard Track (9-12 months): For organizations building new implementations. Includes full development lifecycle support, architecture review, and iterative testing.

Custom Track (12-18 months): For complex, multi-system implementations requiring extensive integration work and custom protocol development.
