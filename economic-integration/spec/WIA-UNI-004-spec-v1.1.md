# WIA-UNI-004: Economic Integration Standard v1.1

**Status:** Official Standard
**Version:** 1.1.0
**Date:** December 25, 2025
**Authors:** WIA Standards Committee

---

## Enhancements in v1.1

This version adds the following features to v1.0:

- Advanced trade analytics and reporting
- Digital payment integration
- Expanded SEZ management capabilities
- Enhanced compliance automation
- Multi-party joint venture support

---

## New Features

### 1. Trade Analytics Dashboard

Real-time analytics for trade flows, trends, and performance metrics.

### 2. Digital Payment Integration

- Blockchain-based settlement options
- Digital currency support (when available)
- Instant cross-border payments for approved transactions

### 3. SEZ Management Platform

- Centralized platform for multiple SEZ management
- Tenant services and utilities management
- Infrastructure monitoring and maintenance

### 4. Compliance Automation

- AI-powered sanctions screening
- Automated risk scoring
- Real-time regulatory updates

### 5. Multi-Party Joint Ventures

Support for joint ventures with more than two parties, including international partners.
