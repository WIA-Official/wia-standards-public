# WIA-UNI-004: Economic Integration Standard v1.2

**Status:** Official Standard
**Version:** 1.2.0
**Date:** December 25, 2025
**Authors:** WIA Standards Committee

---

## Enhancements in v1.2

Building on v1.0 and v1.1, this version adds:

- Advanced infrastructure coordination
- Regional integration framework
- Sustainability and ESG metrics
- Labor mobility protocols
- Technology transfer standards

---

## New Features

### 1. Infrastructure Coordination

- Cross-border railway management
- Energy grid integration protocols
- Telecommunications network standards
- Port and logistics coordination

### 2. Regional Integration

- Northeast Asian economic community framework
- Multi-lateral cooperation protocols
- Transit trade facilitation

### 3. ESG Integration

- Environmental impact assessment standards
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Social responsibility metrics
- Governance transparency requirements

### 4. Labor Mobility

- Cross-border work permit systems
- Skills certification and recognition
- Worker protection standards
- Training and capacity building

### 5. Technology Transfer

- IP protection frameworks
- Technology licensing standards
- Know-how transfer protocols
- Innovation collaboration
