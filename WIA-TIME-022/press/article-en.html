Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-TIME-022, an international standard for emergency retrieval protocols for stranded time travelers, as free and open source under MIT License. This standard includes sub-5-minute emergency response, distress signal systems, search and rescue procedures, medical emergency care, and timeline crisis response, positioning the Republic of Korea as a leader in time travel emergency rescue technology.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Every year, hundreds of time travelers become stranded somewhere in spacetime. They drift due to energy depletion, become unable to return due to equipment failure, and without rescue within hours during medical emergencies, lives are at risk. Without standardized emergency rescue systems, time travel is no different from Russian roulette.

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum, CEO:
"A traveler who ran out of energy in 1969, a researcher injured in the medieval era, an explorer who fell ill in the future. Wherever they are, whenever they became stranded, we have a duty to find and rescue them. This isn't a technology issue—it's a matter of humanity."

WIA's Solution: WIA-TIME-022 Emergency Retrieval

WIA-TIME-022 standardizes automatic emergency detection systems, multi-frequency distress signals, emergency response team deployment within 5 minutes, optimized search patterns, temporal-specific medical emergency care, and paradox-preventing rescue protocols. The goal is response within 5 minutes for life-threatening situations and within 30 minutes for serious danger.
