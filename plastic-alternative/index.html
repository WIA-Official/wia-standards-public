WIA-ENE-048: Plastic Alternative 🍃

Sustainable Material Standards for a Plastic-Free Future

About WIA-ENE-048

WIA-ENE-048 defines comprehensive standards for plastic alternative materials, including bioplastics (PLA, PHA), cellulose-based materials, seaweed packaging, and mushroom-based alternatives. Our mission is to accelerate the transition to sustainable materials that are biodegradable, renewable, and compatible with existing manufacturing systems.
