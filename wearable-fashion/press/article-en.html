WIA Wearable Fashion International Standard Released for Free

Standardizing Smart Clothing and LED Fashion Technology

👔

Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released international standard WIA-IND-003 covering LED systems (200 units), battery life (5-10 hours), IP67 waterproof, thermal management (<45°C), energy harvesting, under MIT license for free.

🚨 Current Situation

$50B smart clothing market by 2030, but lacks washability, battery life, and safety standards.

Sam-Heum Yeon, CEO:
"Fusion of fashion and technology enables both self-expression and health monitoring."

WIA's Solution

LED systems (200 units), battery life (5-10 hours), IP67 waterproof, thermal management (<45°C), energy harvesting

Sam-Heum Yeon, CEO:
"LED jackets with 200 units run 8 hours, achieving machine-washable IP67 rating."
