Communication is a fundamental human right. The WIA Sign Language Recognition Standard ensures that sign language technology works seamlessly across all platforms and languages, empowering the deaf community worldwide and breaking down communication barriers.

About This Ebook

This official ebook provides a comprehensive guide to the WIA Sign Language Recognition Standard. It covers the complete specification across all four phases, from data formats to real-time translation systems with AI/ML integration.

Prerequisites

To get the most out of this ebook, readers should have:

  • Basic understanding of computer vision and video processing
  • Familiarity with AI/ML concepts (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
  • Knowledge of REST APIs and web protocols
  • Programming experience (Python, JavaScript, or similar)
  • Understanding of JSON data formats

Note: No prior knowledge of sign languages is required—we explain all concepts from first principles.

Supporting the Deaf Community

The WIA Sign Language Recognition Standard is developed with direct input from the deaf community. Our mission is to:

  • Break down communication barriers
  • Ensure accurate representation of all sign languages
  • Respect linguistic and cultural diversity
  • Provide free, open standards for all
  • Enable equal access to technology
