Autonomous delivery robots represent the future of urban logistics, reducing costs, emissions, and delivery times while creating safer, more efficient cities. The WIA ROB-010 Standard ensures interoperability, safety, and ethical deployment of delivery robots worldwide.

About This Ebook

This official ebook provides a comprehensive guide to the WIA ROB-010 Delivery Robot Standard. It covers the complete specification across all four phases, from data formats to fleet integration for autonomous last-mile delivery applications in urban environments.
