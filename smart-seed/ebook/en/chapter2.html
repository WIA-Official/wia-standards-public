CHAPTER 2

🔐 Seed Variety Registration & IP Protection

Learn how to register new seed varieties, protect intellectual property rights, integrate with UPOV PLUTO database, and use blockchain for tamper-proof breeding pedigrees.

2.1 Plant Variety Protection (PVP)

Plant variety protection grants breeders exclusive rights to their new varieties for 20-25 years. The WIA Smart Seed Standard integrates with UPOV (International Union for the Protection of New Varieties of Plants) to streamline registration and enforcement.

DUS Requirements

  • Distinctness: Clearly distinguishable from existing varieties
  • Uniformity: Sufficiently uniform in essential characteristics
  • Stability: Characteristics remain unchanged through reproduction

2.3 Blockchain-Based IP Protection

All variety registrations are recorded on blockchain with cryptographic proof, preventing backdating and ensuring global recognition of breeder rights.

Smart Contract Benefits: Automatic royalty distribution, licensing tracking, and infringement detection through on-chain variety fingerprinting.

2.4 Integration with UPOV PLUTO

The WIA system automatically submits variety data to the UPOV PLUTO database, reducing paperwork and ensuring global IP protection compliance.

2.5 Breeding Pedigree Management

Track parent lines, crossing dates, and selection history with immutable blockchain records. Essential for proving novelty and maintaining genetic diversity.

Chapter Summary

  • Plant variety protection grants breeders 20-25 years of exclusive rights
  • DUS criteria (Distinctness, Uniformity, Stability) are required for registration
  • WIA integrates with UPOV PLUTO for global IP protection
