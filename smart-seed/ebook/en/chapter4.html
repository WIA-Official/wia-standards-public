CHAPTER 4

✅ Seed Certification & OECD Integration

Master seed certification workflows, OECD Seed Schemes compliance, field inspection protocols, and blockchain-backed certificate issuance.

4.1 Seed Certification Classes

  • Breeder Seed: Original seed stock maintained by breeder
  • Foundation Seed: First generation from breeder seed
  • Registered Seed: Progeny of foundation seed
  • Certified Seed: Progeny of registered seed, sold to farmers

4.2 OECD Seed Schemes

WIA-AGRI-011 integrates with OECD Seed Schemes covering cereals, maize, vegetables, and forage crops. Automated compliance checking ensures seed lots meet international standards before certification.

4.3 Field Inspection Requirements

Field inspections verify variety purity, isolation distances, and disease incidence. WIA captures inspection data including GPS coordinates, photos, and inspector signatures.

4.4 Blockchain Certificates

All certificates are issued as blockchain transactions, providing:

  • Tamper-proof certification records
  • Instant global verification
  • Automatic expiry tracking
  • Revocation capabilities for non-compliance

Chapter Summary

  • Four seed certification classes: Breeder, Foundation, Registered, Certified
  • OECD Seed Schemes ensure international trade compatibility
  • Field inspections verify variety purity and isolation distances
  • Blockchain certificates provide tamper-proof global verification
