CHAPTER 7

🏛️ Seed Bank Integration & Global Networks

Connect with Svalbard Global Seed Vault, KOPIA, IRRI, CIMMYT, and international germplasm repositories for genetic diversity conservation.

7.1 Svalbard Global Seed Vault

The "Doomsday Vault" in Norway stores backup copies of 1+ million seed samples. WIA enables automated deposit requests with complete variety documentation and blockchain-verified authenticity.

7.2 KOPIA (Korean Plant Genetic Resource Center)

WIA integrates with Korea's national germplasm repository, enabling:

  • Automated accession number assignment
  • Characterization data submission
  • Material transfer agreement (MTA) tracking
  • Genetic diversity database updates

7.3 IRRI (International Rice Research Institute)

For rice varieties, WIA connects to IRRI's genebank containing 130,000+ rice accessions, facilitating:

  • Pedigree data exchange
  • Breeding program collaboration
  • Climate adaptation research

7.4 CGIAR Data Standards

WIA supports MCPD (Multi-Crop Passport Descriptors) format used by CGIAR research centers worldwide for standardized germplasm data exchange.

Chapter Summary

  • Svalbard Vault provides global seed backup for catastrophic events
