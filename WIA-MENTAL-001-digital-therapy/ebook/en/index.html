Welcome to Digital Therapy

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-MENTAL-001 Digital Therapy Standard, a revolutionary framework for delivering evidence-based mental health interventions through digital platforms. Across eight detailed chapters, you'll discover how modern technology is transforming mental healthcare delivery while maintaining clinical efficacy and patient safety.

This standard embodies the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit All Humanity" - by making quality mental healthcare accessible to all, regardless of geographic or economic barriers.
