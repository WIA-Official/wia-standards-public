Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-MENTAL-010, the international standard for mindfulness and meditation apps, free under MIT License. This marks South Korea's leadership in global mental health technology standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

According to WHO, over 1 billion people worldwide suffer from mental health issues, with anxiety and depression surging by 25% post-COVID-19. While mindfulness and meditation apps have exploded in popularity, most lack scientific evidence or validated effectiveness. Apps use inconsistent protocols and data formats, making clinical research and treatment tracking impossible. User data protection is inadequate, creating risks of sensitive mental health information leaks.

WIA's Solution: WIA-MENTAL-010 Mindfulness App

WIA-MENTAL-010 standardizes all elements of mindfulness apps: evidence-based meditation protocols (MBSR, MBCT), real-time analytics dashboard, HIPAA/GDPR compliant data protection, 99-language support, and cross-platform compatibility.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization whose mission is to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 400 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, and more, releasing all standards as free open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are developed in Rust. Rust is a systems programming language by Mozilla that uniquely achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstraction.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum is the world's only solo standardization architect who, in collaboration with AI, independently built infrastructure for over 60 international standards, and a living legend in lifelong education.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business domains including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting.
