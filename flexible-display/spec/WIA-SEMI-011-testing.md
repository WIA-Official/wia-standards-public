# WIA-SEMI-011: Testing Procedures

## Mechanical Testing

### Fold Cycle Endurance Test
**Equipment:**
- Automated folding machine
- Environmental chamber
- Force/torque sensors
- High-speed camera

**Procedure:**
1. Sample preparation (5 minimum)
2. Baseline measurement
3. Cycling (200,000 cycles minimum)
4. Intermediate inspections (every 50,000 cycles)
5. Final evaluation

**Pass Criteria:**
- Complete all cycles without failure
- Brightness degradation <10%
- Uniformity >80%
- No visible defects

### Bend Radius Compliance
**Method:**
1. Static radius measurement
2. Dynamic radius measurement
3. Force overload test

### Crease Measurement
**Equipment:**
- Laser profilometer
- Confocal microscope
- Spectrophotometer

**Limits:**
- <0.5mm after 200K cycles (standard)
- <0.3mm after 200K cycles (premium)

## Optical Testing

### Brightness and Uniformity
- 9-point measurement (VESA FPDM2)
- Uniformity ≥85%
- After cycling verification

### Color Accuracy
- ΔE <2 (excellent)
- ΔE <5 (acceptable)
- Viewing angle testing

## Environmental Testing

### Temperature Extremes
- Cold: -20°C for 4 hours
- Hot: +60°C for 4 hours
- Thermal shock: 30 cycles

### Humidity Testing
- 85°C/85% RH for 1,000 hours
- Water resistance (IPX7/IPX8)

## Drop Testing
- 26 drops from 1.5m
- 6 faces, 12 edges, 8 corners
