# WIA-SEMI-011: Certification Guide

## Certification Process

### Step 1: Application
- Submit product details
- Technical documentation
- Manufacturing information

### Step 2: Sample Submission
- Minimum 10 units
- Production representative
- Random selection

### Step 3: Testing
- Full test suite (2-4 months)
- Mechanical endurance
- Optical/electrical/environmental
- Test report generation

### Step 4: Evaluation
- Results review
- Compliance verification
- Additional testing if needed

### Step 5: Certification Decision
**Pass:** Certificate issued
**Fail:** Non-compliance report
**Conditional:** Corrective action required

### Step 6: Surveillance
- Annual re-testing
- Market surveillance
- 3-year validity

## Certification Tiers

### Standard Tier
- 200K cycles minimum
- Baseline optical performance
- Cost-effective certification

### Premium Tier
- 300K cycles
- Enhanced optical specs
- Premium market positioning

### Ultra Tier
- 500K cycles
- Exceptional performance
- Highest quality tier

## Certification Mark

**Logo Usage:**
- Product packaging
- Marketing materials
- Certification number inclusion

**Benefits:**
- Consumer confidence
- Market differentiation
- Quality assurance

## Laboratory Accreditation

**Requirements:**
- ISO/IEC 17025 accredited
- WIA-approved facilities
- Calibrated equipment
- Trained personnel

**Approved Labs:**
- WIA Seoul Laboratory
- WIA Shenzhen Laboratory
- Partner facilities worldwide

## Fees and Timeline

**Application Fee:** $5,000
**Testing Fee:** $15,000-30,000
**Annual Surveillance:** $3,000
**Timeline:** 3-5 months

## Compliance Maintenance

**Requirements:**
- Quality system documentation
- Traceability records
- Change notification
- Continued compliance

**Violations:**
- Warning (first offense)
- Suspension (repeated)
- Revocation (serious)
