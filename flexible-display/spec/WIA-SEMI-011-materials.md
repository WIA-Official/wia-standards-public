# WIA-SEMI-011: Materials Specification

## Substrate Materials

### Ultra-Thin Glass (UTG)
**Properties:**
- Thickness: 25-50 μm
- Composition: Alkali-free aluminosilicate
- Young's Modulus: 70-80 GPa
- Minimum bend radius: 1.5-3mm
- Surface hardness: 6-7 Mohs

**Manufacturing:**
- Float glass process
- Ion exchange strengthening
- Chemical thinning
- Edge polishing

### Polyimide (PI)
**Properties:**
- Thickness: 20-100 μm
- Tg: 250-400°C
- Young's Modulus: 2.5-8 GPa
- CTE: 3-40 ppm/°C

**Types:**
- Standard PI (brown/amber)
- Colorless PI (CPI)
- Black PI (light shielding)
- Patternable PI

## Barrier Layers

### Requirements
- WVTR: <10⁻⁶ g/m²/day
- OTR: <10⁻⁵ cm³/m²/day
- Flexibility: bend with substrate
- Optical: >90% transparency

### Structure
Multi-layer [Inorganic/Organic/Inorganic/Organic/Inorganic]

**Inorganic (50-200nm each):**
- SiNₓ, SiOₓ, Al₂O₃

**Organic (1-5μm each):**
- Polyacrylates
- Epoxy resins

## OLED Materials

### Emissive Layer
- RGB side-by-side
- White + color filter
- Tandem structure
- QD-OLED

### Electrodes
- Anode: ITO or silver (100-200nm)
- Cathode: Al or Ag (100nm)

## Adhesives

### Requirements
- Low modulus (1-10 MPa)
- Temperature stability
- Optical clarity
- Reworkability

### Types
- Silicone-based
- Acrylic PSA
- Optical clear adhesive (OCA)
