Standard Introduction

The WIA Aircraft Component Standard (WIA-SPACE-016) provides a comprehensive framework for the design, manufacturing, testing, and certification of aircraft components. This standard covers all major aircraft components from structural elements like fuselage, wings, and tail to landing gear systems, flight controls, hydraulic systems, and avionics.

Topics include advanced aerospace materials (aluminum alloys, composites, titanium), AS9100 quality management, NADCAP special process certification, FAA/EASA regulatory compliance, preventive maintenance, and lifecycle management - encompassing all aspects of the aircraft component industry.
