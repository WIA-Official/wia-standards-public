3.1 Certification Overview

The CRYO-FACILITY certification process evaluates facilities against comprehensive standards for safety, reliability, and operational excellence. Certification provides assurance to preserved individuals, regulators, and the public that a facility meets rigorous requirements.

Certification Levels

CERTIFICATION LEVEL COMPARISON:
+----------+-------------+-------------+-------------+
| Aspect   | Level 1-2   | Level 3     | Level 4     |
+----------+-------------+-------------+-------------+
| Focus    | Safety      | Reliability | Excellence  |
| Scope    | Core        | Enhanced    | Comprehensive|
| Duration | 2 years     | 3 years     | 5 years     |
| Renewal  | Full review | Streamlined | Continuous  |
+----------+-------------+-------------+-------------+

Certification Status Types

  • [ID:PENDING] Application submitted, under review
  • [ID:PROVISIONAL] Conditional certification with requirements
  • [ID:CERTIFIED] Full certification achieved
  • [ID:SUSPENDED] Temporarily suspended for non-compliance
  • [ID:REVOKED] Certification withdrawn
  • [ID:EXPIRED] Certification period ended without renewal

3.3 Evaluation Criteria

Scoring Categories

Certification scoring evaluates eight key areas:

SCORING WEIGHTS:
+------------------+--------+----------------------------------+
| Category         | Weight | Focus Areas                      |
+------------------+--------+----------------------------------+
| Infrastructure   | 20%    | Building, location, capacity     |
| Safety           | 20%    | Hazard control, emergency prep   |
| Redundancy       | 15%    | Backup systems, fail-safes       |
| Personnel        | 10%    | Staffing, training, competency   |
| Financial        | 15%    | Stability, reserves, insurance   |
| Documentation    | 5%     | Records, procedures, compliance  |
| Emergency        | 10%    | Response plans, drills, contacts |
| Patient Care     | 5%     | Service quality, communication   |
+------------------+--------+----------------------------------+

Minimum Score Requirements

LEVEL THRESHOLDS:
+--------+------------------+--------------------+
| Level  | Overall Minimum  | Category Minimum   |
+--------+------------------+--------------------+
| 1      | 60%              | 50% in each        |
| 2      | 70%              | 60% in each        |
| 3      | 80%              | 70% in each        |
| 4      | 90%              | 80% in each        |
+--------+------------------+--------------------+

Critical Requirements

[!] Certain requirements are mandatory regardless of score:

  • [!] Valid facility permits and licenses
  • [!] Required insurance coverage in force
  • [!] No unresolved safety violations
  • [!] Minimum staffing levels met
  • [!] Emergency plan documented and tested

3.5 Scoring Methodology

Evidence-Based Evaluation

Each criterion is evaluated based on documented evidence:

EVIDENCE TYPES:
+------------------+----------------------------------+
| Evidence Type    | Examples                         |
+------------------+----------------------------------+
| Documentation    | Policies, procedures, records    |
| Physical         | Equipment, facilities, systems   |
| Observation      | Operations, staff performance    |
| Testing          | Equipment function, drills       |
| Interview        | Staff knowledge, competency      |
+------------------+----------------------------------+

Scoring Scale

CRITERION SCORING:
+-------+-------------+----------------------------------+
| Score | Rating      | Description                      |
+-------+-------------+----------------------------------+
| 0     | Not Met     | Requirement not addressed        |
| 1     | Partial     | Partially addressed, gaps exist  |
| 2     | Meets       | Fully meets requirement          |
| 3     | Exceeds     | Exceeds requirement              |
+-------+-------------+----------------------------------+

Score Calculation

CALCULATION FORMULA:

Category Score = (Sum of criterion scores / Maximum possible) x 100

Overall Score = Sum of (Category Score x Category Weight)

Example:
- Infrastructure: 85% x 0.20 = 17.0
- Safety: 90% x 0.20 = 18.0
- Redundancy: 80% x 0.15 = 12.0
- Personnel: 75% x 0.10 = 7.5
- Financial: 85% x 0.15 = 12.75
- Documentation: 70% x 0.05 = 3.5
- Emergency: 80% x 0.10 = 8.0
- Patient Care: 75% x 0.05 = 3.75
----------------------------------------
Overall Score: 82.5% (qualifies for Level 3)

3.6 Certification Decisions

Decision Outcomes

DECISION TYPES:
+------------------+----------------------------------+
| Decision         | Description                      |
+------------------+----------------------------------+
| Certified        | Full certification at level      |
| Provisional      | Conditional with improvement plan|
| Deferred         | Additional information required  |
| Denied           | Does not meet requirements       |
+------------------+----------------------------------+

Provisional Certification

Provisional certification may be granted when:

  • [i] Minor non-conformances exist that can be corrected
  • [i] Facility demonstrates commitment to improvement
  • [i] No critical safety issues are present
  • [i] Corrective action plan is accepted

Provisional certification terms:

  • [!] Maximum duration: 12 months
  • [!] Progress reporting required quarterly
  • [!] Follow-up inspection before conversion
  • [!] Reverts to denied if requirements not met

  • [OK] No major non-conformances in current period

Streamlined renewal benefits:

3.9 Level Advancement

Upgrade Requirements

Facilities may apply for level advancement when:

  • [i] Current certification maintained for minimum period
  • [i] All requirements for higher level are met
  • [i] Self-assessment indicates readiness
  • [i] Upgrade fee submitted with application

Minimum Time at Level

LEVEL ADVANCEMENT TIMELINE:
+---------------+----------------------------------+
| From - To     | Minimum Time at Current Level    |
+---------------+----------------------------------+
| Level 1 to 2  | 1 year                           |
| Level 2 to 3  | 2 years                          |
| Level 3 to 4  | 3 years                          |
+---------------+----------------------------------+

Upgrade Inspection

Level advancement requires:

  • [OK] Full inspection against new level requirements
  • [OK] Demonstration of enhanced capabilities
  • [OK] Verification of upgraded systems
  • [OK] Staff competency for new level requirements

Chapter Summary

The certification process provides a structured pathway for facilities to demonstrate compliance with CRYO-FACILITY standards. Through rigorous evaluation, ongoing surveillance, and continuous improvement requirements, certification ensures facilities maintain the highest standards of preservation care.

Understanding the certification process enables facilities to prepare effectively, achieve certification at the appropriate level, and maintain compliance throughout the certification period.
