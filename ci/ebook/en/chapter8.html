ISO 14971: Risk Management

Risk Management Process:

1. Risk Analysis
   ├── Identify hazards
   ├── Estimate risk for each hazard
   └── Evaluate risk acceptability

2. Risk Control
   ├── Risk control option analysis
   ├── Implementation of risk controls
   ├── Residual risk evaluation
   └── Risk/benefit analysis

3. Risk Evaluation
   ├── Evaluate overall residual risk
   └── Risk management review

Example Hazards:
├── Electrical shock → Control: Current limiting
├── Excessive stimulation → Control: Compliance monitoring
├── Infection → Control: Sealed enclosure
├── Data breach → Control: Encryption, access control
└── Firmware corruption → Control: Signed updates

8.7 Clinical Evaluation

Clinical Study Design

Study Parameters:

Population:
- Adults (18-75 years) with severe-to-profound hearing loss
- Cochlear implant candidates or existing users
- N = 50 participants

Primary Endpoint:
- Octave discrimination accuracy
- Target: >80% correct identification

Secondary Endpoints:
- Music appreciation scores
- Speech understanding (CNC words)
- Quality of life (NCIQ questionnaire)
- User satisfaction

Duration:
- 6 months follow-up
- Baseline, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months

Metrics:
- Octave confusion matrix
- Melody recognition test
- Timbre identification
- Pitch ranking

Performance Benchmarks

Metric Standard CI WIA-CI Target Clinical Significance
Octave Discrimination ~50% >85% p < 0.001
Melody Recognition ~30% >60% p < 0.001
Music Enjoyment (1-10) 4.2 >7.0 p < 0.01
Speech in Noise (dB SNR) +10 dB +8 dB Non-inferior

8.9 Certification Roadmap

Phase 1: Development (Months 1-6)

  • Design and implement WIA-CI standard
  • Internal testing and validation
  • Documentation preparation
  • Risk management (ISO 14971)

Phase 2: Verification (Months 7-12)

  • IEC 60601-1 electrical safety testing
  • IEC 60601-1-2 EMC testing
  • Biocompatibility testing (ISO 10993)
  • Software validation (IEC 62304)
  • Wireless certification (FCC, CE, KC)

Phase 3: Clinical Evaluation (Months 13-18)

  • IRB/Ethics committee approval
  • Clinical study execution
  • Data analysis and reporting
  • Clinical evaluation report

Phase 4: Regulatory Submission (Months 19-24)

  • FDA 510(k) submission (USA)
  • CE marking technical file (Europe)
  • MFDS approval (Korea)
  • Additional markets as needed
