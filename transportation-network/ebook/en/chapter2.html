CHAPTER 2

Current Challenges and Barriers

2.1 Technical Challenges

Inter-Korean transportation integration faces several technical obstacles that WIA-UNI-008 addresses through standardization:

Infrastructure Compatibility

  • Track Gauge: Both Koreas use standard gauge (1435mm), but connections require standardization of signaling and electrification
  • Railway Signaling: Different signaling systems require unified ERTMS/ETCS implementation
  • Power Systems: Harmonization of electrical grid standards for electrified railways

2.2 Regulatory and Political Barriers

Beyond technical issues, regulatory harmonization presents challenges:

  • Customs and immigration procedures
  • Safety and security standards
  • Cross-border payment systems
  • Liability and insurance frameworks

2.3 Data and Information Exchange

Lack of standardized data formats has hindered integration efforts. WIA-UNI-008 Phase 1 addresses this with JSON-LD schemas for semantic interoperability.

2.4 International Coordination

Connecting to Trans-Siberian Railway, Chinese rail networks, and ASEAN corridors requires alignment with international standards:

  • UIC (International Union of Railways) standards
  • ICAO (Aviation) and IMO (Maritime) compliance
  • WCO (World Customs Organization) frameworks
  • ISO standards for supply chain and logistics
Solution: WIA-UNI-008 provides a comprehensive framework that bridges these gaps through standardized data formats, APIs, protocols, and integration strategies.
