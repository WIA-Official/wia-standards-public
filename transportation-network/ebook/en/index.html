WIA-UNI-008

Transportation Network Standard - Complete Guide

Welcome to the Complete Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of the WIA-UNI-008 Transportation Network Standard, from foundational concepts to advanced implementation strategies for inter-Korean transportation integration.

Chapters: 8 comprehensive modules covering data formats, APIs, protocols, and international integration.

CHAPTER 1
Introduction

Overview of inter-Korean transportation integration, historical context, and the strategic importance of unified transportation networks.
CHAPTER 2
Current Challenges

Technical, political, and logistical barriers to transportation integration and strategies to overcome them.
CHAPTER 3
Standard Overview

Complete architecture of WIA-UNI-008, design principles, and four-phase implementation approach.
CHAPTER 4
Phase 1: Data Format

JSON-LD schemas for routes, bookings, cargo tracking, and vehicle registration.
CHAPTER 5
Phase 2: API Interface

RESTful APIs for booking systems, real-time tracking, and logistics management.
CHAPTER 6
Phase 3: Protocol

Railway signaling (ERTMS), traffic control (ITS), aviation (ICAO), and maritime (AIS) protocols.
CHAPTER 7
Phase 4: Integration

Trans-Siberian Railway connection, international standards, and regional cooperation frameworks.
CHAPTER 8
Implementation

Practical deployment guidance, certification process, and best practices for adopters.
