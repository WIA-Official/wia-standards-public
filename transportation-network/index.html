Key Routes & Use Cases

🚄 Trans-Korean Railway (TKR)

Connecting Busan-Seoul-Pyongyang-Sinuiju-China-Russia with high-speed rail integration. Enables 13-day rail freight from Busan to Rotterdam, reducing shipping time by 50% compared to sea routes and connecting Korea to the Eurasian railway network.

🛣️ Gyeongui & Donghae Highway Corridors

Western and Eastern highway connections enabling road freight transport across the DMZ with intelligent toll systems, unified traffic management, and 24/7 cargo logistics operations supporting economic integration.

✈️ Joint Airspace Management

Coordinated air traffic control across Korean Peninsula airspace, enabling direct Seoul-Pyongyang flights, international route optimization, and unified aviation safety standards compliant with ICAO requirements.

🚢 West Sea Shipping Route

Inter-Korean coastal shipping corridor connecting Incheon, Nampo, and other west coast ports with standardized cargo handling, customs procedures, and vessel traffic management systems.

📦 Unified Logistics Platform

Digital logistics ecosystem integrating rail, road, air, and sea freight with real-time tracking, automated customs clearance, and predictive supply chain management across all transportation modes.

🚇 Seoul-Kaesong Commuter Rail

Daily commuter railway service between Seoul metropolitan area and Kaesong Industrial Complex with integrated ticketing, security screening, and passenger information systems supporting cross-border workforce mobility.
