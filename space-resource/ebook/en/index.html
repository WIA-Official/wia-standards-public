The era of space resource mining has arrived.

Asteroids contain trillions of dollars worth of rare metals, the Moon harbors helium-3 fusion fuel, and Mars has the water and oxygen needed for human settlement. This ebook covers everything about space resource mining—technology, law, economics, and humanity's future.

From lunar mining to asteroid capture, ISRU (In-Situ Resource Utilization) technology, orbital logistics systems, and the future of the space economy, we provide systematic and practical knowledge.
