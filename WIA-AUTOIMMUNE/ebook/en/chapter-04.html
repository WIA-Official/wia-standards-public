Chapter 4: Disease-Specific Guidelines

While general principles apply across autoimmune conditions, each disease requires specific diagnostic and therapeutic considerations. This chapter provides condition-specific guidelines for major autoimmune diseases covered by WIA-AUTOIMMUNE.

4.1 Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)

RA Management Standards
========================
Diagnosis:
├── ACR/EULAR 2010 Classification Criteria
├── Anti-CCP and RF testing
├── Joint imaging (X-ray, ultrasound, MRI)
└── Synovial fluid analysis if monoarticular

Treatment Targets:
├── Remission (DAS28 <2.6 or CDAI ≤2.8)
├── Low disease activity acceptable
├── Prevent radiographic progression
└── Maintain physical function

Monitoring Schedule:
├── Disease activity every 1-3 months until target
├── Annual X-rays for progression
├── Cardiovascular risk assessment
└── Regular comorbidity screening

4.3 Multiple Sclerosis (MS)

MS Treatment Ladder
====================
Platform Therapies (First-line):
├── Interferon beta (Avonex, Rebif, Betaseron)
├── Glatiramer acetate (Copaxone)
├── Teriflunomide (Aubagio)
└── Dimethyl fumarate (Tecfidera)

High-Efficacy Therapies:
├── Natalizumab (Tysabri) - JCV monitoring
├── Fingolimod (Gilenya)
├── Ocrelizumab (Ocrevus)
├── Alemtuzumab (Lemtrada)
└── Ofatumumab (Kesimpta)

Acute Relapse: IV methylprednisolone 1g x 3-5 days

4.4 Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)

UC vs Crohn's Management

  • Ulcerative Colitis: 5-ASA (mesalamine) first line for mild-moderate; steroids, biologics for moderate-severe
  • Crohn's Disease: Earlier biologic use recommended; anti-TNF or vedolizumab; surgical consultation for strictures/fistulas
  • Monitoring: Endoscopic assessment, fecal calprotectin, colorectal cancer surveillance

4.6 Vasculitis

ANCA-Associated Vasculitis (AAV)
=================================
Induction:
├── GPA/MPA: Rituximab OR cyclophosphamide
├── Plus high-dose glucocorticoids
├── Plasma exchange for severe renal/pulmonary
└── Duration: 3-6 months

Maintenance:
├── Rituximab (preferred) OR azathioprine
├── Duration: 24-48 months minimum
├── Glucocorticoid taper
└── Monitor ANCA, relapse signs

Monitoring:
├── BVAS (Birmingham Vasculitis Activity Score)
├── Renal function, urinalysis
├── Chest imaging
└── Infection surveillance

4.7 Sjogren's Syndrome

Sjogren's Syndrome Management
==============================
Diagnosis (ACR/EULAR 2016):
├── Labial salivary gland biopsy (focus score ≥1)
├── Anti-SSA/Ro antibodies
├── Ocular staining score ≥5
├── Schirmer test ≤5mm/5min
└── Unstimulated salivary flow ≤0.1mL/min

Treatment by Manifestation:
├── Sicca (eyes): Artificial tears, punctal plugs, cyclosporine drops
├── Sicca (oral): Saliva substitutes, pilocarpine, cevimeline
├── Systemic: HCQ for fatigue/arthralgias
├── Severe organ: Steroids, MTX, rituximab
└── Lymphoma surveillance: Annual monitoring

Monitoring Requirements:
├── Ophthalmologic exam every 6-12 months
├── Dental evaluation every 6 months
├── Annual lymphoma screening (labs, exam)
├── Pulmonary function if respiratory symptoms
└── Renal function (RTA risk)

SpA Treatment Algorithm

Step 1: NSAIDs (at least 2 different, 2-4 weeks each)
        + Physical therapy (essential)

Step 2: If inadequate response
        → Peripheral arthritis: Sulfasalazine, MTX
        → Axial disease: Proceed to biologics

Step 3: bDMARD Selection
        ├── TNFi (first-line): All manifestations
        ├── IL-17i: Axial, peripheral, psoriasis (avoid in IBD)
        └── JAKi: Emerging option (upadacitinib)

4.9 Autoimmune Hepatitis

Autoimmune Hepatitis Standards
===============================
Diagnosis:
├── Elevated transaminases, IgG
├── Autoantibodies (ANA, SMA, LKM-1)
├── Histology (interface hepatitis)
├── Exclude viral hepatitis, drug-induced
└── Simplified diagnostic criteria (IAIHG)

Treatment Protocol:
├── Induction: Prednisone 40-60mg/day
├── Taper over 4-8 weeks to maintenance
├── Azathioprine added (1-2mg/kg/day)
├── Goal: Biochemical remission (normal ALT, IgG)
└── Maintenance: AZA monotherapy if possible

Monitoring Schedule:
├── LFTs weekly during induction
├── Monthly during taper
├── Every 3 months on maintenance
├── Annual elastography/biopsy for fibrosis
└── HCC surveillance if cirrhosis

Second-line Options:
├── Mycophenolate mofetil
├── Tacrolimus
├── Budesonide (non-cirrhotic)
└── Rituximab (refractory cases)
