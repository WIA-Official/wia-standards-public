💡 Why WIA-MED-008?

Current digital pathology market challenges:

  • ❌ Proprietary formats from each vendor
  • ❌ Lack of interoperability between systems
  • ❌ No AI model standardization
  • ❌ High implementation costs and complexity

WIA-MED-008 solves these problems:

  • ✅ Open standard for all system compatibility
  • ✅ Standardized AI model interfaces
  • ✅ Clear implementation guidelines
  • ✅ Free open-source license
