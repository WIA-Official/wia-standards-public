                        ABOUT THIS GUIDE
================================================================================

    This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the WIA standards for
    cryopreservation consent management. It covers all aspects of informed
    consent, from initial capacity assessment through revival decisions.

    [ID] sections explain core consent concepts.
    [OK] indicates completed or verified items.
    [!]  marks critical warnings or requirements.
    [i]  provides additional information.
