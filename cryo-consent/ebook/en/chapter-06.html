6.1 SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] REQUIRED SIGNATURES:

    +----------------------------------------------------------+
    | Signer              | Required | When                    |
    +----------------------------------------------------------+
    | Subject             | YES      | Before activation       |
    | Witness 1           | YES      | Same session as subject |
    | Witness 2           | YES      | Same session as subject |
    | Notary              | YES      | After all signatures    |
    | Spouse              | RECOMMENDED | Before activation    |
    | Legal Counsel       | OPTIONAL | Complex cases           |
    | Medical Advisor     | OPTIONAL | Health-related clauses  |
    +----------------------------------------------------------+

[ID] SIGNATURE RECORD:

    SignatureRecord {
      signerId:     "SIGNER-001"
      signerName:   "John William Smith"
      signerRole:   "SUBJECT"

      signatureType: "DIGITAL"  // WET | DIGITAL | BIOMETRIC
      signatureData: "[ENCRYPTED SIGNATURE DATA]"

      timestamp:     "2025-03-01T10:30:00Z"
      location:      "Seoul, South Korea"
      ipAddress:     "203.0.113.50"

      verificationStatus: "VERIFIED"
      verificationMethod: "PKI Certificate"
      verificationDate:   "2025-03-01T10:31:00Z"
    }

[ID] SIGNER ROLES:

    SUBJECT             -> The person being preserved
    SPOUSE              -> Subject's spouse
    CHILD               -> Subject's child (adult)
    GUARDIAN            -> Designated guardian
    WITNESS             -> Independent witness
    NOTARY              -> Licensed notary public
    LEGAL_COUNSEL       -> Attorney reviewing document
    MEDICAL_ADVISOR     -> Physician reviewing medical aspects
    FACILITY_REP        -> Preservation facility representative


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.2 WITNESS REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[!] WITNESS QUALIFICATIONS:

    Witnesses MUST be:
    - Over 18 years of age
    - Of sound mind
    - Not related to subject by blood or marriage
    - Not designated as guardians
    - Not employees of the preservation facility
    - Present during subject's signature

[ID] WITNESS CERTIFICATION:

    WitnessCertification {
      witnessId:      "WITNESS-001"
      witnessName:    "Robert Johnson"

      certification: """
        I, Robert Johnson, hereby certify that:
        1. I witnessed John William Smith sign this document
        2. The subject appeared to be of sound mind
        3. No coercion or undue influence was observed
        4. The subject acknowledged understanding the document
        5. I am not related to the subject
        6. I have no financial interest in this matter
      """

      signature:      "[SIGNATURE]"
      signedAt:       "2025-03-01T10:35:00Z"
      location:       "Seoul, South Korea"
    }

[ID] TWO-WITNESS RULE:

    Both witnesses must:
    - Be present simultaneously
    - Witness subject sign or acknowledge signature
    - Sign immediately after subject
    - Provide contact information for verification

6.3 NOTARIZATION PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] NOTARIZATION RECORD:

    NotarizationRecord {
      notaryId:           "NOTARY-KR-2025-001"
      notaryName:         "Park Soo-jin"
      notaryLicense:      "KR-NOTARY-12345"
      jurisdiction:       "South Korea"

      notarizationDate:   "2025-03-01T11:00:00Z"
      notarizationLocation: "Seoul Notary Office"
      sealNumber:         "SEAL-2025-0301-001"

      expirationDate:     "2030-03-01"
      verificationUrl:    "https://verify.notary.kr/SEAL-2025-0301-001"
    }

[ID] NOTARIZATION STEPS:

    1. IDENTITY VERIFICATION
       - Notary verifies subject identity
       - Government ID required
       - Photo comparison

    2. CAPACITY CONFIRMATION
       - Notary confirms subject appears competent
       - No signs of coercion observed
       - Subject acknowledges document

    3. WITNESS VERIFICATION
       - Notary confirms witness identities
       - Witnesses present during notarization

    4. DOCUMENT REVIEW
       - Notary confirms document is complete
       - All required signatures present
       - No alterations or irregularities

    5. NOTARIZATION
       - Notary affixes seal
       - Notary signs and dates
       - Certificate attached

[ID] INTERNATIONAL NOTARIZATION:

    For cross-jurisdictional validity:
    - Apostille certification (Hague Convention countries)
    - Embassy/consulate authentication (non-Hague)
    - Multiple jurisdiction notarization recommended

6.4 DIGITAL SIGNATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ID] DIGITAL SIGNATURE STANDARDS:

    Supported Standards:
    - PKCS #7 / CMS (Cryptographic Message Syntax)
    - PAdES (PDF Advanced Electronic Signatures)
    - XAdES (XML Advanced Electronic Signatures)
    - Blockchain-anchored signatures

[ID] DIGITAL SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS:

    DigitalSignatureRequirements {
      algorithm:        "RSA-2048" or "ECDSA-P256" or "Ed25519"
      hashFunction:     "SHA-256" or better
      timestampAuthority: "WIA Timestamp Service"

      certificateRequirements: {
        issuer:          "Qualified Certificate Authority"
        keyUsage:        ["digitalSignature", "nonRepudiation"]
        validityPeriod:  "Must be valid at signing time"
      }
    }

[ID] BIOMETRIC SIGNATURES:

    BiometricSignature {
      biometricType:  "FINGERPRINT"  // or "FACIAL" or "VOICE"
      captureDevice:  "WIA Certified Scanner Model X"
      template:       "[ENCRYPTED BIOMETRIC TEMPLATE]"
      livenessCheck:  true
      capturedAt:     "2025-03-01T10:30:00Z"
    }

[ID] SIGNATURE VERIFICATION:

    Verification Process:
    1. Certificate chain validation
    2. Signature cryptographic verification
    3. Timestamp verification
    4. Revocation status check
    5. Document integrity check

    VerificationResult {
      signatureValid:      true
      certificateValid:    true
      timestampValid:      true
      documentIntegrity:   true
      overallResult:       "VALID"
    }
