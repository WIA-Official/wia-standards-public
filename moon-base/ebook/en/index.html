📖 About This Book

The WIA Moon Base Standard is a comprehensive guide for establishing and operating permanent human settlements on the lunar surface. From the Artemis Program to long-term base operations, this standard covers every aspect including lunar environment analysis, site selection, construction technologies, resource utilization, life support systems, and the future lunar economy.

弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) - Based on this philosophy, the Moon Base is designed for the benefit of all humanity and the future of space exploration.
