WIA Military AI — Governance & Conformance Simulator

This interactive view walks operators through the WIA Military AI standard's governance surface only: weapons-review record-keeping, human-on-the-loop oversight signals, audit-log shape, and conformance-tier disclosure. The simulator does not model targeting, sensor fusion, or effectors. Its role is to help integrators verify that their compliance evidence trail satisfies the PHASE-2 audit contract before submission.

1. Configure a synthetic deployment record

Fill the fields below with values from your weapons-review and authorization documentation. The simulator will then emit the WIA-compliant record envelope, run the conformance gates, and show you which evidence elements are missing for Tier 2 / Tier 3 promotion.

2. WIA-compliant audit-log envelope

The envelope follows the PHASE-2 schema (RFC 9457 problem-details for error responses, RFC 7519 JWT identity claims, ISO/IEC 17065:2012 conformity-assessment evidence pointer). It is the artifact your Tier 2 / Tier 3 assessor will verify against the spec.

4. Out of scope (explicitly)

  • Targeting decisions, sensor fusion, kinetic effects, or effector activation logic.
  • Classified or weapon-specific tactical procedures — those remain with the operating authority.
  • Lethal-autonomy adjudication; the WIA standard requires operator oversight per the weapons review.
