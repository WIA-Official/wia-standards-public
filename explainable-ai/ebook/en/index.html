About This Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Explainable AI (XAI), the WIA-AI-009 standard. This book explores the critical field of making artificial intelligence systems transparent, interpretable, and trustworthy for humans.

As AI systems become increasingly complex and are deployed in high-stakes domains like healthcare, finance, and criminal justice, the ability to explain and understand their decisions becomes paramount. This standard provides the frameworks, methods, and best practices for implementing explainability across all AI systems.
