About WIA-BIO Standard

Purpose and Scope

WIA-BIO provides standardized protocols for biotechnology operations worldwide. This comprehensive framework covers everything from basic laboratory procedures to advanced genetic engineering techniques, ensuring consistent safety and quality across all implementations.

The biotechnology industry faces unique challenges that require specialized standards. WIA-BIO addresses these challenges by providing clear guidelines for research institutions, commercial enterprises, and regulatory bodies. Our framework integrates seamlessly with existing international regulations while providing enhanced safety and quality measures.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive laboratory standards covering BSL-1 through BSL-4 facilities
  • Detailed genetic engineering protocols including CRISPR-Cas9 guidelines
  • Risk assessment frameworks for biological materials handling
  • Quality control procedures aligned with GMP requirements
  • Ethical governance frameworks for biotechnology research
  • Implementation guides with step-by-step certification processes

WIA Philosophy

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit All Humanity"

The WIA-BIO standard embodies the principle that biotechnology advances should benefit all of humanity. By establishing rigorous safety and ethical standards, we ensure that biotechnology innovation proceeds responsibly while maximizing benefits for global health and welfare.

Getting started with WIA-BIO compliance requires understanding your organization's current capabilities and identifying gaps against our standard requirements.

   - Assess current infrastructure
   - Identify upgrade requirements

   - Create compliance roadmap

Step 4: Continuous Improvement
   - Monitor compliance metrics

Target Audience

This standard is designed for:

  • Research Institutions: Universities, government labs, and independent research facilities
  • Pharmaceutical Companies: Drug developers and manufacturers
  • Biotech Startups: Emerging companies in gene therapy, synthetic biology, and related fields
  • Regulatory Bodies: Government agencies overseeing biotechnology activities
  • Healthcare Providers: Hospitals and clinics using biotechnology products

Certification Process

Organizations seeking WIA-BIO certification must complete a structured assessment process demonstrating compliance with all applicable requirements. The certification is valid for three years with annual surveillance audits.

Certification Timeline
======================

Month 1-2:   Gap Analysis and Planning
Month 3-4:   Documentation Development
Month 5-6:   Implementation and Training
Month 7:     Internal Audit
Month 8:     External Certification Audit
Month 9:     Certification Decision
Month 10+:   Ongoing Compliance Maintenance
