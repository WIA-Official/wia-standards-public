About This Guide
================================================================================

This ebook provides comprehensive guidance on all aspects of smart pill
technology, including ingestible sensors, drug delivery systems, gastrointestinal
monitoring, wireless communication, and regulatory approval processes. It serves
both medical professionals and technology developers with practical information.



"弘益人間" - Benefit All Humanity.

Smart pill technology embodies this principle by enabling non-invasive health
monitoring and precision drug delivery, improving patient care, expanding
medical access, and contributing to the health and well-being of humanity
worldwide.
