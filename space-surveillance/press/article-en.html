WIA Announces Space Surveillance Standard - Protecting Earth's Orbit

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the Space Surveillance standard.

Space Debris Tracking

The Republic of Korea is emerging as a space technology powerhouse. This standard tracks satellites and space debris to prevent collisions.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "This standard goes beyond technological advancement to improve everyone's lives and create a sustainable future." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this technology will provide tangible benefits to people worldwide and contribute to human progress."

"WIA standards establish a foundation for technology to benefit humanity, enabling everyone to live better lives and build a sustainable future together."
