About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of healthcare data standards and interoperability as defined by the WIA-MEDICAL standard. Whether you're a health IT professional implementing EHR integrations, a developer building FHIR-based applications, a compliance officer ensuring HIPAA adherence, or a healthcare administrator planning digital transformation, this guide provides the technical depth and practical insights you need.

With 96% of hospitals now using certified health IT systems and 84% implementing FHIR APIs, understanding healthcare interoperability standards has never been more critical. The transition from legacy HL7 v2 to modern FHIR-based exchange is reshaping how health data flows between systems, enabling better patient care and clinical decision support.

The WIA-MEDICAL standard builds upon established frameworks like HL7 FHIR while providing additional guidance for global interoperability, ensuring healthcare systems worldwide can exchange information seamlessly and securely.
