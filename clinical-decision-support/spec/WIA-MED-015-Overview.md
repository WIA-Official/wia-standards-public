# WIA-MED-015: Clinical Decision Support Standards

## Overview

WIA-MED-015 defines international standards for Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) to ensure safety, effectiveness, and usability in healthcare environments.

## Scope

This standard covers:

1. **System Architecture** - Design principles and components
2. **Clinical Guidelines Integration** - Evidence-based medicine implementation
3. **Drug Safety** - DDI detection, allergy warnings, dose calculation
4. **Diagnostic Support** - Symptom-based suggestions and differential diagnosis
5. **Data Standards** - HL7 FHIR, SNOMED CT, LOINC, RxNorm
6. **Alert Management** - Fatigue prevention and optimization
7. **Security & Privacy** - HIPAA, GDPR compliance
8. **Performance Metrics** - Quality measures and monitoring

## Key Principles

- **Patient Safety First** - All decisions prioritize patient wellbeing
- **Evidence-Based** - Recommendations grounded in scientific evidence
- **Transparent** - Explainable decision logic
- **Fair** - Unbiased across all populations
- **Physician Support** - Assists, not replaces, clinical judgment

## Certification Levels

- 🥉 **Bronze** - Basic CDSS functionality
- 🥈 **Silver** - Advanced guideline integration
- 🥇 **Gold** - ML-based predictions and personalization
- 💎 **Platinum** - Multi-site validation with published evidence

## Standards Compliance

Systems must comply with:
- HL7 FHIR R4+
- CDS Hooks 2.0+
- SNOMED CT
- LOINC
- RxNorm
- HIPAA (US)
- GDPR (EU)
