[i] About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA LLM Interoperability Standard, a protocol enabling different AI models to communicate, collaborate, and form federations for solving complex problems.

Core Vision

As AI systems become more specialized, no single model can excel at everything. The WIA LLM Interoperability Standard enables AI models to work together, combining their unique strengths to deliver better outcomes for humanity.
