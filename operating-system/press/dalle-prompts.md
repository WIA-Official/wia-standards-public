## Usage Notes

- All images should be generated at high resolution (1792x1024 or similar)
- Consistent color scheme: Dark navy (#0f172a), Blue (#3B82F6), with strategic use of red (#EF4444), green (#10B981), and gold for security
- Style: Professional, technical, yet accessible and inspiring
- Avoid text in images (will be added in HTML articles)
- Emphasize layered architecture, security, orchestration, and system stability
