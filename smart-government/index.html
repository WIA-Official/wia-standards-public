WIA Smart Government Standard

WIA-SOC-006: AI-Based Administrative Services

The WIA Smart Government Standard defines a comprehensive framework for implementing AI-powered government services. This standard enables public institutions to leverage artificial intelligence, predictive analytics, and automation to deliver efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric administrative services. By establishing unified protocols for data exchange, security, and integration, WIA-SOC-006 facilitates the transformation of traditional government operations into intelligent, responsive, and accessible digital services that serve all citizens equally.
