Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the comprehensive international standard WIA-SOCIAL for Legal System Harmonization technology under MIT License for free. This announcement marks South Korea's leadership in next-generation technology standardization.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Currently, the Legal System Harmonization field suffers from severely limited interoperability due to disparate data formats and APIs from different manufacturers, forcing developers to learn dozens of SDKs. The absence of standardization seriously hinders both technology accessibility and innovation speed.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

Yeon Sam-heum, CEO:
"Legal System Harmonization technology should no longer be the privilege of a few corporations. Every developer and researcher worldwide has the right to leverage this innovation. This is not a technical issue but a matter of equal opportunity."

WIA's Solution: WIA-SOCIAL

WIA-SOCIAL standardizes all aspects of Legal System Harmonization, from data formats to APIs, protocols, and integration guidelines.
