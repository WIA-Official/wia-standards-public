Welcome to the WIA-FIN-005 Ebook

This comprehensive guide covers the complete WIA Central Bank Digital Currency Standard, from foundational concepts to advanced implementation strategies. Whether you're a central banker, fintech developer, or policy maker, this ebook provides the knowledge you need to understand and implement interoperable CBDCs.

Our Philosophy

弘益人間 (홍익인간) - "Benefit All Humanity"

The WIA-FIN-005 standard is built on the principle that digital currencies should enhance financial inclusion, reduce transaction costs, and improve monetary policy effectiveness while respecting individual privacy and national sovereignty. Every specification in this standard is designed to benefit all of humanity, not just privileged few.
