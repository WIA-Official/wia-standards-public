GDPR Compliance

Comprehensive Data Protection and Privacy Framework

Overview

WIA-SEC-024 provides a comprehensive framework for GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) compliance, enabling organizations to protect personal data, respect data subject rights, and maintain transparent data processing practices. This standard encompasses consent management, data subject rights fulfillment, data protection impact assessments, breach notification procedures, and complete audit trails for regulatory compliance across all EU member states and beyond.

Key Features

👤

Data Subject Rights

Complete implementation of all GDPR data subject rights including access, rectification, erasure (right to be forgotten), data portability, restriction of processing, and objection to automated decision-making. Automated workflows ensure timely responses within legal deadlines.

✅

Consent Management

Granular consent tracking with proof of consent, withdrawal mechanisms, and consent version control. Support for explicit consent, legitimate interest assessments, and purpose limitation ensuring transparent and lawful data processing activities.

🛡️

Data Protection Officer (DPO)

Comprehensive DPO tools including data processing inventories, privacy impact assessments, vendor management, training programs, and incident response workflows. Centralized dashboard for monitoring compliance status and managing data protection activities.

📊

Processing Agreements

Standardized data processing agreements with controllers and processors, including technical and organizational measures, sub-processor management, cross-border transfer safeguards, and audit rights ensuring accountability throughout the data supply chain.

🚨

Breach Notification

Automated breach detection, risk assessment, and notification workflows meeting the 72-hour supervisory authority notification requirement. Template-based communications for data subjects, incident documentation, and remediation tracking for regulatory compliance.
