About This Guide

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-EDU-022 E-Sports Education Standard, designed to help educators, administrators, and students build world-class educational esports programs that combine competitive gaming with valuable life skills, digital citizenship, and career development.

Whether you're launching a new program, improving an existing one, or exploring esports as a career path, this guide provides the frameworks, best practices, and practical strategies you need to succeed.
