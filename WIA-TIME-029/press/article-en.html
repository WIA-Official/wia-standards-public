Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-TIME-029, an international time adaptation standard helping travelers successfully integrate into different temporal eras, as free and open source under MIT License. It provides a complete adaptation ecosystem including cultural acclimatization, language integration, and psychological support.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

A modern person traveling to the 1920s faces a triple barrier of language, culture, and technology. Archaic English dialects, completely different social norms, life without electricity—time travelers are exposed to mental collapse risks from extreme culture shock. Without systematic adaptation protocols, even survival in historical environments becomes impossible. This isn't a technology issue—it's a matter of human dignity and survival.

WIA's Solution: WIA-TIME-029 Time Adaptation

WIA-TIME-029 is an international standard helping time travelers successfully adapt to all eras. It provides a complete adaptation framework including era-specific cultural education, language integration systems, psychological adaptation support, and re-adaptation protocols.
