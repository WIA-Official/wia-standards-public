Chapter 7: Phase 4 - Integration

Integrating consciousness measurement into healthcare, research, and AI systems

7.1 Healthcare System Integration

Electronic Health Records (EHR)

Integration with hospital EHR systems using HL7 FHIR R4:

FHIR Resources:
- Observation: PCI values, Φ estimates
- DiagnosticReport: Consciousness assessment reports
- Procedure: TMS-EEG measurement records

Hospital Information Systems

Workflow integration points:

  • Admission: Trigger consciousness monitoring order for ICU/neuro patients
  • Monitoring: Continuous or scheduled PCI measurements
  • Alerts: Integration with hospital alarm systems
  • Discharge: Summary reports and follow-up recommendations

ICU Monitoring

Real-time consciousness monitoring alongside vital signs:

  • Central monitoring display with PCI trend graphs
  • Bedside monitor integration (Philips IntelliVue, etc.)
  • Priority-based alarm escalation
  • Suppression during known interventions

7.5 Security and Privacy

Data Protection

  • Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 / OIDC
  • Authorization: Role-based access control
  • Audit: Complete access logging

Compliance

  • HIPAA (US healthcare)
  • GDPR (EU data protection)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Local regulations as applicable

7.6 Deployment Guidelines

Clinical Deployment Requirements

  • Regulatory approval (FDA, CE marking)
  • Clinical validation studies
  • Staff training (16+ hours)
  • Ongoing quality assurance

Technical Deployment

  • On-premise: HIPAA/GDPR compliant servers
  • Cloud: AWS/Azure/GCP healthcare regions with BAA
  • Monitoring: Health dashboards, latency tracking, error monitoring
