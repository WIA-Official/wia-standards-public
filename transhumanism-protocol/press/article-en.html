WIA Announces Transhumanism Protocol - Ethical Framework for Human Enhancement

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the WIA-AUG-015 Transhumanism Protocol standard. This standard provides a comprehensive framework for the safe development of human enhancement technologies, defining consciousness continuity, identity preservation, risk management, and ethical governance.

Safe Expansion of Human Capabilities

The Republic of Korea is at the forefront of advanced medical technology and AI research. WIA-AUG-015 defines five enhancement levels from baseline (H0) to posthuman (PH), encompassing six capability domains: physical, cognitive, sensory, lifespan, emotional, and consciousness. It provides safety protocols for various transition pathways including gradual biological enhancement, bio-digital integration, consciousness uploading, and human-AI merger.

Key Features:
  • Enhancement Stage Classification: Clear definitions from H0 (baseline) to PH (posthuman)
  • Consciousness Continuity: Verification of identity and self-awareness preservation
  • Risk Assessment: Comprehensive risk analysis for each transition stage
  • Ethical Governance: Responsible development and use of enhancement technologies
  • Morphological Freedom: Guarantee of individual bodily autonomy

Responsible Technological Development

This standard ensures that human enhancement technologies develop safely and ethically. All enhancements must undergo informed consent, ethics committee review, medical board approval, and safety certification. Strict criteria including continuity scores above 95% and identity preservation are applied to expand capabilities while preserving human essence.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The Transhumanism Protocol establishes an ethical foundation for human enhancement technologies." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), these technologies will improve everyone's quality of life while preserving human dignity and identity, ensuring benefits reach everyone without deepening inequality."

"Human enhancement is our future. But that future must be created carefully and responsibly. The WIA-AUG-015 standard enables all humanity to live healthier and more capable lives by maximizing potential while preserving human essence."
