### Coverage Areas

This comprehensive ebook covers:
- **Virtual Reality (VR)** - Fully immersive digital environments
- **Augmented Reality (AR)** - Digital overlay on physical world
- **Mixed Reality (MR)** - Hybrid virtual-physical interactions
- **Extended Reality (XR)** - Umbrella term for all immersive tech
- **Spatial Computing** - 3D interface paradigms
- **Haptic Feedback** - Touch and force feedback systems
- **Eye Tracking** - Gaze-based interaction
- **Hand Tracking** - Gesture recognition
- **Passthrough Technology** - Real-world video integration
- **6DOF Tracking** - Six degrees of freedom movement

### Standard Objectives

1. **Interoperability** - Cross-platform VR/AR content standards
2. **Performance** - Latency, frame rate, and quality benchmarks
3. **Safety** - User health and comfort guidelines
4. **Accessibility** - Inclusive design principles
5. **Privacy** - Biometric and spatial data protection
6. **Security** - Anti-tampering and DRM
7. **Quality** - Visual fidelity and audio standards
8. **Integration** - Enterprise system compatibility

### Why This Standard Matters

Virtual Reality is transitioning from niche gaming peripheral to mainstream computing platform. With Apple Vision Pro's launch in 2024 and Meta's Quest 3 reaching millions of users, spatial computing is becoming the next interface paradigm after mobile.

This standard provides:
- **Technical specifications** for developers
- **Certification criteria** for hardware manufacturers
- **Best practices** for content creators
- **Compliance guidelines** for enterprise deployment
- **Future-proof architecture** for emerging technologies
