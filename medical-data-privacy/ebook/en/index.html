About This Guide

This comprehensive guide addresses the critical challenge of protecting sensitive health information in an era of digital transformation. As healthcare organizations increasingly rely on electronic health records, telehealth platforms, mobile health applications, and data analytics, the risks to patient privacy have never been greater—nor have the consequences of failure.

In 2023 alone, healthcare organizations experienced 725 major data breaches exposing over 133 million patient records. The average cost of a healthcare data breach reached $10.9 million, making healthcare the most expensive industry for data breaches for the thirteenth consecutive year. These statistics underscore the urgent need for robust privacy protection frameworks.

The WIA-MEDICAL-DATA-PRIVACY standard establishes comprehensive requirements for protecting patient information throughout its lifecycle—from collection and storage to use, sharing, and destruction. This guide provides healthcare leaders, IT professionals, privacy officers, and compliance teams with the knowledge and tools needed to implement effective privacy protections while enabling the beneficial use of health data for care delivery, research, and quality improvement.
