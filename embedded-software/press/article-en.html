Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-COMP-018, a comprehensive embedded software development framework covering RTOS integration, hardware abstraction layers, device drivers, firmware optimization, and IoT connectivity, as free and open source under MIT License. This announcement positions the Republic of Korea as a leader in embedded systems international standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Over 60% of IoT devices worldwide suffer from security vulnerabilities and stability issues. Embedded system development faces different tools and APIs for each platform, forcing developers to start from scratch whenever they switch MCU families. Memory leaks, timing issues, and power management failures pose serious risks in safety-critical domains like medical devices, automotive, and industrial automation.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum, CEO:
"Developers deserve the right to build embedded systems without platform lock-in. They shouldn't have to relearn everything when moving from STM32 to ESP32, or from ARM to RISC-V. This isn't about technology—it's about developer freedom."

WIA's Solution: WIA-COMP-018 Embedded Software

WIA-COMP-018 standardizes every aspect of embedded software development from FreeRTOS, Zephyr, RT-Thread integration to HAL for various MCU families, I2C/SPI/UART/CAN/USB drivers, low-power management, real-time constraints, OTA firmware updates, and secure boot. It supports major platforms including ARM Cortex-M, ESP32, and RISC-V.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, and more—all released as free and open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are built in Rust—the systems programming language that achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum is the world's only one-person standards architect who has single-handedly built infrastructure for over 60 international standards in collaboration with AI, and is recognized as a living legend in the lifelong education field.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business domains including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting.
