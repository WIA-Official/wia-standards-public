Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the comprehensive international standard WIA-IND-002 for Smart Textile technology under the MIT License for free. This announcement marks South Korea's leadership in setting international standards for this field.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard is Needed

Currently, the Smart Textile field suffers from fragmented data formats and APIs across manufacturers, severely limiting interoperability. The lack of standardization significantly hinders technology accessibility and innovation speed.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

CEO Yeon Sam-heum:
"Smart Textile technology should no longer be a privilege of a few companies. Developers and researchers worldwide deserve access to this innovation."

WIA's Solution: WIA-IND-002

WIA-IND-002 standardizes all aspects of Smart Textile, from data formats to APIs, protocols, and integration guidelines.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standardization organization whose mission is to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 600 international standards in various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free open source.

About CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum is the world's only solo standardization architect who has built over 60 international standard infrastructures in collaboration with AI, and is recognized as a living legend in the field of lifelong education.
