Welcome to WIA Digital Funeral Standard

The WIA Digital Funeral Standard represents a comprehensive framework for integrating digital memorial services, virtual attendance, and livestreaming capabilities into modern funeral and memorial architectures. This standard defines protocols, interfaces, and best practices for compassionate, accessible, and secure digital end-of-life services.

As society increasingly embraces digital technologies, the need for standardized approaches to funeral services, memorial experiences, and grief support becomes critical. This documentation provides funeral homes, technology providers, and service organizations with the tools and knowledge necessary to implement digital memorial systems that are respectful, inclusive, and technically robust.

Our standard covers everything from basic livestreaming protocols to advanced digital legacy management, virtual cemetery platforms, and AI-powered grief support networks. Each chapter builds upon previous concepts while remaining accessible to readers with varying levels of technical expertise.
