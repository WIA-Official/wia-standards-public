The Philosophy

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) is more than a principle—it's a way of life. Originating from ancient Korea over 5,000 years ago, it calls us to ask: "Does this benefit all of humanity?"

In the age of AI, blockchain, and global connectivity, this philosophy is more relevant than ever. WIA-CORE-005 provides a concrete framework to measure, track, and optimize your impact on humanity.

7 Dimensions of Impact

❤️

Social Good

Strengthening communities, fostering inclusion, and building bridges between people. Measures the positive social connections and community resilience your work creates.

♿

Accessibility

Breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for everyone, regardless of ability, background, or resources. True benefit means universal access.

Sustainability

Protecting our planet for future generations. Measuring environmental impact, resource efficiency, and long-term ecological harmony.

🏥

Health & Wellbeing

Physical, mental, and emotional wellness for all. From healthcare access to stress reduction, every contribution to human wellbeing counts.

💰

Economic Equity

Fair distribution of resources and opportunities. Reducing inequality and ensuring economic systems serve humanity, not the other way around.

Education

Knowledge sharing, skill development, and lifelong learning. Empowering people with the tools to improve their lives and contribute to society.

Innovation

Creative solutions to human challenges. Measuring breakthrough thinking, technological advancement, and novel approaches to age-old problems.

Hongik Impact Score (HIS)

A comprehensive 0-1000+ scale measuring your total impact across all seven dimensions.

0 200 400 600 800 1000+

Your HIS is calculated through rigorous analysis of quantitative metrics, qualitative assessments, and real-world impact data. It's not just a number— it's a reflection of your commitment to 弘益人間.
