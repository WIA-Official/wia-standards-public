Complete Guide to Intelligent Animal Identification & Monitoring

"Benefit All Humanity"

The WIA Livestock Tracking Standard embodies the philosophy that technology must serve farmers, animals, and consumers. From RFID tagging to blockchain traceability, this standard ensures food safety, animal welfare, and transparent supply chains from farm to table.

About This Ebook

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of modern livestock tracking, from basic RFID tagging to advanced blockchain-based traceability systems. Whether you're a farmer, veterinarian, slaughterhouse operator, retailer, or consumer, this ebook will help you understand and implement the WIA-AGRI-009 standard.

What You'll Learn:

  • RFID and GPS tracking technologies for livestock
  • Health monitoring and disease prevention systems
  • Farm-to-table traceability with blockchain
  • Integration with farm management software
  • Compliance with international regulations
  • Practical implementation strategies

🌟 Standard Highlights

  • Universal Coverage: Supports cattle, pigs, sheep, poultry, and aquaculture
  • Technology Agnostic: Works with RFID, GPS, LoRaWAN, Bluetooth, and cellular
  • Blockchain Ready: Built-in support for immutable traceability records
  • API-First Design: RESTful APIs for seamless integration
  • Global Compliance: Meets ISO, GS1, ICAR, and national standards
  • Open Source: MIT License for worldwide adoption
