6.4 Predictive Analytics with AI

  • Calving Prediction: ML models analyze activity patterns to predict calving 24-48 hours early
  • Disease Outbreak: Anomaly detection flags unusual behavior patterns (entire herd not moving → potential disease)
  • Optimal Sale Timing: Predict when cattle reach target weight based on current ADG and feed prices
  • Heat Detection (Breeding): Accelerometer data identifies estrus cycle for AI scheduling

6.5 Data Security & Privacy

Protecting sensitive farm data:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions (owner, veterinarian, inspector have different access)
  • Data Ownership: Farmers own their data, not software vendors
  • Backup: Automated daily backups to prevent data loss
  • Compliance: GDPR (if exporting to EU), local data protection laws

Summary

Modern farm management software transforms raw livestock data (RFID scans, GPS coordinates, weight measurements) into actionable business intelligence. APIs enable integration across systems, while AI-powered analytics provide predictive insights for optimized decision-making.
