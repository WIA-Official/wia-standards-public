Key Features

🏷️ RFID Tagging

Unique identification for every animal with ISO 11784/11785 compliant RFID tags

📍 GPS Monitoring

Real-time location tracking with GPS collars and LoRaWAN network

❤️ Health Records

Complete medical history, vaccinations, treatments, and growth records

🔗 Blockchain Traceability

Immutable farm-to-table traceability for food safety and consumer trust

🐄 Multi-Species Support

Cattle, pigs, sheep, poultry, and aquaculture tracking

📊 Analytics Dashboard

Herd management insights, growth rates, and productivity metrics

🔔 Smart Alerts

Automated notifications for health issues, location anomalies, and events

🌐 Global Standards

Compliant with ISO 11784/11785, GS1, and national livestock regulations
