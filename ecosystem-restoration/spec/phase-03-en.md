# WIA-ENE-072: Ecosystem Restoration Standard
## Phase 03: Verification & Testing

### Version: 1.0
### Status: Active
### Last Updated: 2025-12-25

---

## 1. Introduction

Phase 03 establishes requirements for monitoring restoration progress and verifying achievement of objectives.

---

## 2. Monitoring Program

### 2.1 Monitoring Design

**REQUIREMENT 2.1.1**: Monitoring SHALL include:
- Restoration site plots (stratified random)
- Reference site plots (same methods)
- Control sites (if applicable)
- Consistent sampling locations

**REQUIREMENT 2.1.2**: Monitoring frequency:
- Year 1: Quarterly
- Years 2-5: Annual
- Years 6-10: Biennial
- Years 10+: Every 5 years

### 2.2 Indicators

**REQUIREMENT 2.2.1**: Monitor key indicators:
- Vegetation cover and composition
- Species richness and diversity
- Structure (height, layers, density)
- Invasive species cover
- Soil properties (selected parameters)
- Hydrology (water levels, quality)
- Wildlife presence and abundance

---

## 3. Data Analysis

### 3.1 Statistical Analysis

**REQUIREMENT 3.1.1**: Analysis SHALL:
- Compare restoration to reference sites
- Assess temporal trends
- Evaluate treatment effects
- Test against success criteria

### 3.2 Reporting

**REQUIREMENT 3.2.1**: Annual reports SHALL include:
- Monitoring data summary
- Progress toward objectives
- Problems identified
- Adaptive management recommendations

---

## 4. Adaptive Management

**REQUIREMENT 4.1.1**: Implement adaptive management:
- Review monitoring results
- Identify underperforming areas
- Adjust strategies
- Document learning

---

## 5. Phase 03 Completion

Phase 03 continues throughout project life. Interim milestones:
- [ ] Year 1: Establishment success verified
- [ ] Year 5: Mid-term objectives assessed
- [ ] Year 10: Long-term trajectory confirmed
