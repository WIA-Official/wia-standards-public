# WIA-ENE-072: Ecosystem Restoration Standard
## Phase 04: Certification & Maintenance

### Version: 1.0
### Status: Active
### Last Updated: 2025-12-25

---

## 1. Introduction

Phase 04 defines requirements for certifying restoration success and ensuring long-term sustainability.

---

## 2. Certification Requirements

### 2.1 Success Criteria Assessment

**REQUIREMENT 2.1.1**: Certification requires:
- All success criteria met or on trajectory
- Self-sustaining ecosystem evident
- Native species diversity restored
- Ecosystem functions recovered
- No intensive management required

### 2.2 Documentation

**REQUIREMENT 2.2.1**: Certification package SHALL include:
- Complete monitoring dataset
- Comparative analysis to reference
- Photographic documentation
- Peer review report
- Stakeholder acceptance

---

## 3. Certification Process

### 3.1 Application

**REQUIREMENT 3.1.1**: Applicant SHALL submit:
- Certification application form
- All required documentation
- Certification fee

### 3.2 Review

**REQUIREMENT 3.2.1**: WIA SHALL conduct:
- Document review
- Site inspection
- Stakeholder consultation
- Certification decision

### 3.3 Certification Levels

**Level 1**: Partial Success (50-70% of criteria met)
**Level 2**: Substantial Success (70-90% of criteria met)
**Level 3**: Full Success (>90% of criteria met)

---

## 4. Long-Term Stewardship

### 4.1 Stewardship Plan

**REQUIREMENT 4.1.1**: Certified sites SHALL have:
- Long-term stewardship plan
- Funding mechanism (endowment, covenant)
- Responsible party identified
- Monitoring schedule (every 5 years minimum)

### 4.2 Maintenance

**REQUIREMENT 4.2.1**: Ongoing maintenance may include:
- Invasive species control
- Infrastructure maintenance
- Adaptive management
- Public access management

---

## 5. Certification Validity

**REQUIREMENT 5.1.1**: Certification is valid:
- For 10 years from issuance
- Renewable upon re-assessment
- Subject to revocation if conditions deteriorate

---

## 6. Phase 04 Completion

Phase 04 is complete when:
- [ ] Certification awarded
- [ ] Stewardship plan approved
- [ ] Stewardship funding secured
- [ ] Public recognition event held
