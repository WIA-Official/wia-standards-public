# WIA-ENE-072: Ecosystem Restoration Standard
## Phase 02: Implementation

### Version: 1.0
### Status: Active
### Last Updated: 2025-12-25

---

## 1. Introduction

Phase 02 defines requirements for implementing restoration actions based on plans developed in Phase 01.

---

## 2. Site Preparation

### 2.1 Access and Infrastructure

**REQUIREMENT 2.1.1**: Site preparation SHALL include:
- Access roads and trails (minimize disturbance)
- Equipment staging areas
- Temporary facilities (if needed)
- Erosion control during construction

### 2.2 Threat Removal

**REQUIREMENT 2.2.1**: Ongoing stressors SHALL be addressed:
- Invasive species control
- Pollution source elimination
- Drainage modifications
- Grazing exclusion (if applicable)

---

## 3. Restoration Interventions

### 3.1 Vegetation Establishment

**REQUIREMENT 3.1.1**: Plant materials SHALL meet standards:
- Native species from appropriate seed zones
- Genetic diversity (multiple source populations)
- Quality specifications (height, root development)
- Disease-free certification

**REQUIREMENT 3.1.2**: Planting SHALL follow protocols:
- Appropriate timing (seasonal, weather)
- Proper techniques (depth, spacing)
- Protection measures (shelters, fencing)
- Quality control (supervision, inspection)

### 3.2 Soil Restoration

**REQUIREMENT 3.2.1**: Soil interventions SHALL:
- Match treatment to soil limitations
- Use appropriate amendments
- Minimize compaction
- Prevent erosion

### 3.3 Hydrological Restoration

**REQUIREMENT 3.3.1**: Water management SHALL:
- Restore natural hydrology where possible
- Install structures per engineering specifications
- Monitor water levels and flow
- Adapt to observed conditions

---

## 4. Implementation Documentation

**REQUIREMENT 4.1.1**: All activities SHALL be documented:
- As-built drawings and GPS locations
- Dates and weather conditions
- Materials used (species, quantities, sources)
- Contractors and labor
- Deviations from plans
- Photographic documentation

---

## 5. Phase 02 Completion

Phase 02 is complete when:
- [ ] All planned interventions implemented
- [ ] As-built documentation submitted
- [ ] Site stabilized (erosion control)
- [ ] Initial establishment monitoring initiated
