🦋 Ecosystem Restoration

Complete E-Book - English Edition

WIA-ENE-072 Standard v1.0

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Ecosystem Restoration.

This e-book provides detailed, practical guidance for planning, implementing, and monitoring ecosystem restoration projects across diverse biomes. From initial assessment to long-term management, each chapter builds on scientific principles while incorporating traditional ecological knowledge and community engagement strategies.

Whether you're a restoration practitioner, land manager, researcher, or community leader, this resource will equip you with the knowledge and tools needed to successfully restore degraded ecosystems and recover biodiversity.
