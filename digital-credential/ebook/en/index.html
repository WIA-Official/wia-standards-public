WIA-EDU-011 Complete Guide

About This Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on the WIA Digital Credential Standard (WIA-EDU-011). This book covers everything you need to know about creating, issuing, verifying, and managing blockchain-based digital credentials that are secure, verifiable, and internationally recognized.

Digital credentials represent the future of educational certification, combining the trust of traditional degrees with the security and portability of blockchain technology. This standard ensures that credentials are tamper-proof, instantly verifiable, and accessible to all stakeholders.
