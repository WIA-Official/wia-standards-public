Chapter 7: International Recognition

The Global Education Mobility Challenge

Over 6 million students study abroad annually, but credential verification across borders remains slow, expensive, and error-prone. WIA-EDU-011 solves this by creating a globally-recognized standard for digital credentials.

Cross-Border Verification

Traditional Process

  • Request official transcripts (2-6 weeks)
  • Translate documents ($50-$200 per document)
  • Get apostille or embassy certification ($100-$300)
  • Mail documents internationally ($30-$100)
  • Credential evaluation service ($200-$600)
  • Total time: 6-12 weeks
  • Total cost: $400-$1,200

WIA-EDU-011 Process

  • Share credential from wallet (instant)
  • Automatic verification (3 seconds)
  • Built-in metadata translation
  • Blockchain-anchored authenticity
  • Total time: < 1 minute
  • Total cost: $0
