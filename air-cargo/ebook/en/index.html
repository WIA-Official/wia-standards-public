About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive WIA Air Cargo Standard documentation. This guide provides in-depth coverage of air cargo operations, regulations, technology implementations, and future trends in the aviation logistics industry.

The air cargo industry moves over 61 million metric tons of goods annually, representing approximately 35% of global trade value. This standard addresses the complete ecosystem of air freight operations, from booking and documentation to tracking and delivery.

Each chapter includes practical TypeScript code examples, real-world case studies, regulatory requirements, and implementation guidelines for developers, logistics professionals, and industry stakeholders.
