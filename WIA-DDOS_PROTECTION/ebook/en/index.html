📖 Guide Overview

WIA-DDOS_PROTECTION is a comprehensive standard for protecting systems from Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks. This guide covers defense strategies across all layers, from network infrastructure to application level, ensuring robust protection against modern cyber threats.

Designed for security professionals at all levels—from beginners to advanced engineers—this practical learning resource combines real-world case studies with the latest defense techniques. Learn systematically from understanding DDoS attack vectors to implementing cutting-edge mitigation strategies.

Learning Objectives: Understand DDoS attack types, establish multi-layer defense strategies, leverage cloud-based DDoS services, build real-time monitoring and response systems, implement rate limiting, utilize CDN and WAF technologies, and develop comprehensive incident response plans.

弘益人間 (Hong-Ik-In-Gan)

Benefit All Humanity

The WIA-DDOS_PROTECTION standard embodies this ancient Korean philosophy by ensuring that digital services remain accessible, reliable, and secure for all users worldwide. By defending against DDoS attacks, we protect the fundamental right to access information and services in our interconnected digital world.
