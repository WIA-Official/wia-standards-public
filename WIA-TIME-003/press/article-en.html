Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the WIA-TIME-003 Quantum Time Theory standard under MIT License for free. This standard integrates cutting-edge quantum physics concepts including quantum superposition of time states, many-worlds interpretation, quantum entanglement across time, and temporal quantum tunneling, providing the theoretical foundation for time travel and quantum computing.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Modern physics raises fundamental questions about the nature of time. While Einstein's relativity unified time and space, quantum mechanics tells us that time can exist in superposition of multiple states until observed. However, these revolutionary concepts lack standardized frameworks, leading researchers to use different interpretations and formulas. For advancement in time travel research, quantum computing, and quantum cryptography, a unified quantum time theory standard is essential.

WIA's Solution: WIA-TIME-003 Quantum Time Theory

WIA-TIME-003 integrates core concepts of modern quantum physics into time theory, including the Wheeler-DeWitt equation, Everett's many-worlds interpretation, EPR paradox, Schrödinger equation, decoherence theory, and WKB approximation. It provides TypeScript SDK and CLI tools for quantum time state manipulation, wavefunction collapse simulation, and decoherence rate calculation.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization whose mission is to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 600 international standards across diverse fields including time travel, quantum computing, AI, security, environment, and healthcare, releasing all standards as free open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are developed in Rust. Rust is a systems programming language developed by Mozilla that uniquely achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.
