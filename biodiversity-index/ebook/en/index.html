WIA Biodiversity Index Standard

A Comprehensive Guide to Measuring and Preserving Life on Earth
Version 1.0 | December 2025

About This eBook

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA Biodiversity Index Standard from fundamentals to advanced implementation. Whether you're a conservation biologist, environmental manager, policy maker, or developer, this book provides the knowledge and tools needed to measure, monitor, and preserve biodiversity effectively.

The standard is built on the philosophy of 弘益人間 (홍익인간) - Benefit All Humanity - extending this principle to all life on Earth.
