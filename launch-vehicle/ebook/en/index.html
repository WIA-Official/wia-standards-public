WIA-SPACE-004 (Launch Vehicle Standard) is a comprehensive standard for launch vehicle design, manufacturing, and operations. As the space age accelerates, commercial space launches become routine, and reusable rockets emerge, systematic launch vehicle standards are more important than ever.

Core Elements of Launch Vehicles

  • Propulsion Systems: Rocket engines, solid/liquid fuel, thrust control
  • Structural Systems: Multi-stage rockets, fairings, separation mechanisms
  • Guidance/Navigation/Control: GNC systems, attitude control, trajectory tracking
  • Launch Facilities: Launch pads, fuel supply, ground support equipment
  • Safety Systems: Emergency escape, flight termination systems, range safety
  • Payload Integration: Interface standards, vibration protection, environmental control
  • Launch Operations: Countdown, launch windows, weather criteria
  • Reusability: Recovery systems, landing technology, maintenance processes

Core Philosophy

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - Broadly Benefit Humanity

Space belongs to all humanity. By standardizing and sharing launch vehicle technology, we increase space accessibility, reduce space development costs, and enable more people to benefit from space.
