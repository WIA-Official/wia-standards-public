Core Features

🔍

Decision Logging

Comprehensive logging of all AI decisions with context, inputs, outputs, and reasoning traces. Capture every critical decision point for full transparency and accountability.

📊

Audit Trail Management

Immutable audit trails with cryptographic verification. Track decision lineage, detect anomalies, and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

✅

Compliance Checking

Automated compliance verification against industry standards (GDPR, CCPA, AI Act). Real-time alerts for policy violations and regulatory risks.

⚠️

Risk Assessment

Continuous risk monitoring and impact analysis. Identify high-risk decisions, bias patterns, and potential failures before they cause harm.
