Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released a comprehensive international standard for Digital Addiction Prevention under MIT License for free. This announcement positions South Korea as a leader in international standards for this field.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard is Needed

The Digital Addiction Prevention field currently suffers from interoperability issues and inefficiency due to lack of standardization. A unified global standard is urgently needed.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

Dr. Yeon Sam-heum, CEO:
"Digital Addiction Prevention is a core technology of the future. We aim to prevent chaos caused by lack of standards and create a foundation for global progress together."

WIA's Solution: WIA-CHILD-007

WIA-CHILD-007 ensures interoperability of the Digital Addiction Prevention ecosystem through standardized data formats, API interfaces, and communication protocols.

Standard Components

Phase Title Description
1 Data Format Standard data format and structure definition
2 API Interface SDK and API specification
3 Protocol Communication protocol and interoperability
4 Integration Platform integration guide

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization with the mission of providing equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 400 international standards in various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free open source.
