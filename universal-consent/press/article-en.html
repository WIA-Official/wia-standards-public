Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the international standard WIA-CORE-002 for universal consent management with full GDPR/CCPA compliance under MIT License at zero cost. This announcement presents a new paradigm that simultaneously achieves personal data self-determination and corporate regulatory compliance.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Globally, over 400 million personal data breaches occur annually, with GDPR violation fines reaching 4% of total revenue. Companies spend millions of dollars annually on compliance costs navigating disparate privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, etc.) across different jurisdictions, yet remain continuously exposed to risk due to the absence of consistent consent management systems. Users lack proper control over how their data is used.

CEO Yeon Samheum:
"Personal data is the oil of the 21st century. But unlike oil, ownership of personal data must belong to individuals. Current systems are company-driven with users playing a passive role. This standard corrects that power imbalance. It enables users to truly control their own data."

WIA's Solution: WIA-CORE-002 Universal Consent

WIA-CORE-002 is a universal consent management framework that integrates full GDPR/CCPA compliance, consent propagation, granular permission control, and cross-platform consent synchronization. As a single source of truth, it maintains consistent consent state across all systems while providing complete cryptographically verifiable audit trails.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization whose mission is to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA is developing over 600 international standards in various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free open source.

🦀 Technology Stack: WIA's core APIs are developed in the Rust language. Rust is a systems programming language developed by Mozilla and is the only language that achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Samheum is the world's only solo standardization architect who has built over 60 international standard infrastructures in collaboration with AI, and is recognized as a living legend in lifelong education.

Since founding SmileStory Inc. in 2009, he has operated 11 business areas including travel, insurance, publishing, education, media, web/software development, medical devices, medical tourism, and business consulting.
