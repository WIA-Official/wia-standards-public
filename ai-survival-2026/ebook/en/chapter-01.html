Introduction: A New Chapter in Human History

In 2025, artificial intelligence is no longer a technology of the future. The entire world is being swept into the massive vortex of the AI revolution, representing a paradigm shift comparable to the steam engine, electricity, and the internet in human history. The global AI market has grown to approximately $400-700 billion in 2025 (estimates vary by source), with projections reaching $827 billion by 2030 at an annual growth rate of 27.7%. Gartner expects worldwide AI spending to reach $1.5 trillion in 2025.

Expected Global AI Spending in 2025 (Gartner)

This chapter maps the complete landscape of the global AI ecosystem in 2025, examining Korea's position and role within it. From America's Stargate Project to China's AI+ Initiative, Europe's AI Gigafactories, and the Middle East's ambitious investments, we vividly present the scene of nations racing toward AI supremacy.

Part 1: The Global AI Market Explosion

The Stargate Project: The Largest AI Infrastructure Investment in History

On January 21, 2025, President Donald Trump announced the Stargate Project, with participation from OpenAI, SoftBank, Oracle, and MGX. This project will invest $500 billion in American AI infrastructure by 2029, with an initial $100 billion deployed immediately. The first data center, built in Abilene, Texas, is already operational, armed with Oracle cloud infrastructure and Nvidia chips.

In September 2025, OpenAI and Oracle announced five new AI data center sites, representing a total capacity of 7 gigawatts and more than $40 billion in investment. This is ahead of the original schedule, with OpenAI CFO Sarah Friar emphasizing, "No one in history has ever built data centers this fast."

"No one in history has ever built data centers this fast."

— Sarah Friar, CFO of OpenAI

The scale of the Stargate Project is comparable to the Manhattan Project. A single site is expected to have power capacity exceeding 1 gigawatt, enough to power about 750,000 homes. This project goes beyond simple infrastructure construction—it's the core of America's national strategy to maintain AI supremacy.

Big Tech's AI Arms Race

American big tech companies are pouring astronomical funds into AI development. The U.S. AI market size is $7.4 billion in 2025, the largest in the world, with OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Meta competing for AI dominance in their respective domains.

OpenAI launched GPT-5 in August 2025, maintaining its lead in the AI model competition. GPT-5 is designed as a unified system that automatically routes between fast responses and deep thinking based on user requests, recording a state-of-the-art 94.6% on the AIME 2025 mathematics benchmark. GPT-5's context window is 272,000 tokens (input), 128,000 tokens (output), enabling processing of large documents.

Google counters with Gemini 2.5. Gemini 2.5 Pro shows strength in multimodal capabilities, particularly excelling in document and image analysis. Gemini's knowledge cutoff is January 2025, more recent than GPT-5's June 2024, and integration with Google Search enables real-time information access.

China's Strategic AI Investment

China is undertaking massive state-level investment to keep up with the U.S. in the AI supremacy race. In March 2025, China announced a 1 trillion yuan ($138 billion) national venture capital guide fund, focused on cutting-edge technology sectors including AI, quantum technology, semiconductors, and renewable energy.

$138B
China's National AI Investment Fund (2025)

China's 2025 AI investment is expected to reach $84-98 billion, with government investment accounting for $56 billion and internet companies for $24 billion. This contrasts with America's private-led investment approach, taking a government-led form.

The AI+ Initiative: AI Integration Across the Economy

China's AI+ Initiative was announced at the Central Economic Work Conference in December 2024, aiming to broadly integrate AI into six core areas of the economy and society by 2027. This extends the success of the past Internet+ policy to the AI sector.

By 2027, adoption rates of next-generation intelligent terminals and AI agents are expected to exceed 70%, meaning AI technology will become commonplace throughout China. As of late 2024, nearly 200 generative AI models are registered and in service in China, with over 600 million registered users.

DeepSeek's Revolution and Cost Innovation

In early 2025, Chinese startup DeepSeek brought revolutionary cost innovation to the AI industry. DeepSeek-R1's API call costs are less than 5% compared to OpenAI o1, achieving similar performance to GPT-4o at much lower costs. This results from utilizing Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture, FP8 mixed precision training, and distillation techniques.

Part 2: The Global Big Tech AI Supremacy War

GPT-5's Innovation

GPT-5 was launched on August 7, 2025, featuring a unified system and smart router. If a user's request is simple, it uses a fast model; if complex, it activates a deeper, slower "thinking" function. GPT-5 delivers state-of-the-art performance in coding, math, writing, health, and visual recognition.

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman described GPT-5 as "an important step on the road to AGI," stating it provides "PhD-level skills in many domains." Early testers were deeply impressed by the leap in coding and problem-solving capabilities.

Gemini 2.5's Strengths

Google's Gemini 2.5 shows strength in multimodal AI. Gemini 2.5 is designed to process text, images, and audio as multimodal from the start. Gemini 2.5 Deep Think showed impressive performance at USAMO 2025 (USA Mathematical Olympiad), solving problems that were completely outside AI's realm just a few years ago.

Gemini 2.5 Pro recorded 72.5% on SWE-Bench, demonstrating top-tier coding ability alongside Anthropic's Claude Opus 4. It ranked first in WebDev Arena.

Part 3: Korea's AI Ecosystem - Challenges and Opportunities

Market Size and Growth Rate

Korea's AI market was 2.6123 trillion won in 2023, expected to grow at an annual average of 14.9% to reach 4.4636 trillion won by 2027. Some forecasts estimate Korea's 2025 AI market size at 1.9074 trillion won, with long-term growth projections reaching 10.5 trillion won.

According to Gartner, the global AI market is expected to grow from $233.4 billion in 2024 to $1.7716 trillion in 2032, at an annual average of 29.2%. Korea's growth rate (14.9%) is about half the global average (29.2%), indicating the need for catch-up.

The World's Second AI Basic Law

The AI Basic Law, which passed the National Assembly on December 26, 2024, was promulgated on January 21, 2025, and will take effect from January 22, 2026. This is the second comprehensive AI legislation in the world after the EU.

On September 8, 2025, the Ministry of Science and ICT released the draft enforcement decree for the AI Basic Law, prepared through over 70 rounds of opinion collection from industry, academia, civil society, and related ministries. The ministry prepared the enforcement decree with emphasis on promotion rather than regulation, considering global regulatory trends and the domestic AI industry.

Naver: Sovereign AI and HyperCLOVA X

Naver is pursuing a "Sovereign AI" strategy based on its own large language model HyperCLOVA X, emphasizing data sovereignty. Naver plans to introduce AI Briefing, a new AI search service, in the first half of 2025, including features for result summarization, source display, and related video/image provision.

Kakao: AI Orchestration and OpenAI Partnership

Kakao has adopted an "AI Model Orchestration" strategy, utilizing both self-developed AI models and excellent external APIs appropriately. Kakao has formed a strategic alliance with OpenAI and prohibited the use of DeepSeek.

Part 4: Korea from a Global Perspective

World-Class Semiconductor Capabilities

Korea ranked 6th overall in global AI competitiveness with its hardware competitiveness (semiconductors, batteries). SK hynix's HBM is an essential component for Nvidia's H100 and H200 GPUs, and Samsung Electronics is also accelerating AI semiconductor development.

Korea's HBM Dominance

SK hynix holds over 50% of the global HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) market share, recording an overwhelming first place. HBM is high-performance memory essential for AI model training and inference, closely integrated with GPUs to maximize data transfer speed. Global AI companies like OpenAI, Google, and Microsoft cannot build next-generation AI systems without SK hynix's HBM.

Software and Data Weaknesses

However, Korea shows distinct weaknesses in software capabilities and data infrastructure. The large-scale foundation model development capabilities possessed by global AI leaders like OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic are still lacking in Korea.

While Naver's HyperCLOVA X shows excellent performance in Korean language processing, it significantly lags behind OpenAI and Google in global general-purpose AI model competition. The language barrier of Korean limits data collection and model training, making it difficult to secure competitiveness in the English-centered global AI ecosystem.

Conclusion: 2025, At the Crossroads of the AI Revolution

2025 will be recorded as a decisive year in AI history. America's Stargate Project, China's AI+ Initiative, Europe's Gigafactories, and the Middle East's astronomical investments all concentrated in this period. GPT-5 and Gemini 2.5 took another step toward AGI, and DeepSeek opened the possibility of AI democratization through cost revolution.

Korea stands at the center of this massive change. Its world-class semiconductor capabilities represented by HBM, the platform competitiveness of Naver and Kakao, and the manufacturing know-how of Samsung and Hyundai are assets of Korean AI. However, the software gap, talent drain, and ecosystem vulnerabilities are challenges to overcome.

"In the AI era, Korea's destiny is in our hands."

The global AI landscape is rapidly reshaping, and Korea needs strategic choices to survive in this competition. We can't be number one in every field. But we can be the best in the world in the fields we choose.

2025 is just the beginning. The real story of the AI revolution unfolds from now on.
