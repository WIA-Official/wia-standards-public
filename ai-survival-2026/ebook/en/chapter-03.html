Major AI Models Comparison

GPT vs Claude vs Gemini, and the Korean LLM Challenge

Introduction: The Benchmark Paradox

As of November 2025, the global LLM market is witnessing unprecedented "performance convergence." OpenAI's GPT-4o, Anthropic's Claude 4 Opus, and Google's Gemini 2.5 Pro record nearly identical scores of 85-90 on major benchmarks.

85-90
MMLU Score Range for GPT-4o, Claude 4, Gemini 2.5 (2025)

The Benchmark Paradox

Definition: As benchmark scores of major LLMs converge, actual business value is determined by differences in specialized areas.

Implication: "Which model is the best?" is no longer a meaningful question. The real question is "What model is optimal for our work?"

1. Changes in the Global LLM Market Landscape

The global LLM market in 2025 is undergoing dramatic restructuring. Just two years ago, OpenAI dominated with 50% market share, but currently it has been cut in half to 25%. Instead, Anthropic has emerged as the new leader with 32%, and Google is rapidly catching up at 20%.

1.2 Strategic Positioning of Major Players

Anthropic: Rose to the top with 32% market share. Claude 4 series effectively mitigated hallucination and bias issues through its Constitutional AI safety framework. Claude 4 Opus recorded 93.7% on HumanEval (coding benchmark), surpassing GPT-4o (90.2%).

OpenAI: Market share dropped from 50% to 25%. GPT-5's launch delay, high pricing (GPT-4.5 input token $75/M vs Gemini Flash $0.15/M — 500x difference), and governance concerns from the Sam Altman incident contributed. However, OpenAI remains unmatched in reasoning with o3 and dominates consumer market (ChatGPT).

Google: At 20% share (3rd place), showing fastest recent growth. Gemini 2.5 Pro's 1M token context window enables 10x longer document processing than competitors. Excellent price competitiveness ($1.25/M input) and dominant image processing (OCR) performance.

2. Korean AI Industry Analysis

Korea maintains 6th-7th place in global AI competitiveness, establishing itself as an Asian AI powerhouse. In 2025, the domestic AI market size reaches 3.4385 trillion won, with a high annual growth rate of 14.3%, projected to reach 4.4636 trillion won by 2027.

Naver - HyperCLOVA X

Naver's HyperCLOVA X sets the benchmark for Korean language AI models. HyperCLOVA X THINK, unveiled in June 2025, is a reasoning-specialized model that learned 6 trillion tokens of high-quality Korean-English data, proving performance on par with GPT-4o1.

Upstage - Solar Pro 2

Upstage's Solar Pro 2 surprised the world by achieving 70B-class performance with a compact 31B parameters. In July 2025, it scored 58 on Artificial Analysis's Intelligence Index, entering global 12th place and achieving 5 points higher than GPT-4o.

LG AI Research - EXAONE

LG AI Research's EXAONE is the only Korean model selected as a "notable AI model" by Stanford University's AI Index 2025. EXAONE 3.5, released in December 2024, comes in three sizes (2.4B, 7.8B, 32B) and showed superiority in usability, long text processing, coding, and math compared to Meta's Llama, Google's Gemma, and Alibaba's Qwen.

Key Takeaways

  • Benchmark scores are converging; selection criteria shift to specialized capabilities
  • Anthropic leads enterprise market (32%), OpenAI dropped to 25%
  • Korean LLMs (HyperCLOVA X, Solar Pro 2, EXAONE) show competitive Korean language performance
  • Open source models (Llama 4, Mixtral) challenge proprietary models
  • TCO-based decision framework needed, not just "best model" selection
