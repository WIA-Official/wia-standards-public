Humanity's Final Invention: At the Threshold

Introduction: 2025, AGI Is No Longer Science Fiction

In November 2025, the AI industry is converging on one question. Not "When will AGI arrive?" but "When AGI arrives, will we be ready?" OpenAI CEO Sam Altman has publicly stated AGI could be achieved within 2025. Google DeepMind's Demis Hassabis projects 5-10 years. Anthropic's Dario Amodei pinpoints 2027.

But more important than numbers is the paradigm shift. GPT-5, released by OpenAI in August 2025, wasn't just a model upgrade. Equipped with a unified reasoning system, GPT-5 achieved 94.6% accuracy on AIME 2025 math competition, demonstrating expert-level reasoning for the first time. Hallucinations decreased 6x from previous models. This isn't a "smart parrot" anymore — it's a system that truly reasons.

The Three-Layer Framework for AGI Timelines

Layer 1: Technical Timeline — When is AGI technically feasible? Current: GPT-5 (2025) → Proto-AGI (2027-2030?) → Full AGI (?). Problem: Definition is ambiguous.

Layer 2: Social Timeline — When will society feel AGI's impact? Job automation tipping point: 2031 projected. Key finding: Social adaptation lags technology by 3-5 years.

Layer 3: Geopolitical Timeline — When will AGI determine national hegemony? US-China AI competition critical period: 2025-2028. Trump administration declared AGI achievement goal within term.

1. Current AGI Technology Level

Expert projections for AGI have dramatically shifted. Just 5 years ago, experts predicted 2060. In 2025, predictions have rapidly moved forward to 2026-2030. This reflects structural changes from explosive LLM development like ChatGPT.

AGI Benchmarks: Three Levels

Level Definition Estimated
Level 1: Task Automation AGI Performs all economically valuable tasks cheaper than humans 2028-2030
Level 2: Cognitive AGI Human-level reasoning, learning, creativity 2030-2035
Level 3: Full AGI Adapts to all environments including physical robot tasks 2040+

Key discovery: Level 1 (task automation) is achievable first. By 2027, it will be widely recognized that "almost all economically valuable labor (mental and physical) will be automatable."

2. Major Companies' AGI Roadmaps

OpenAI: Stargate Project — $500B Bet

OpenAI announced Stargate in January 2025: $500 billion over 4 years, partnered with Microsoft, Oracle, SoftBank. Goal: 5 gigawatts of AI datacenter capacity. The Texas Abilene site is already operational with Nvidia GB200 racks, developing 2 million chip capacity. This represents OpenAI's conviction that Scaling Law still works.

2026-2027
Anthropic CEO's AGI Prediction (Dario Amodei)

Google DeepMind: World Model Vision

Demis Hassabis's vision is based on the "World Model" concept — AI that can understand and simulate the world like the human brain. Gemini 2.5 Pro with 1 million token context window is the first step. Google's roadmap requires three breakthroughs: Reasoning (Deep Think mode), Planning (Project Mariner multi-agent), and Memory (Project Astra).

Anthropic: Safety-First, But Fast

Dario Amodei stated: "If you extrapolate current curves, it looks like 2026 or 2027." Anthropic's Constitutional AI trains models with a "digital constitution" for ethical decisions. Their ASL (AI Safety Level) system has 5 levels, with ASL-3 (malicious actor capability enhancement) expected by 2026.

3. China: State-Led AGI Strategy

China's 2017 "AI Development Plan" set a clear goal: global AI leader by 2030. This isn't just a technology goal — it's national security and economic sovereignty. Key players include BAAI (Beijing Academy of AI) developing Wu Dao 3.0 and Shanghai AI Lab with InternLM3.

DeepSeek demonstrated AGI development is no longer reserved for $100 billion budgets. They trained a GPT-4o level model for just $5.6 million. Despite US chip sanctions, DeepSeek-R1 matched OpenAI o1 in reasoning performance. The path to AGI is not singular, and it's closer than we thought.
