Welcome to the WIA-SPORTS-TECH E-Book

This comprehensive guide explores the cutting-edge technologies revolutionizing sports performance, training, medicine, and fan experiences. From wearable sensors tracking every physiological metric to AI systems analyzing game strategy in real-time, sports technology is transforming how athletes train, compete, and recover.

The global sports technology market reached $31.1 billion in 2025, with professional teams investing an average of $2.3 million annually in technology infrastructure. This ebook examines the standards, protocols, and best practices that ensure these technologies work together seamlessly while protecting athlete privacy and promoting fair competition.
