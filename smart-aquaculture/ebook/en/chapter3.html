CHAPTER 3

🏥 Fish Health & Disease Prevention

Learn computer vision-based disease detection, behavioral monitoring, and predictive health analytics for aquaculture.

3.1 Common Aquaculture Diseases

Bacterial Diseases

  • Vibriosis: Caused by Vibrio bacteria. Symptoms: lethargy, hemorrhages, ulcers. Mortality: 10-50% if untreated.
  • Furunculosis: Aeromonas salmonicida. Boil-like lesions. Common in cold-water fish.
  • Columnaris: Flavobacterium. White patches on gills, fins. Spreads rapidly in warm water.

Viral Diseases

  • Viral Hemorrhagic Septicemia (VHS): High mortality in salmonids. No treatment, prevention via biosecurity.
  • Lymphocystis: Viral, causes wart-like growths. Low mortality but reduces marketability.

Parasitic Diseases

  • Sea Lice (Lepeophtheirus): Ectoparasites common in salmon farms. Damage skin, stress fish.
  • Ich (Ichthyophthirius): Freshwater parasite. White spots on skin. Treat with salt baths or formalin.

3.2 Computer Vision for Disease Detection

AI-powered cameras analyze fish appearance and behavior to detect diseases 2-3 days before clinical signs:

  • Skin Color Analysis: Darkening or pale color indicates stress or disease
  • Swimming Behavior: Erratic movements, loss of balance, surface gulping
  • Feeding Response: Sudden drop in appetite is early disease indicator
  • Body Lesions: Detect hemorrhages, ulcers, fin damage automatically

3.3 Biosecurity Protocols

  • Quarantine new fish for 14-21 days before stocking
  • Disinfect equipment between tanks (iodine, hydrogen peroxide)
  • Limit farm access, use footbaths at entrances
  • Source fingerlings only from certified disease-free hatcheries
  • Vaccinate when available (vibriosis, furunculosis vaccines exist)

3.4 Treatment Strategies

Antibiotics: Last resort due to resistance concerns. Common: oxytetracycline, florfenicol. Observe withdrawal periods (typically 500-degree days).

Probiotics: Beneficial bacteria (Bacillus, Lactobacillus) added to feed improve gut health and immunity.

Immunostimulants: Beta-glucans, vitamins C&E boost immune response naturally.
