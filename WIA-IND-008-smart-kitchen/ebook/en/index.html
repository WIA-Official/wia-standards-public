About This eBook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on the WIA-IND-008 Smart Kitchen Standard. This eBook provides an in-depth exploration of how intelligent technology is revolutionizing home cooking, from connected appliances to AI-powered meal planning.

Through 8 detailed chapters, you'll discover how smart kitchen ecosystems work, learn about cutting-edge technologies, and understand how to integrate these systems into your daily life. Each chapter includes practical examples, technical insights, and forward-looking perspectives on the future of culinary technology.

Guided by the principle of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this standard aims to make cooking more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone.
