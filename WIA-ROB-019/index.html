📐 Technical Architecture

Body Structure

Standardized skeletal framework with 30+ degrees of freedom, modular joint design, and biomechanical proportions.

Sensor Suite

IMU arrays, force-torque sensors, cameras, LiDAR, tactile sensors, and proprioceptive feedback systems.

Control Systems

Real-time kinematics/dynamics, trajectory planning, adaptive controllers, and model predictive control.

AI Integration

Deep learning for perception, reinforcement learning for control, and transformer models for language understanding.

Power Management

Battery systems (LiPo/Li-ion), regenerative braking, wireless charging, and energy harvesting capabilities.

Communication

WiFi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, 5G connectivity, edge computing, and secure encrypted channels.

Performance Metrics

Walking Speed

Target: 1.2 m/s average, 1.8 m/s maximum, with stable gait on various terrains.

Battery Life

Minimum 4 hours continuous operation, 8-12 hours typical use with energy optimization.

Payload Capacity

Carry 5-15 kg while maintaining stable locomotion and balance control.

Response Time

< 100ms sensory processing, < 50ms motor control loop, < 200ms total system latency.

Operating Range

Temperature: -10°C to 45°C, Humidity: 10-90% RH, Indoor/Outdoor capable.

Accuracy

Position: ±5mm, Orientation: ±1°, Force control: ±0.5N, Vision: 95%+ recognition.
