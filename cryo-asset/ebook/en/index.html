About This Guide
================================================================================

This ebook provides comprehensive guidance on the CRYO-ASSET standard for
managing assets during cryopreservation. It covers all aspects of asset
registry management, from initial registration through final restoration
or distribution.



"Hongik Ingan" - To broadly benefit humanity.

The CRYO-ASSET standard embodies this principle by ensuring that the
assets of preserved individuals are protected and managed responsibly,
serving both the individual's future interests and the broader good
of their families and communities.
