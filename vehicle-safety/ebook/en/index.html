Vehicle Safety: The Complete Technical Guide

Comprehensive Standards for Modern Vehicle Safety Systems

About This Book

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA-AUTO-022 Vehicle Safety standard, providing detailed technical specifications, implementation guidelines, and best practices for automotive safety systems. From active safety technologies like AEB and ESC to passive protection mechanisms including airbags and structural design, this book serves as the definitive reference for automotive engineers, safety professionals, and certification authorities.

弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) - Every standard, every test, every requirement in this book is designed with one goal: to save lives and reduce injuries through advanced vehicle safety technology.
