# WIA-PET-009 Pet Cloning Standard v1.1

**Version**: 1.1
**Release Date**: 2023-06-01
**Status**: Superseded by v2.0

## Summary

Version 1.1 adds comprehensive cryopreservation protocols and enhances quality control measures based on 18 months of industry feedback and implementation experience.

## Key Additions in v1.1

### Cryopreservation Protocols
- Controlled-rate freezing procedures
- Long-term storage requirements  
- Post-thaw viability assessment
- Quality control for frozen samples

### Enhanced Quality Control
- Mouse Embryo Assay (MEA) introduced
- Equipment calibration schedules  
- Statistical process control
- Contamination screening improved

### Animal Welfare Enhancements
- Oocyte collection limits: Maximum 6 per year
- Minimum 60-day rest periods between collections
- Retirement requirements for donor animals
- Enhanced pain management protocols

### Success Metrics
- Tracking requirements for all stages
- Blastocyst formation rate targets established
- Pregnancy rate documentation required

**Version 2.0** (2025-01-15): Current version
