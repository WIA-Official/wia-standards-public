## Technical Notes

1. **IPA Accuracy**: IPA transcriptions are approximate and may vary by dialect
2. **Tone Markers**: Tones are represented using suprasegmental IPA notation
3. **Stress Patterns**: Primary (ЛҲ) and secondary (ЛҢ) stress marked where applicable
4. **Phonemic vs Phonetic**: Some transcriptions are phonemic, others phonetic
5. **Universal Coverage**: ANY language can be represented through IPA вҶ’ WIA Braille
