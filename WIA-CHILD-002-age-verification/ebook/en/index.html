About This Standard

This standard is developed by WIA (World Certification Industry Association) as an international standard for protecting children and youth in digital environments. It provides comprehensive guidelines to ensure children's safety online and deliver healthy digital experiences.

This standard was developed through collaboration with child protection experts, technology companies, and government agencies worldwide, reflecting recommendations from international organizations such as UNICEF and ITU.
