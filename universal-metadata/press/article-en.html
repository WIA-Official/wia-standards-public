Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-CORE-008, a universal metadata framework that describes and enriches all types of data, as free and open source under MIT License. This announcement positions the Republic of Korea as a leader in data interoperability standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Over 80% of global data is stored without consistent metadata. Researchers waste an average of 30% of their time searching for data, and even when found, most data cannot be verified for reliability, provenance, or quality. We have data but can't find it, understand it, or trust it—this situation persists.

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum, CEO:
"Without data, AI is useless. But without metadata, data is useless. In healthcare, finance, science, and IoT, data abounds, but if we don't know what it is, where it came from, or how trustworthy it is, it's worthless. WIA-CORE-008 solves this problem."

WIA's Solution: WIA-CORE-008 Universal Metadata

WIA-CORE-008 defines a comprehensive metadata framework capable of describing data across all domains including healthcare, finance, science, IoT, and media. It includes automated quality assessment, version tracking, provenance management, and privacy controls, with an extensible structure supporting domain-specific custom fields.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, and more—all released as free and open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are built in Rust—the systems programming language that achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.

👤 About the CEO

Dr. Yeon Sam-Heum is the world's only one-person standards architect who has single-handedly built infrastructure for over 60 international standards in collaboration with AI, and is recognized as a living legend in the lifelong education field.
