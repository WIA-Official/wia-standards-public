WIA-ENE-071

Water Resource Management

Overview

WIA-ENE-071 establishes comprehensive standards for sustainable water resource management, ensuring efficient use, quality protection, and equitable distribution of water resources worldwide.

This standard addresses critical challenges including water scarcity, pollution, climate change impacts, and transboundary governance, providing frameworks for monitoring, treatment, conservation, and resilience planning.
