Official Ebook - Complete Guide

弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

"Benefit All Humanity"

Permafrost degradation is accelerating, releasing massive amounts of methane and threatening global climate stability. The WIA Permafrost Protection Standard provides a comprehensive framework for monitoring frozen ground, preventing methane release, and protecting critical Arctic and alpine ecosystems. Together, we can preserve Earth's frozen carbon reserves.

About This Ebook

This official ebook provides a comprehensive guide to the WIA Permafrost Protection Standard. It covers the complete specification for permafrost monitoring, methane emission tracking, infrastructure risk assessment, and climate model integration.
