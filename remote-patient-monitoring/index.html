Core Features

⌚

Wearable Integration

Seamlessly connect with FDA-approved wearable devices to collect continuous health data including heart rate, blood pressure, glucose levels, oxygen saturation, and activity metrics.

🚨

Real-time Alerts

Intelligent alert system that notifies healthcare providers and caregivers of critical health events, threshold violations, and concerning trends requiring immediate attention.

📱

Patient Dashboard

User-friendly mobile and web dashboards providing patients with real-time visibility into their health metrics, medication schedules, and progress toward wellness goals.

🧬

Chronic Disease Management

Specialized monitoring protocols for diabetes, hypertension, heart failure, COPD, and other chronic conditions with evidence-based care pathways and intervention strategies.

📈

Predictive Analytics

AI-powered analytics to identify early warning signs, predict adverse events, and enable proactive interventions before conditions worsen or hospitalization becomes necessary.

💰

Reimbursement Support

Automated documentation and billing integration supporting CPT codes 99453-99458, 99091, and other RPM reimbursement codes for Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payers.

Use Cases

💙

Heart Failure Management

Monitor daily weight, blood pressure, and symptoms to detect early signs of decompensation. Reduce hospital readmissions by 30-50% through proactive intervention and medication adjustments.

🩺

Diabetes Care

Continuous glucose monitoring with automated insulin delivery, carb counting assistance, and hypoglycemia alerts. Improve A1C levels and reduce severe hypoglycemic events.

🏥

Post-Surgical Recovery

Track vital signs, pain levels, mobility, and wound healing after hospital discharge. Enable early detection of complications while reducing unnecessary follow-up visits.

👴

Aging in Place

Support elderly patients living independently with fall detection, medication reminders, and continuous health monitoring. Provide peace of mind for family caregivers.

🫀

Hypertension Control

Daily blood pressure monitoring with lifestyle coaching and medication titration. Achieve better BP control rates compared to traditional in-office management.
