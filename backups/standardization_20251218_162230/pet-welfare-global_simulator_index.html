1.3 Certification Tiers

Tier Requirements:
==================

BRONZE (Entry Level):
  - Five Freedoms Score: >= 60%
  - No critical non-conformities
  - Basic facility standards met
  - Valid for 1 year

SILVER (Standard):
  - Five Freedoms Score: >= 75%
  - No major non-conformities
  - Enhanced welfare practices
  - Valid for 2 years

GOLD (Advanced):
  - Five Freedoms Score: >= 85%
  - Exceeds minimum standards
  - Proactive welfare programs
  - Valid for 3 years

PLATINUM (Excellence):
  - Five Freedoms Score: >= 95%
  - Industry leadership
  - Innovation in welfare
  - Valid for 3 years + Ambassador status
