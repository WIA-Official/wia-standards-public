[i] About This Simulator

This simulator demonstrates the WIA LLM Interoperability Standard, enabling different AI models to communicate, collaborate, and form federations for complex tasks.

Three Core Standards:

  • WIA-LLM-CAPABILITY - AI declares its abilities
  • WIA-LLM-MESSAGE - AI-to-AI messaging protocol
  • WIA-LLM-FEDERATION - Multi-AI collaboration framework
