[i] About This Simulator

This simulator demonstrates the WIA Cryopreservation Standard, defining technical protocols for biological preservation at ultra-low temperatures.

Key Concepts:

  • Vitrification - Glass-state preservation without ice crystals
  • CPA - Cryoprotective agents preventing cell damage
  • VQI - Vitrification Quality Index for assessment
  • LN2 - Liquid nitrogen storage at -196C
