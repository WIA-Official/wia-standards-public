# WIA-PET-008 Pet Tracking Standard - Specification v2.0

**Version:** 2.0.0  
**Date:** 2025-12-25  
**Major Release:** AI-powered features, blockchain integration, global expansion

## Major Changes in v2.0

### AI and Machine Learning
- Behavior prediction and anomaly detection
- Health risk assessment and early disease detection
- Personalized recommendations for activity and care
- Image recognition for pet identification
- Natural language interface for queries

### Blockchain Integration
- Decentralized pet identity and ownership records
- Immutable medical history and vaccination records
- Smart contracts for automated pet services
- NFT-based pet profiles and achievements

### Advanced Positioning
- UWB (Ultra-Wideband) for centimeter-level accuracy
- 5G connectivity for real-time video streaming
- Satellite communication for remote areas
- AR-assisted pet finding with visual guidance

### Global Features
- Support for 200+ countries and territories
- Multi-language support (50+ languages)
- Regional compliance (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL, etc.)
- Cross-border pet travel tracking

### Environmental Sustainability
- Solar charging integration
- Energy harvesting from pet movement
- Carbon-neutral tracking with offset programs
- Recyclable and biodegradable device materials
