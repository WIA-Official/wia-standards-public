Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released WIA-TIME-032, systematically controlling time travel access, as free and open source under MIT License. It provides a complete time access control framework covering multi-factor authentication, hierarchical permissions, and protected era restrictions.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

If anyone can time travel, history is threatened. The Kennedy assassination site, WWII, moments of scientific discovery—these critical historical events must be protected from unauthorized access. Without access control, timeline collapse will occur.

WIA's Solution: WIA-TIME-032 Time Access Control

WIA-TIME-032 is an international standard for safely managing time travel access. It provides a complete access control system covering 5-tier access levels, multi-factor authentication, protected era classification, and real-time auditing.
