Chapter 8: Certification & Compliance

8.1 Certification Levels

The WIA AI Afterlife Ethics Standard defines three certification levels for organizations providing digital afterlife services.

Level Name Requirements Allowed Services
Level 1 Memorial Provider Basic ethics training, data protection Static memorials only
Level 2 Interactive Provider Level 1 + psychology consultation Interactive bots, avatars
Level 3 Consciousness Custodian Level 2 + ethics board oversight Full consciousness services

8.2 Core Certification Requirements

Ethics & Governance Requirements

  • Established ethics review board with external members
  • Documented ethics policy publicly available
  • Annual ethics training for all staff
  • Clear escalation procedures for ethics concerns
  • Published appeal process for affected parties
  • Regular ethics impact assessments
  • Transparency reports published quarterly

Consent Management Requirements

  • Multi-witness consent verification system
  • Consent document integrity verification (cryptographic)
  • Support for consent revocation at any time
  • Clear scope definitions in all consent documents
  • Periodic consent revalidation (for living subjects)
  • Proxy consent limitations properly enforced
  • Time-limited consent options available

Technical Security Requirements

  • AES-256 or equivalent encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3+ for all data in transit
  • Hardware Security Module (HSM) for key management
  • Multi-factor authentication for all access
  • Immutable audit logs with blockchain anchoring
  • Regular penetration testing (at least annually)
  • Incident response plan with 24-hour notification
  • Data residency compliance with local regulations

Operational Requirements

  • 24/7 monitoring for ethics violations
  • Personality drift detection and alerting
  • Abuse prevention systems active
  • Grief counseling resources available
  • Session time limits enforced
  • Regular integrity checks on consciousness data
  • Backup and disaster recovery tested quarterly

Critical Unauthorized resurrection, consent forgery Immediate Certification revocation
High Data breach, identity theft 24 hours Suspension pending review
Medium Policy violations, process failures 7 days Corrective action required
Low Documentation gaps, minor issues 30 days Improvement plan

8.6 Certification Checklist

Pre-Certification: Organizations must complete all items in this checklist before applying for WIA AI Afterlife Ethics certification.

Final Certification Checklist

  • Ethics review board established and operational
  • All staff completed ethics training
  • Consent management system fully operational
  • Security infrastructure meets all requirements
  • Audit logging and monitoring active
  • Incident response procedures documented and tested
  • Transparency report published
  • Third-party security audit completed
  • Ethics impact assessment documented
  • Appeal process established and publicized
  • Data residency requirements confirmed
  • Insurance coverage for digital afterlife services
  • Legal compliance review completed
  • User documentation and consent forms reviewed
  • Emergency shutdown procedures documented
Certification Process: Submit your completed documentation to the WIA AI Afterlife Ethics Certification Board. Review typically takes 4-6 weeks for Level 1, 8-12 weeks for Level 2 and 3.
