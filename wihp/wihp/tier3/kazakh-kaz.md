# WIHP: Kazakh (Қазақша) - kaz

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Қазақ тілі / Qazaq tili
- **ISO 639-3:** kaz
- **Speaker Population:** ~13 million
- **Language Family:** Turkic → Kipchak → Kipchak-Nogai
- **Writing System:** Cyrillic, Latin (new), Arabic (historical)
- **Phonological Type:** Agglutinative, vowel harmony

## Phonological Features
- **Vowel Harmony:** Front/back distinction
- **9 Vowels:** Including front rounded ö, ü
- **Consonant Assimilation:** Extensive
- **Stress:** Generally on final syllable

## Phonological Rules

### Vowel Harmony
Front/back vowel harmony affects suffixes:
- Back: а, о, ұ, ы
- Front: ә, ө, ү, і, е

### Consonant Assimilation
Extensive voicing and place assimilation in suffixes.

### Uvular Consonants
Distinctive қ /q/ and ғ /ɣ/ common in Turkic.

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Front Rounded:** ө→ㅚ, ү→ㅟ
2. **Uvular:** қ→ㅋ, ғ→ㄱ
3. **Velar Nasal:** ң→ㅇ
4. **Central Vowel:** ы→ㅡ
5. **Harmony:** Affects suffix vowels
