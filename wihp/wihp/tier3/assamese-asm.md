# Assamese (অসমীয়া) - WIHP A-Grade Mapping

## Language Information
- **Language**: Assamese (অসমীয়া)
- **ISO 639-3**: asm
- **Region**: Assam, Northeast India
- **Speakers**: ~15 million
- **Script**: Eastern Nagari (Assamese script)
- **WIHP Tier**: 3

## Phonological Features
- Unique vowel system with 8 oral and 4 nasal vowels
- Breathy voiced stops (voiced aspirates)
- Rich sibilant inventory
- Distinctive /x/ (velar fricative) not found in other Indo-Aryan languages
- Vowel harmony patterns
- Final vowel deletion in casual speech

## Standard 50 Sentences

### Basic Communication (1-10)
1. **নমস্কাৰ** /nɔmɔskaɹ/ → 노모스카르 "Hello"
2. **আপুনি কেনে আছে?** /apuni kene ase/ → 아푸니 케네 아세 "How are you?"
3. **মই ভালে আছো** /mɔi bʱale asɔ/ → 모이 발레 아소 "I am fine"
4. **ধন্যবাদ** /dʱɔnjɔbad/ → 돈요바드 "Thank you"
5. **অনুগ্ৰহ কৰি** /ɔnugɹɔhɔ kɔɹi/ → 오누그로호 코리 "Please"
6. **মাফ কৰিব** /mapʰ kɔɹib/ → 마프 코리브 "Sorry"
7. **হয়** /hɔj/ → 호이 "Yes"
8. **নহয়** /nɔhɔj/ → 노호이 "No"
9. **পুনৰ লগ পাম** /punɔɹ lɔg pam/ → 푸노르 로그 팜 "See you again"
10. **শুভ ৰাতিপুৱা** /subʱ ɹatipuwa/ → 수브 라티푸와 "Good morning"

### Daily Life (11-20)
11. **মোৰ নাম ৰাম** /mɔɹ nam ɹam/ → 모르 남 람 "My name is Ram"
12. **মই অসমীয়া** /mɔi ɔsɔmija/ → 모이 오소미야 "I am Assamese"
13. **এইটো কি?** /eitɔ ki/ → 에이토 키 "What is this?"
14. **পানী দিয়ক** /pani dijɔk/ → 파니 디욕 "Give me water"
15. **মই ভোকত আছো** /mɔi bʱɔkɔt asɔ/ → 모이 보콧 아소 "I am hungry"
16. **আজি বতৰ ভাল** /azi bɔtɔɹ bʱal/ → 아지 보토르 발 "The weather is good today"
17. **মই শুবলৈ যাওঁ** /mɔi sublɔi zaõ/ → 모이 수블로이 자오 "I am going to sleep"
18. **সময় কিমান?** /sɔmɔj kiman/ → 소모이 키만 "What time is it?"
19. **মোক সহায় কৰক** /mɔk sɔhaj kɔɹɔk/ → 목 소하이 코록 "Help me"
20. **মই বুজি পালো** /mɔi buzi palɔ/ → 모이 부지 팔로 "I understood"

### Questions and Directions (21-30)
21. **বজাৰ ক'ত?** /bɔzaɹ kɔt/ → 보자르 콧 "Where is the market?"
22. **ইয়াৰ দাম কিমান?** /ijaɹ dam kiman/ → 이야르 담 키만 "How much is this?"
23. **আপুনি ক'ৰ পৰা আহিছে?** /apuni kɔɹ pɔɹa ahise/ → 아푸니 코르 포라 아히세 "Where are you from?"
24. **গুৱাহাটী কিমান দূৰ?** /guwahaʈi kiman duɹ/ → 구와하티 키만 두르 "How far is Guwahati?"
25. **সোঁফালে যাওক** /sõpʰale zaɔk/ → 솜팔레 자옥 "Go right"
26. **বাওঁফালে ঘূৰক** /baõpʰale gʱuɹɔk/ → 바옴팔레 구록 "Turn left"
27. **চিকিৎসালয় ক'ত?** /tsikitsalɔj kɔt/ → 치키차로이 콧 "Where is the hospital?"
28. **এইটো সঁচা নে?** /eitɔ sõsa ne/ → 에이토 손사 네 "Is this true?"
29. **আপুনি অসমীয়া জানে নে?** /apuni ɔsɔmija zane ne/ → 아푸니 오소미야 자네 네 "Do you know Assamese?"
30. **মই হেৰাই গ'লো** /mɔi heɹai gɔlɔ/ → 모이 헤라이 골로 "I am lost"

### Bihu Festival and Culture (31-40)
31. **বিহুৰ শুভেচ্ছা** /bihuɹ subʱetsa/ → 비후르 수베차 "Happy Bihu"
32. **ব'হাগ বিহু আহিছে** /bɔhag bihu ahise/ → 보하그 비후 아히세 "Bohag Bihu has come"
33. **বিহু নাচ সুন্দৰ** /bihu nats sundɔɹ/ → 비후 나츠 순도르 "Bihu dance is beautiful"
34. **জাপি পিন্ধক** /zapi pindʱɔk/ → 자피 핀독 "Wear the japi (traditional hat)"
35. **গামোচা লওক** /gamɔtsa lɔɔk/ → 가모차 로옥 "Take the gamosa (traditional cloth)"
36. **পিঠা খাওক** /pitʱa kʰaɔk/ → 피타 카옥 "Eat pitha (rice cake)"
37. **ঢোল বজোৱা** /ɖʱɔl bɔzɔwa/ → 돌 보조와 "Play the dhol (drum)"
38. **পেঁপা বাওক** /pẽpa baɔk/ → 펨파 바옥 "Play the pepa (horn)"
39. **গগনা বজাওক** /gɔgɔna bɔzaɔk/ → 고고나 보자옥 "Play the gogona (bamboo instrument)"
40. **হুঁচৰি গাওক** /hũsɔɹi gaɔk/ → 훈소리 가옥 "Sing husori (Bihu song)"

### Advanced Expressions (41-50)
41. **এখন ভাল কিতাপ** /ekʰɔn bʱal kitap/ → 에콘 발 키탑 "A good book"
42. **চাহ খাব নে?** /tsah kʰab ne/ → 차흐 카브 네 "Will you have tea?"
43. **অসমৰ প্ৰাকৃতিক সৌন্দৰ্য অপূৰ্ব** /ɔsɔmɔɹ pɹakɹitik sɔundɔɹjɔ ɔpuɹbɔ/ → 오소모르 프라크리틱 소운도료 오푸르보 "Assam's natural beauty is wonderful"
44. **এক নম্বৰ ৰঙালী বিহু** /ek nɔmbɔɹ ɹɔŋali bihu/ → 에크 놈보르 롱알리 비후 "Rongali Bihu is number one"
45. **চিৰিসাহৰীয়া মাছ ভাল** /tsiɹisahɔɹija mats bʱal/ → 치리사호리야 마츠 발 "Chirisahariya fish is good"
46. **মাজুলী সুন্দৰ দ্বীপ** /mazuli sundɔɹ dwip/ → 마줄리 순도르 드윕 "Majuli is a beautiful island"
47. **কাজিৰঙা ৰাষ্ট্ৰীয় উদ্যান** /kaziɹɔŋa ɹaʂʈɹijɔ udjɔn/ → 카지롱아 라스트리요 우됸 "Kaziranga National Park"
48. **এশিঙীয়া গঁড় বিখ্যাত** /esiŋija gõɽ bikʰjat/ → 에싱이야 곤드 비캬트 "One-horned rhino is famous"
49. **মই অসমক ভাল পাওঁ** /mɔi ɔsɔmɔk bʱal paõ/ → 모이 오소목 발 파옴 "I love Assam"
50. **জয় আই অসম** /zɔj ai ɔsɔm/ → 조이 아이 오솜 "Victory to Mother Assam"

## Phonological Rules
1. **Velar fricative /x/**: Unique to Assamese among Indo-Aryan languages
2. **Final vowel reduction**: Word-final vowels often reduced in casual speech
3. **Breathy voice**: Voiced aspirates (bʱ, dʱ, gʱ) are distinctive
4. **Sibilant mergers**: Historical sibilants merged to /s/ and /x/
5. **Assimilation**: Consonants assimilate in clusters
6. **Nasal vowels**: Contrastive in minimal pairs
7. **Tone-like pitch**: Some words distinguish meaning by pitch
8. **Vowel harmony**: Back vowels tend to co-occur
