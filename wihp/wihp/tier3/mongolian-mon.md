# WIHP: Mongolian (Монгол хэл) - mon

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Монгол хэл (Mongol khel)
- **ISO 639-3:** mon
- **Speaker Population:** ~5 million
- **Language Family:** Mongolic
- **Writing System:** Cyrillic (official), Traditional Mongolian script
- **Phonological Type:** Agglutinative, vowel harmony

## Phonological Features
- **Vowel Harmony:** Pharyngeal/neutral distinction
- **Long Vowels:** Phonemic
- **Palatalization:** Before front vowels
- **Final Consonants:** Common

## Standard Sentences (50 Sentences)

## Phonological Rules

### Vowel Harmony
Back vowels (а, о, у) vs front vowels (э, ө, ү) must match in suffixes.

### Long Vowels
Vowel length is phonemic and written with double letters.

### Final Devoicing
Voiced consonants may devoice word-finally.
