# WIHP: Irish (Gaeilge) - gle

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Gaeilge
- **ISO 639-3:** gle
- **Speaker Population:** ~1.7 million (L1+L2)
- **Language Family:** Indo-European → Celtic → Goidelic
- **Writing System:** Latin alphabet (with síneadh fada)
- **Phonological Type:** Initial mutations, broad/slender distinction

## Phonological Features
- **Broad vs Slender:** Consonants palatalized or velarized
- **Initial Mutation:** Lenition and eclipsis
- **Vowel Length:** Marked with fada (á, é, í, ó, ú)
- **Three Dialects:** Munster, Connacht, Ulster

## Standard Sentences (50 Sentences)

### Basic Communication (1-10)
1. **Dia duit.** /dʲiə dɪtʲ/
   - 디아 딧
   - "Hello." (God to you)

2. **Conas atá tú?** /ˈkɔnəsˠ əˈtˠaː tˠuː/
   - 코나스 아타 투
   - "How are you?"

3. **Tá mé go maith.** /tˠaː mʲeː gɔ mˠah/
   - 타 메 고 마
   - "I'm fine."

4. **Go raibh maith agat.** /gɔ ɾˠo mˠah ˈagətˠ/
   - 고 러브 마 아갓
   - "Thank you."

5. **Sea.** /ʃa/
   - 샤
   - "Yes."

6. **Ní hea.** /nʲiː ha/
   - 니 하
   - "No."

7. **Tá brón orm.** /tˠaː bˠɾˠoːn ɔɾˠəmˠ/
   - 타 브론 오름
   - "I'm sorry."

8. **Le do thoil.** /lʲə dɔ hɔlʲ/
   - 레 도 홀
   - "Please."

9. **Ní thuigim.** /nʲiː ˈhɪɟɪmʲ/
   - 니 히김
   - "I don't understand."

10. **An bhfuil Béarla agat?** /ə wɪlʲ ˈbʲeːɾˠlˠə ˈagətˠ/
    - 안 윌 베를라 아갓
    - "Do you speak English?"

### Personal Information (11-20)
11. **Cad is ainm duit?** /kad ɪsˠ ˈanʲɪmʲ dɪtʲ/
    - 카드 이스 아님 딧
    - "What is your name?"

12. **... is ainm dom.** /... ɪsˠ ˈanʲɪmʲ dɔmˠ/
    - ... 이스 아님 돔
    - "My name is..."

13. **Cad as duit?** /kad asˠ dɪtʲ/
    - 카드 아스 딧
    - "Where are you from?"

14. **Is as Éirinn dom.** /ɪsˠ asˠ ˈeːɾʲɪnʲ dɔmˠ/
    - 이스 아스 에린 돔
    - "I'm from Ireland."

15. **Cén aois thú?** /keːn iːʃ huː/
    - 겐 이시 후
    - "How old are you?"

16. **Tá mé tríocha bliain d'aois.** /tˠaː mʲeː ˈtʲɾʲiːxə bʲlʲiənʲ diːʃ/
    - 타 메 트리하 블리안 디시
    - "I am thirty years old."

17. **Cad é do phost?** /kad eː dɔ fˠɔsˠtˠ/
    - 카드 에 도 포스트
    - "What is your job?"

18. **Is múinteoir mé.** /ɪsˠ ˈmuːnʲtʲoːɾʲ mʲeː/
    - 이스 문토르 메
    - "I am a teacher."

19. **Cá bhfuil cónaí ort?** /kaː wɪlʲ ˈkoːnˠiː ɔɾˠtˠ/
    - 카 윌 코니 오르트
    - "Where do you live?"

20. **Tá cónaí orm i mBaile Átha Cliath.** /tˠaː ˈkoːnˠiː ɔɾˠəmˠ ɪ mˠalʲə aː klʲiə/
    - 타 코니 오름 이 발랴 아 클리아
    - "I live in Dublin."

### Daily Life (21-30)
21. **Cén t-am é?** /keːn tˠamˠ eː/
    - 겐 탐 에
    - "What time is it?"

22. **Cén lá atá ann inniu?** /keːn lˠaː əˈtˠaː ɔn ɪˈnʲuː/
    - 겐 라 아타 안 이뉴
    - "What day is it today?"

23. **Tá an aimsir go breá inniu.** /tˠaː ən ˈamʲʃɪɾʲ gɔ bʲɾʲaː ɪˈnʲuː/
    - 타 안 암시르 고 브랴 이뉴
    - "The weather is nice today."

24. **Tá ocras orm.** /tˠaː ˈɔkɾˠəsˠ ɔɾˠəmˠ/
    - 타 오크라스 오름
    - "I'm hungry."

25. **Tá tart orm.** /tˠaː tˠaɾˠtˠ ɔɾˠəmˠ/
    - 타 타르트 오름
    - "I'm thirsty."

26. **Tá tuirse orm.** /tˠaː ˈtˠɪɾʲʃə ɔɾˠəmˠ/
    - 타 트르셔 오름
    - "I'm tired."

27. **Cá bhfuil an leithreas?** /kaː wɪlʲ ən ˈlʲehɾʲəsˠ/

    - "Where is the bathroom?"

28. **Cé mhéad a chosnaíonn sé?** /keː vʲeːd ə xɔsˠnˠiːnˠ ʃeː/

    - "How much does it cost?"

29. **Tá sé daor.** /tˠaː ʃeː deːɾˠ/
    - 타 셰 데르
    - "It's expensive."

30. **Tá sé saor.** /tˠaː ʃeː sˠeːɾˠ/
    - 타 셰 세르
    - "It's cheap."

### Travel & Directions (31-40)
31. **Cá bhfuil an stáisiún?** /kaː wɪlʲ ən ˈsˠtˠaːʃuːnˠ/
    - 카 윌 안 스타션
    - "Where is the station?"

32. **Conas a théim go dtí an baile?** /ˈkɔnəsˠ ə heːmʲ gɔ dʲiː ən ˈbalʲə/
    - 코나스 아 헴 고 디 안 발랴
    - "How do I get to town?"

33. **Téigh díreach ar aghaidh.** /tʲeː dʲiːɾʲəx ɛɾˠ ˈəiː/
    - 테 디라흐 에르 아이
    - "Go straight ahead."

34. **Cas ar chlé.** /kasˠ ɛɾˠ xlʲeː/
    - 카스 에르 흘레
    - "Turn left."

35. **Cas ar dheis.** /kasˠ ɛɾˠ jɛʃ/
    - 카스 에르 예시
    - "Turn right."

36. **An bhfuil sé i bhfad?** /ə wɪlʲ ʃeː ɪ wadˠ/
    - 안 윌 셰 이 와드
    - "Is it far?"

37. **Tá sé gar.** /tˠaː ʃeː gaɾˠ/
    - 타 셰 가르
    - "It's close."

38. **Ba mhaith liom ticéad.** /bə wah lʲʊmˠ tʲɪˈceːdˠ/
    - 바 와 룸 티게드
    - "I would like a ticket."

39. **Cén uair a fhágann an traein?** /keːn uəɾʲ ə ˈaːgənˠ ən tˠɾˠenʲ/
    - 겐 우어르 아 아간 안 트렌
    - "When does the train leave?"

40. **Cá bhfuil mé ag tuirlingt?** /kaː wɪlʲ mʲeː əg ˈtˠɪɾʲlʲɪŋʲtʲ/
    - 카 윌 메 아그 트를린트
    - "Where do I get off?"

### Social & Cultural (41-50)
41. **Sláinte!** /ˈsˠlˠaːnʲtʲə/

    - "Cheers!" (Health)

42. **Lá breithe shona duit!** /lˠaː ˈbʲɾʲɛhə ˈhɔnˠə dɪtʲ/

    - "Happy birthday!"

    - 아블리안 피 와샤 딧
    - "Happy New Year!"

    - 타 그라 아감 딧
    - "I love you."

46. **Tá cumha orm i do dhiaidh.** /tˠaː ˈkuːwə ɔɾˠəmˠ ɪ dɔ ˈjiːj/
    - 타 쿠와 오름 이 도 이
    - "I miss you."

47. **Ádh mór!** /aː mˠoːɾˠ/
    - 아 모르
    - "Good luck!"

48. **Slán.** /sˠlˠaːnˠ/
    - 슬란
    - "Goodbye."

49. **Oíche mhaith.** /ˈiːçə wah/
    - 이햐 와
    - "Good night."

50. **Feicfidh mé arís thú.** /ˈfʲɛcɪ mʲeː əˈɾʲiːʃ huː/
    - 페키 메 아리시 후
    - "See you again."

## Initial Mutations

### Lenition (Séimhiú) - adds 'h'
| Original | Lenited |
|----------|---------|
| b → bh | m → mh |
| c → ch | p → ph |
| d → dh | s → sh |
| f → fh | t → th |
| g → gh | - |

### Eclipsis (Urú) - prefixes
| Original | Eclipsed |
|----------|---------|
| b → mb | f → bhf |
| c → gc | g → ng |
| d → nd | p → bp |
| t → dt | - |

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Broad/Slender:** Same Hangul (palatalization subtle)
2. **Lenited Consonants:** bh/mh→ㅂ (v/w sound)
3. **Ch:** /x/ or /ç/ → ㅎ
4. **Long Vowels:** Same Hangul (fada not marked)
5. **Eclipsis:** Write both consonants
