# WIHP: Pashto (پښتو) - pus

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** پښتو (Pashto)
- **ISO 639-3:** pus
- **Speaker Population:** ~50 million
- **Language Family:** Indo-European → Iranian → Eastern Iranian
- **Writing System:** Arabic script (Pashto variant)
- **Phonological Type:** SOV word order, retroflex consonants

## Phonological Features
- **Retroflex Consonants:** ṭ, ḍ, ṇ, ṛ
- **Stress:** Phonemic and unpredictable
- **Gender System:** Masculine and feminine
- **Rich Consonant System:** Including retroflex series

## Standard Sentences (50 Sentences)

### Basic Communication (1-10)
1. **سلام** /saˈlɑm/
   - 살람
   - "Hello."

2. **څنګه یې?** /tsəŋˈga je/
   - 층가 예
   - "How are you?"

3. **ښه یم.** /ʂa jəm/
   - 샤 임
   - "I'm fine."

4. **مننه.** /manaˈna/
   - 마나나
   - "Thank you."

5. **هو.** /ho/
   - 호
   - "Yes."

6. **نه.** /na/
   - 나
   - "No."

7. **بخښنه غواړم.** /baxʂaˈna ɣwɑˈɺəm/
   - 박샤나 과름
   - "I'm sorry."

8. **مهرباني وکړئ.** /mehrbaˈni wəˈkɺəj/
   - 메흐르바니 워크레이
   - "Please."

9. **زه نه پوهېږم.** /za na poheˈʐəm/
   - 자 나 포헤즘
   - "I don't understand."

10. **انګلیسي ویلی شې?** /ɪŋgliˈsi wajˈlaj ʂe/
    - 잉글리시 와일라이 셰
    - "Can you speak English?"

## Phonological Rules

### Retroflex Consonants
Distinctive retroflex series: ṭ /ʈ/, ḍ /ɖ/, ṇ /ɳ/, ṛ /ɺ/.

### Unique Affricates
Pashto has ts /ts/ and dz /dz/ written with specific letters څ and ځ.

### Stress
Stress is phonemic and unpredictable, affecting meaning.

### Consonant Clusters
Complex clusters possible word-initially.

---

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Retroflex:** ṭ→ㅌ, ḍ→ㄷ, ṇ→ㄴ, ṛ→ㄹ
2. **Affricates:** څ→ㅊ (ts), ځ→ㅈ (dz)
3. **Fricatives:** خ→ㅎ, غ→ㄱ
4. **Schwa:** Map to ㅓ
5. **Stress:** Not marked in Hangul
