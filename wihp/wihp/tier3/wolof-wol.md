# WIHP: Wolof (Wolof) - wol

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Wolof
- **ISO 639-3:** wol
- **Speaker Population:** ~10 million
- **Language Family:** Niger-Congo → Atlantic → Senegambian
- **Writing System:** Latin alphabet (Wolofal Arabic script historically)
- **Phonological Type:** Noun class system, consonant harmony

## Phonological Features
- **Noun Classes:** 10+ grammatical classes
- **Consonant Harmony:** ATR-based
- **Gemination:** Phonemic consonant length
- **Long Vowels:** Phonemic length distinction

## Standard Sentences (50 Sentences)

### Basic Communication (1-10)
1. **Na nga def?** /na ŋa dɛf/
   - 나 응아 데프
   - "Hello, how are you?"

2. **Jàmm rekk.** /ɟamm rɛkk/
   - 잠 레크
   - "I'm fine." (peace only)

3. **Jërëjëf.** /ɟɛrɛɟɛf/
   - 제레제프
   - "Thank you."

4. **Waaw.** /waːw/
   - 와우
   - "Yes."

5. **Déet.** /deːt/
   - 데트
   - "No."

6. **Baal ma.** /baːl ma/
   - 발 마
   - "Sorry."

7. **Baal ma ak sunu...** /baːl ma ak sunu/
   - 발 마 악 수누
   - "Please."

8. **Dégguma.** /degguma/
   - 데구마
   - "I don't understand."

9. **Ndax dégg nga Angale?** /ndax dɛgg ŋa aŋgalɛ/
   - 은닥스 데그 응아 앙갈레
   - "Do you speak English?"

10. **Salaam aleekum.** /salaːm alɛːkum/
    - 살람 알레쿰
    - "Peace be upon you." (Islamic greeting)

### Personal Information (11-20)
11. **Noo tudd?** /noː tudd/
    - 노 투드
    - "What is your name?"

12. **Maa ngi tudd...** /maː ŋgi tudd/
    - 마 응기 투드
    - "My name is..."

13. **Fan nga jóge?** /fan ŋa ɟoːgɛ/
    - 판 응아 조게
    - "Where are you from?"

14. **Maa ngi jóge Sénégal.** /maː ŋgi ɟoːgɛ seneɡal/
    - 마 응기 조게 세네갈
    - "I'm from Senegal."

15. **Ñaata at ngay am?** /ɲaːta at ŋaj am/
    - 냐타 아트 응아이 암
    - "How old are you?"

16. **Maa ngi am ñaar-fukki at.** /maː ŋgi am ɲaːr fukki at/
    - 마 응기 암 냐르 푸키 아트
    - "I am thirty years old."

17. **Lu ngay liggéey?** /lu ŋaj liggeːj/
    - 루 응아이 리게이
    - "What do you do?"

18. **Jàngalekat laa.** /ɟaŋgalɛkat laː/
    - 장갈레카트 라
    - "I am a teacher."

19. **Fan nga dëkk?** /fan ŋa dɛkk/
    - 판 응아 덱
    - "Where do you live?"

20. **Maa ngi dëkk Dakar.** /maː ŋgi dɛkk dakaːr/
    - 마 응기 덱 다카르
    - "I live in Dakar."

### Daily Life (21-30)
21. **Ban waxtu la?** /ban waχtu la/
    - 반 와흐투 라
    - "What time is it?"

22. **Ban bés la tey?** /ban beːs la tɛj/
    - 반 베스 라 테이
    - "What day is today?"

23. **Wéeru bi rafet na tey.** /weːru bi rafɛt na tɛj/
    - 웨루 비 라페트 나 테이
    - "The weather is nice today."

24. **Xiif naa.** /xiːf naː/
    - 히프 나
    - "I'm hungry."

25. **Mar naa.** /mar naː/
    - 마르 나
    - "I'm thirsty."

26. **Sonn naa.** /sonn naː/
    - 손 나
    - "I'm tired."

27. **Ana wanag wi?** /ana wanag wi/
    - 아나 와나그 위
    - "Where is the bathroom?"

28. **Ñaata lay jar?** /ɲaːta laj ɟar/
    - 냐타 라이 자르
    - "How much does it cost?"

29. **Seer na.** /seːr na/
    - 세르 나
    - "It's expensive."

30. **Yomb na.** /jomb na/
    - 욤브 나
    - "It's cheap."

### Travel & Directions (31-40)
31. **Ana gaar gi?** /ana gaːr gi/
    - 아나 가르 기
    - "Where is the station?"

32. **Naka laa dem ci dëkk bi?** /naka laː dɛm tʃi dɛkk bi/
    - 나카 라 뎀 치 덱 비
    - "How do I get to the city?"

33. **Jëm sa kanam.** /ɟɛm sa kanam/
    - 젬 사 카남
    - "Go straight."

34. **Wëlbati ci càmmoñ.** /wɛlbati tʃi tʃammoɲ/
    - 웰바티 치 참몬
    - "Turn left."

35. **Wëlbati ci ndeyjoor.** /wɛlbati tʃi ndɛjɟoːr/
    - 웰바티 치 은데이조르
    - "Turn right."

36. **Sori na?** /sori na/
    - 소리 나
    - "Is it far?"

37. **Jege na.** /ɟɛgɛ na/
    - 제게 나
    - "It's close."

38. **Bëgg naa ab biye.** /bɛgg naː ab bijɛ/
    - 베그 나 압 비예
    - "I want a ticket."

39. **Kañ la oto bi di dem?** /kaɲ la oto bi di dɛm/
    - 칸 라 오토 비 디 뎀
    - "When does the bus leave?"

40. **Fan laa wara wàcci?** /fan laː wara watʃtʃi/
    - 판 라 와라 와치
    - "Where do I get off?"

### Social & Cultural (41-50)
41. **Jàmm!** /ɟamm/
    - 잠
    - "Cheers!" (peace)

42. **Dewenati bi bu baax!** /dɛwɛnati bi bu baːx/
    - 데웨나티 비 부 바흐
    - "Happy birthday!"

43. **Yendu ak jàmm!** /jɛndu ak ɟamm/
    - 옌두 악 잠
    - "Best wishes!"

44. **Bonne année!** (French loan) /bɔn ane/
    - 본 아네
    - "Happy New Year!"

45. **Nob naa la.** /nob naː la/
    - 놉 나 라
    - "I love you."

46. **Namm naa la.** /namm naː la/
    - 남 나 라
    - "I miss you."

47. **Barkeel!** /barkɛːl/
    - 바르겔
    - "Good luck!"

48. **Ba beneen.** /ba bɛnɛːn/
    - 바 베넨
    - "Goodbye." (until next time)

49. **Fanaan ak jàmm.** /fanaːn ak ɟamm/
    - 파난 악 잠
    - "Good night." (sleep with peace)

50. **Insha'Allah.** /inʃaʔalla/
    - 인샤알라
    - "God willing." (future intention)

## Phonological Rules

### Consonant Gemination
Gemination is phonemic:
- benn /bɛnn/ "one" vs ben /bɛn/ different meaning

### Prenasalized Stops
Word-initial clusters: mb, nd, nj, ng are single phonemes.

### Vowel Harmony
Based on ATR (Advanced Tongue Root) feature affecting suffix vowels.

### Consonant Harmony
Root consonants may harmonize for place of articulation.

---

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Prenasalized Stops:** Write as nasal + stop sequence
2. **Long Vowels:** Not distinguished in Hangul output
3. **Gemination:** Single consonant in Hangul
4. **Palatal Stops:** c→ㅊ, j→ㅈ
5. **Uvular:** q maps to ㅋ, x maps to ㅎ
