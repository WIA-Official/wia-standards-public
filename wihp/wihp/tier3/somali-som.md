# WIHP: Somali (Af Soomaali) - som

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Af Soomaali
- **ISO 639-3:** som
- **Speaker Population:** ~22 million
- **Language Family:** Afroasiatic → Cushitic → East Cushitic
- **Writing System:** Latin alphabet (since 1972)
- **Phonological Type:** Agglutinative, tonal

## Phonological Features
- **Pharyngeal Consonants:** Distinctive feature
- **Tone/Pitch Accent:** High and low tones
- **Long Vowels:** Phonemic length distinction
- **Gender System:** Masculine and feminine

## Standard Sentences (50 Sentences)

### Basic Communication (1-10)
1. **Salaan.** /saˈlaːn/
   - 살란
   - "Hello."

2. **Iska warran?** /ˈiska waˈrran/
   - 이스카 와란
   - "How are you?"

3. **Waan fiicanahay.** /waːn fiːtʃaˈnahaj/
   - 완 피차나하이
   - "I am fine."

4. **Mahadsanid.** /mahadsaˈnid/
   - 마하드사니드
   - "Thank you."

5. **Haa.** /haː/
   - 하
   - "Yes."

6. **Maya.** /ˈmaja/
   - 마야
   - "No."

7. **Raalli noqo.** /raːˈlli noˈqo/
   - 랄리 노코
   - "Sorry."

8. **Fadlan.** /ˈfadlan/
   - 파들란
   - "Please."

9. **Ma fahmi karo.** /ma ˈfahmi ˈkaro/
   - 마 파흐미 카로
   - "I don't understand."

10. **Ingiriis ma ku hadashaa?** /ingiˈriːs ma ku hadaˈʃaː/
    - 잉기리스 마 쿠 하다샤
    - "Do you speak English?"

### Personal Information (11-20)
11. **Magacaa?** /maɡaˈtʃaː/
    - 마가차
    - "What is your name?"

12. **Magaceygu waa...** /maɡatʃeˈɡu waː/
    - 마가체구 와
    - "My name is..."

13. **Xaggee ka timid?** /ħaɡˈɡeː ka tiˈmid/
    - 학게 카 티미드
    - "Where are you from?"

14. **Waxaan ka imid Soomaaliya.** /waħaːn ka iˈmid soːmaːˈlija/
    - 와한 카 이미드 소말리야
    - "I'm from Somalia."

15. **Imisa jir ayaad tahay?** /iˈmisa dʒir aˈjaːd taˈhaj/

16. **Waxaan ahay soddon jir.** /waħaːn aˈhaj ˈsoddon dʒir/
    - 와한 아하이 소돈 지르
    - "I am thirty years old."

17. **Maxaad shaqaysaa?** /maħaːd ʃaqajˈsaː/
    - 마하드 샤카이사
    - "What do you do?"

18. **Waxaan ahay macallin.** /waħaːn aˈhaj maˈtʃallin/
    - 와한 아하이 마찰린
    - "I am a teacher."

19. **Xaggee ku nooshahay?** /ħaɡˈɡeː ku noːʃaˈhaj/

20. **Waxaan ku noolahay Muqdisho.** /waħaːn ku noːlaˈhaj muqdiˈʃo/

## Phonological Rules

### Pharyngeals
Somali has pharyngeal consonants (/ħ/ and /ʕ/) typical of Cushitic languages.

### Retroflex Stop
The retroflex /ɖ/ (written dh) is distinctive.

### Vowel Length
Vowel length is phonemic and affects meaning.

### Tone/Pitch Accent
Somali uses pitch to distinguish grammatical meanings (especially gender).

### Assimilation
Consonant clusters undergo various assimilation processes.

---

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Pharyngeals:** ħ→ㅎ, ʕ→ㅇ (special treatment)
2. **Retroflex:** dh /ɖ/ maps to ㄷ
3. **Uvular:** q /q/ maps to ㅋ
4. **Long Vowels:** Represented same as short in Hangul
5. **Glottal Stop:** Use ㅇ in syllable initial
