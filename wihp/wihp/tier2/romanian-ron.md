# WIHP: Romanian (Română) - ron

## Language Overview
- **Native Name:** Română / Limba română
- **ISO 639-3:** ron
- **Speaker Population:** ~24 million
- **Language Family:** Indo-European → Romance → Eastern Romance
- **Writing System:** Latin alphabet (with diacritics: ă, â, î, ș, ț)
- **Phonological Type:** Relatively conservative Romance phonology

## Phonological Features
- **Vowel System:** 7 vowels including central /ə/ and /ɨ/
- **Palatalization:** Before front vowels
- **Definite Article:** Enclitic (suffixed to nouns)
- **Stress:** Variable but predictable

## Phonological Rules

### Palatalization Before Front Vowels
Certain consonants change before e, i:
- c /k/ → /tʃ/ before e, i: pace /patʃe/ "peace"
- g /g/ → /dʒ/ before e, i: ger /dʒer/ "frost"
- ch /k/, gh /g/ preserve velar before e, i

### Central Vowels
Romanian has two central vowels rare in Romance:
- ă /ə/: casă /kasə/ "house"
- â/î /ɨ/: pâine /pɨine/ "bread"

### Diphthongs
Romanian preserves Latin diphthongs and creates new ones:
- ea: seară /searə/ "evening"
- oa: soare /soare/ "sun"

### Definite Article Enclisis
The definite article is suffixed to nouns:
- om "man" → omul "the man"
- casă "house" → casa "the house"

### Stress Patterns
Stress is variable but generally on penultimate or final syllable:
- ˈcasă (penultimate)
- coˈpil (final)

## WIHP Implementation Notes

1. **Central Vowels:** ㅓ for ă /ə/, ㅡ for â/î /ɨ/
2. **Affricates:** ț→ㅊ (ts), c(e,i)→ㅊ (tʃ), g(e,i)→ㅈ (dʒ)
3. **Diphthongs:** Write as vowel sequences (ㅔ아 for ea, ㅗ아 for oa)
4. **Voiced Fricatives:** ㅈ for both z /z/ and j /ʒ/
5. **R-sound:** Romanian trill maps to ㄹ
