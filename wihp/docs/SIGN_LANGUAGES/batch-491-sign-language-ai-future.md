### Introduction
Artificial intelligence is increasingly being applied to sign language technology, from recognition systems to translation and avatar generation. While promising, these technologies face significant challenges and raise important ethical considerations.

#### Current State
| Aspect | Status |
|--------|--------|

| Vocabulary | Often restricted |
