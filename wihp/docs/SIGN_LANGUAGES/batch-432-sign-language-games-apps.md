| **Users** | Learners, deaf, educators |

### Introduction
The digital age has brought numerous apps and games designed to help people learn sign languages, access sign language dictionaries, and engage with deaf culture. These tools range from simple flashcard apps to sophisticated AI-powered learning platforms.
