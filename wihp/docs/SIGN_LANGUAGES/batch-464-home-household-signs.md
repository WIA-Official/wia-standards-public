# Home and Household Signs
## Domestic Vocabulary in Sign Languages

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Topic** | Home and household vocabulary |
| **Context** | Daily living, domestic life |
| **Importance** | Essential vocabulary |
| **Iconicity** | Many action-based signs |
| **Universal** | Common across cultures |

### Introduction
Home and household vocabulary is fundamental for daily communication. Sign languages have extensive vocabulary for rooms, furniture, appliances, and domestic activities, often with iconic signs mimicking actions or object shapes.
