### Introduction
The relationship between cochlear implants (CIs) and sign language has been controversial in deaf communities. While some view CIs as threats to deaf culture, research increasingly supports the benefits of bimodal bilingualism—using both sign language and spoken language via CI. This represents a shift from either/or thinking.
