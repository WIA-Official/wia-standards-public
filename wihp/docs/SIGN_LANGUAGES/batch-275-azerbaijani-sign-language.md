# Azerbaijani Sign Language (AzSL / AID)
## Azerbaycan isharet dili

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Official Name** | Azerbaijani Sign Language |
| **Native Name** | Azerbaycan isharet dili (AID) |
| **ISO 639-3** | Not assigned (subclass of RSL) |
| **Country** | Azerbaijan |
| **Users** | ~31,000 deaf people; ~334,590 D/HH estimated |
| **Status** | Officially recognized (2025) |
| **Language Family** | Based on Russian Sign Language |

### Historical Development
- **Soviet Era**: Russian Sign Language introduced
- **Post-Soviet**: Distinct Azerbaijani variety developed
- **2021**: First digital dictionary project launched (jestdili.az)
- **2022**: AzSLD dataset project initiated
- **2023**: AI recognition system won Technofest 2022
- **June 2025**: Official recognition announced (WFD acknowledgment)

### Official Recognition (2025)
- Amendments to Law on Rights of Persons with Disabilities
- World Federation of the Deaf congratulated Azerbaijan
- Historic milestone for deaf rights in the country
- Recognition essential for equality and inclusion

### Digital Resources
- **jestdili.az**: First online dictionary
  - ~3,000 words translated to sign language
  - 2,100+ words with images and videos
  - Search functionality for learning
  - Supported by Nar and Regional Development PU

### AzSLD Dataset (2024-2025)
- First publicly available AzSL dataset
- 14,000+ video/image samples
- Fingerspelling, words, and sentences
- 10,104 synchronized recordings (two camera angles)
- Developed at ADA University CeDAR
- Funded by BP and Ministry of Education

### Technical Achievements
- Hybrid word recognition system developed
- 13,444 dactyl alphabet samples collected
- CNN/RNN models for sign recognition
- 1st prize at Technofest 2022 (international)
- Real-time fingerspelling-to-text translation

### Education System
- Two Republican Special Boarding Schools
- Specialized education for deaf community
- Bridge to a Silent World book published
- Training schools in capital and regions

### Organizations
