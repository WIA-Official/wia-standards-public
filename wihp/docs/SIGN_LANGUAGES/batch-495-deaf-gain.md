### Introduction
"Deaf Gain" reframes deafness from a deficit model ("hearing loss") to an asset-based perspective, examining what deaf people and deaf ways of being contribute to humanity. This concept challenges medical-deficit views and highlights deaf contributions to society, language, and culture.
