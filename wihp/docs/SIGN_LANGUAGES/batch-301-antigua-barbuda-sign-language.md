# Antigua and Barbuda Sign Language
## Sign Language of Antigua and Barbuda

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Primary Language** | American Sign Language (ASL) |
| **Country** | Antigua and Barbuda |
| **Deaf Population** | ~1,000 estimated |
| **Status** | ASL-dominant |
| **Region** | Eastern Caribbean |

### Language Situation
- American Sign Language (ASL) is primary sign language
- No documented indigenous sign language
- Part of Caribbean ASL sphere
- Limited educational resources for deaf

### Geographic Context
- Sovereign state in Americas
- Between Caribbean Sea and Atlantic Ocean
- Part of Leeward Islands
- English-speaking nation
- Population: ~100,000 total

### Demographics
- Deaf population: ~1,000 estimated
- English: Official language
- Antiguan Creole also spoken
- Commonwealth nation

### UN Initiatives

  - Part of Caribbean SIDS inclusion efforts
