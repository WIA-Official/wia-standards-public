### 2020 Law

- Official recognition
- Access rights

## Research and Documentation

### First Linguistic Description
**Robert Gavrilescu PhD Thesis**

Research approach:
- History and education in specialized schools
- Sociolinguistic variations context
- Corpus from two Romanian regions
- Semiological approach (Christian Cuxac framework)

### Prior Research
- Gavrilescu (2011, 2014, 2016)
- First systematic linguistic studies

### Research Goals
1. Show LSR is a language with:
   - Own grammatical structures
   - Own lexicon
   - Natural variations
2. Typological comparisons:
   - LSR vs Romanian (spoken/written)
   - LSR vs LSF (French Sign Language)

### Research Challenge
> "In Romania, scientific research on LSR has yet to be developed"

---

## Education

### Current Situation
- Deaf education highly valued by community
- Limited opportunities
- Teachers often hearing
- Combination of LSR and spoken/written Romanian
- Many deaf children integrated into hearing schools

### School Integration
- Limited deaf students per classroom
- Mainstream education common
- Specialist schools exist
