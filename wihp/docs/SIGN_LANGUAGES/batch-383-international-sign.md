### Introduction
International Sign (IS) is a form of sign communication used among deaf people from different countries when they don't share a common sign language. It is not a fully developed natural language but rather a contact variety or pidgin that enables cross-linguistic communication.

IS is particularly important at international deaf events, conferences, and sporting competitions like the Deaflympics.
