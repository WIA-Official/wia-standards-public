| **Variation** | Regional, generational |
| **Use** | Essential daily vocabulary |

### Introduction
Clothing vocabulary is essential for everyday communication, from getting dressed to shopping. Many clothing signs in sign languages are iconic, often signed at the location on the body where the item is worn.
