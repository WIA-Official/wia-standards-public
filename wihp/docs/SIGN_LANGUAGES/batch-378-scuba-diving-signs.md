### Introduction
Scuba diving sign language is a standardized set of hand signals used by divers underwater where verbal communication is impossible. Unlike other occupational signs, diving signs are used recreationally by millions of people worldwide and are taught as part of basic certification.

This represents one of the most widely used and well-documented occupational/recreational sign systems in the world.

### Why Signs Are Essential
Underwater conditions:
- Speech impossible with regulator
- Sound travels differently
- Visual contact important
- Safety-critical communication
- Limited visibility possible

### Basic Diving Signs (Universal)
| Sign | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Thumbs up | Ascend/End dive |

| Fist to chest | Low on air |

| Pointing | Look at this |
