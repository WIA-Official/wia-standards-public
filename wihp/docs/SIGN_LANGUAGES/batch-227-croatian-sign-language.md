## Language Classification

### French Sign Language Family
- Descended through Austrian Sign Language (ÖGS)
- Part of broader French SL family
- Historical Austro-Hungarian connections

### Yugoslav Sign Language History
- Formerly considered dialect of Yugoslav SL
- Post-Yugoslavia: Separate national languages
- Dialectal diversity not fully assessed

### Related Languages
- Serbian Sign Language
- Slovenian Sign Language
- Kosovar Sign Language
- Bosnian Sign Language

---

## Linguistic Features

### Word Order
- **SVO** (Subject-Verb-Object)
- Same as spoken Croatian
- Documented in research

### Manual Alphabet
- Two-handed system
- In wide use

---

## Education Centers

### Major Cities
1. **Zagreb** - Capital, main center
2. **Split** - Coastal city
3. **Osijek** - Eastern Croatia

### First School
- Founded: 1885 in Zagreb
- Historic institution
- Beginning of deaf education in Croatia

### Current Situation
- Special schools and regular schools
- Early intervention: Oral only
- Preschools/schools: Oral + Signed Croatian
- True HZJ potential not yet recognized

### Challenge
> "The value and potential of Croatian Sign Language (HZJ) for a healthy and successful development of deaf children has not been recognized yet, either among professionals working with deaf children or among the members of the deaf community themselves."

## Research and Documentation

### HZJ Lab
**Laboratory for Sign Language and Deaf Culture Research**
- Established: 2004
- Location: University of Zagreb
- Focus: HZJ linguistics
- Bilateral American-Croatian project

### Grammar Project (2004+)
**"A Basic Grammar of Croatian Sign Language"**
- Purdue University (USA)
- University of Zagreb (Croatia)
- First systematic grammar work
- International collaboration

### Conference (2001)
- Sign Language and Deaf Culture Conference
- Zagreb, May 3-5, 2001
- Important academic milestone

## Legal Status

### 2015 Recognition
Croatian Sign Language legally recognized

### Media Requirements
- Croatian Radiotelevision (HRT) required to promote HZJ translation
- Program accessibility mandated
- Broadcasting inclusion

- HZJ undervalued
