### Introduction
Contrary to common assumptions, many deaf individuals enjoy and participate in music. Through vibration, visual elements, amplification, and the musical qualities of sign language itself, deaf people engage with music in unique and meaningful ways.

### How Deaf People Experience Music
| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
