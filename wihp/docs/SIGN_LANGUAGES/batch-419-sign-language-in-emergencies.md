### Introduction
Emergencies pose unique challenges for deaf individuals who may miss auditory alerts, phone-based systems, and verbal emergency communications. Ensuring deaf people have equal access to emergency information is a matter of safety and civil rights.

### Communication Barriers
| Barrier | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Sirens | Not heard |
| Announcements | Audio only |
| Phone alerts | May be text but limited |
| TV news | Often no interpreter |
| Radio | Inaccessible |
