### Language Description
Monaco, the world's second-smallest country (2.02 km²) with a population of approximately 40,000, does not have a distinct national sign language. The Deaf community uses French Sign Language (Langue des Signes Française, LSF), reflecting Monaco's French-speaking environment and complete integration with the French cultural and service sphere.

Monaco is entirely surrounded by France (except for its Mediterranean coastline) and French is the official language alongside Monégasque. Deaf Monégasques access all specialized services from France, particularly from the nearby city of Nice.
