# Puerto Rican Sign Language (PRSL)
## Lengua de Senas de Puerto Rico

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Official Name** | Puerto Rican Sign Language |
| **Native Name** | PRSL / PSL |
| **ISO 639-3** | psl |
| **Country** | Puerto Rico (US Territory) |
| **Users** | 8,000-340,000 deaf estimated |
| **Status** | Threatened (6b) - ASL replacing |
| **Language Family** | ASL variety/descendant |

### Historical Timeline
| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1907 | Signs introduced by Catholic nuns |
| Early 1900s | PRSL developed before formal education |
| 20th century | ASL influence through US contact |
| Present | ASL becoming dominant |

### Language Status
- PRSL believed to still exist in western/central areas
- ASL dominant in northeastern/urban areas
- Only 20 of 78 municipalities exposed to ASL schools
- May be replaced by ASL in future generations
- Endangered due to colonization effects

### Linguistic Characteristics
- Related to American Sign Language
- ~54% lexical similarity documented
- Differences in palm orientation
- Rate of sign articulation slower
- More repetition than ASL
- Some unique Puerto Rican signs

### Regional Distribution
- **Urban/Northeast**: ASL dominant
- **Western/Central**: PRSL may persist
- Mountainous regions harder to reach
- Country regions less ASL exposure

### Legal Status
- Under US Americans with Disabilities Act
- Same rights as mainland deaf Americans
- No specific PRSL recognition laws

### Education System
- Signed English and ASL in schools
- First deaf school used ASL
- ASL taught in northeastern schools only
- University of Puerto Rico offers ASL
- Public ASL classes available

### Interpreting Services
- Four established interpreting agencies
- Video relay services available
- **Sign Language Interpreters Inc (SLI)**: Offers PRSL interpretation
- Significant interpreter shortage
- Only one agency offers PRSL specifically

- New generations more ASL-influenced
- PRSL preservation needed
- Documentation could preserve memory of old PRSL
