| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Type** | Tactical/operational signs |

| **Status** | Active, classified/documented |
| **Standardization** | NATO, national standards |

### Introduction
Military tactical hand signals are standardized visual communication systems used by armed forces worldwide for silent communication during operations. These signs enable coordination when verbal communication would compromise tactical advantage.

Unlike recreational sign systems, military signs are operationally critical and sometimes classified, though many basic signals are documented.
