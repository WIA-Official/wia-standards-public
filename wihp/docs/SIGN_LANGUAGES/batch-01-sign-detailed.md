## 1. HELLO (안녕하세요)

| 항목 | 내용 |
|------|------|
| **ASL 표현** | HELLO |
| **의미** | 안녕하세요 (인사) |
| **손 모양 (HS)** | HS-02 (손바닥 - B hand flat, 엄지는 손바닥에) |
| **위치 (LC)** | LC-02 (이마 - 관자놀이/이마 옆) |
| **움직임 (MV)** | MV-03 (전진 - 이마에서 바깥으로 뻗기) |
| **방향 (OR)** | OR-01 (전방 - 손바닥이 앞을 향함) |
| **표정 (FE)** | FE-02 (미소) |
| **WIA Code** | `HS02-LC02-MV03-OR01-FE02` |
| **상세설명** | 손가락 4개를 모아 펴고 엄지는 손바닥에 접음. 이마 옆에서 시작해서 경례하듯 바깥으로 뻗음 |
| **출처** | kidscarehomehealth.com |

**원문 참고:**
> "The word 'hello' in ASL begins with raising your hand, palm facing out, four fingers held straight and together with your thumb curled into your palm. The movement is to touch your four fingertips to your forehead with palm facing out, like a salute, then straightening your hand and arm back to a 90 degree angle."

## 2. THANK YOU (감사합니다)

| 항목 | 내용 |
|------|------|
| **ASL 표현** | THANK-YOU |
| **의미** | 감사합니다 / 고마워요 |
| **손 모양 (HS)** | HS-02 (손바닥 - flat hand) |
| **위치 (LC)** | LC-05→LC-09 (입/턱에서 시작 → 중립공간으로) |
| **움직임 (MV)** | MV-03 (전진 - 턱에서 앞/아래로 호를 그리며) |
| **방향 (OR)** | OR-05→OR-01 (내측에서 전방으로) |
| **표정 (FE)** | FE-02 (미소 - 감사 표현) |
| **WIA Code** | `HS02-LC05-MV03-OR05-FE02` |
| **상세설명** | 펼친 손바닥, 손가락 끝이 턱에서 시작해서 상대방을 향해 앞으로 호를 그리며 내려감. 입술 키스를 보내는 것과 유사 |
| **출처** | handspeak.com, takelessons.com |

**원문 참고:**
> "Thank you is signed one-handed (using your dominant hand), with a flat hand, palm facing towards the face, fingertips starting at the chin, and the hand arcing out from the chin towards the person you are thanking."

## 숫자 상세 설명

### 1-5 (손바닥 몸쪽)
> "When done as casual, isolated signs, numbers '1 through 5' are typically produced palm-back by native Deaf adult signers."

### 6-9 (엄지가 손가락 터치)
> "Numbers 6 through 10 use different handshapes. For 6, touch your thumb to your pinky finger. For 7, touch your thumb to your ring finger. For 8, touch your thumb to your middle finger. For 9, touch your thumb to your index finger."

### 10 (엄지 흔들기)
> "For 10, make a fist and shake it side to side, like you're giving a thumbs-up."

## 1. I LOVE YOU (사랑해요)

| 항목 | 내용 |
|------|------|
| **ASL 표현** | ILY (I-L-Y 결합) |
| **의미** | 사랑해요 |

| **위치 (LC)** | LC-09 (중립 공간) |
| **움직임 (MV)** | MV-00 (정지) 또는 MV-06 (좌우 흔들기) |
| **방향 (OR)** | OR-01 (전방 - 상대방 향함) |
| **표정 (FE)** | FE-02 (미소) |
| **WIA Code** | `HS16-LC09-MV00-OR01-FE02` |
| **상세설명** | 엄지, 검지, 새끼손가락을 펴고 중지+약지는 접음. I+L+Y의 결합 |
| **출처** | handspeak.com, lingvano.com |

**원문 참고:**
> "To sign I love you in American Sign Language (ASL), point out your thumb and index finger to form an 'L'. While keeping them extended, lift your little finger. Your middle and ring finger keep touching your palm."

> "The handshape ILY is all-known in ASL/Deaf culture. The three initials of I, L, and Y handshapes are blended into the standalone ILY handshape with the thumb, index finger and pinky extended."
