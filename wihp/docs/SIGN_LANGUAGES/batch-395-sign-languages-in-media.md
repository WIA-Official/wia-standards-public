# Sign Language in Media

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Topic** | Deaf representation in media |

| **Platforms** | Film, TV, streaming, social |
| **Impact** | Visibility and understanding |

### Introduction
Sign language representation in media has evolved from stereotypical and inaccurate portrayals to more authentic depictions. Media representation shapes public perception of deaf people and sign language, making accurate and positive representation crucial for the deaf community.

Recent years have seen breakthrough moments with deaf actors and authentic sign language in mainstream entertainment.
