### Introduction
Sign language interpreting is the professional practice of facilitating communication between deaf and hearing individuals by rendering spoken language into sign language and vice versa. Professional interpreters are essential for deaf people's access to education, healthcare, legal services, and public life.

Interpreting is a skilled profession requiring fluency in both languages, cultural competence, and ethical adherence.
