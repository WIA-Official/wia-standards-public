# Weather and Nature Signs
## Environmental Vocabulary in Sign Languages

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Topic** | Weather and nature vocabulary |
| **Context** | Daily life, environment |
| **Iconicity** | Often highly visual |
| **Importance** | Common conversation topic |
| **Universal** | All cultures discuss weather |

### Introduction
Weather and nature vocabulary is essential for daily conversation and environmental awareness. Sign languages often have highly iconic signs for weather phenomena and natural elements, reflecting the visual nature of these concepts.
