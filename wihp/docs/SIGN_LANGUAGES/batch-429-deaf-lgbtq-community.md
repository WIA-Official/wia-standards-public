### Introduction
Deaf LGBTQ+ individuals navigate multiple identities and communities. The intersection of deaf and queer identities creates unique experiences, challenges, and cultural expressions. A vibrant deaf LGBTQ+ community exists with its own organizations, events, and cultural practices.
