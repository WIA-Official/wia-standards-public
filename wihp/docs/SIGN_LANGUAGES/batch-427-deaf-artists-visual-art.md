### Introduction
Deaf artists have made significant contributions to visual arts, often creating works that explore deaf experience, identity, and culture. The De'VIA movement formally articulated the concept of deaf-centered art, though deaf artists have always expressed their unique perspectives through visual media.

### De'VIA Manifesto (1989)
| Aspect | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| What | Art by deaf artists about deaf experience |
| Themes | Deaf culture, oppression, liberation |
| Forms | Any visual medium |
| Not all deaf art | Must relate to deaf experience |
