### Introduction
Documenting sign languages presents unique challenges due to their visual nature. This field has developed specialized methodologies for recording, annotating, and analyzing sign languages, essential for preservation, linguistic research, and educational materials development.

### Why Document Sign Languages?
| Reason | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Preservation | Before loss |
| Research | Linguistic analysis |
| Education | Teaching materials |
| Advocacy | Recognition support |
| Community | Resource for users |
