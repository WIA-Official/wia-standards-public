## Constitutional Recognition

### One of 4 EU Countries!
Hungary is one of only 4 countries to recognize sign language in federal constitution.
(Others: Austria, Finland, Portugal)

### 2011 Constitution Article H(3)
> "Hungary defends Hungarian Sign Language as part of Hungarian culture."

---

## Legal Framework

### Act CXXV of 2009
**Passed: November 9, 2009 (Unanimous)**

Key provisions:
1. MJNY = independent natural language
2. State interpretation services guaranteed
3. Interpreter qualifications and payment specified
4. Bilingual education mandatory (Sections 11-14)

### Act Definition
> "Hungarian Sign Language shall mean an independent natural language developed in Hungary, which is used by the deaf community, consists of visual linguistic signs and has a special grammatical system and grammatical rules"

### 2020 Amendment (Unique in EU!)
- HSL equal to spoken Hungarian for state exams
- Deaf AND hearing can take HSL for Master's degree
- Deafblind communication systems recognized
- Right to choose HSL over mainstreaming
- Accessibility technology advancement

### Historic Moment
**First time in Hungarian Parliament history**:
Dr. Ádám Kósa, SINOSZ president and MEP, addressed parliament in sign language during the bill debate.

- Spoken Hungarian influence on signers

## Services

### Free Interpretation
Eligibility:
- Hungarian citizens
- Persons with free movement rights
- Immigrants settled in Hungary
- Who are deaf or deafblind

### KONTAKT Service
Similar to VRS/VRI in USA
- State-funded interpretation
- Limited hours (pandemic affected expansion)
