### Introduction
Sign language technology encompasses digital tools, artificial intelligence, and devices designed to support sign language users. This includes translation systems, learning apps, video communication, and emerging AI-powered recognition and generation systems.

While technology offers promise for accessibility, the deaf community has important perspectives on development priorities and limitations.
