### Historical Development
- **1932**: Society for visually impaired created
- **1934**: Deaf community added to the Society
- **Soviet Era**: RSL introduced through centralized policy
- **2008**: Joined Convention on Rights of Persons with Disabilities
- **2022**: USAID interpreter training program launched
- **2023**: First sign language interpretation service established

### USAID Programs (2022-2024)
- Month-long professional development course
- 271 class hours of training for 12 interpreters
- Partnership with Embassy of Malaysia
- Support from Blind and Deaf Society of Turkmenistan
- "Dispatching Service of Sign Language Interpretation" initiative

### Sign Language Services (2023)
- Free interpretation launched in all velayats (provinces)
- Services available in: Ashgabat, Balkanabat, Bayramali, Dashoguz, Mary, Turkmenabad
- Local BDST branches provide 5-day/week service
- On-call and in-office interpretation
- 500+ individuals supported in first 8 months
