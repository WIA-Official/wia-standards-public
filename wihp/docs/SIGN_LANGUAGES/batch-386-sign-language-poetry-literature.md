### Introduction
Sign language literature encompasses creative and artistic works in sign languages, including poetry, storytelling, theatre, and other performative arts. Like spoken language literature, sign language literature has developed rich traditions with unique aesthetic principles tied to the visual-spatial modality.

Sign language poetry and literature demonstrate that deaf communities have sophisticated artistic traditions equal to any spoken language culture.

- Uses classifiers and movement
- No English translation captures fully
- Demonstrates ASL's poetic capacity
