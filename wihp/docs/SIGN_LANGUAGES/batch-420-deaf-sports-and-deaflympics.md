# Deaf Sports and the Deaflympics
## International Deaf Athletics

### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Event** | Deaflympics (formerly World Games for the Deaf) |
| **Founded** | 1924 |
| **Frequency** | Every 4 years |
| **Governing Body** | ICSD |
| **Significance** | Oldest international disability sport event |

### Introduction
The Deaflympics is the pinnacle of international deaf sports, predating both the Paralympics and Special Olympics. Deaf sports provide opportunities for deaf athletes to compete in an environment where communication is fully accessible, fostering community and demonstrating deaf capability.
