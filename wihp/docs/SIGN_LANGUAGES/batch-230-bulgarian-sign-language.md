## Historic Recognition

### Bulgarian Sign Language Act (January 21, 2021)
**Unanimous parliamentary support!**

- Promulgated: February 2, 2021 (State Gazette no. 9)
- Entered into force: February 6, 2021
- All parliamentary groups voted yes
- Historic day: "New May 24" for deaf community

### What the Law Grants
1. BGSL = natural and independent language
2. Equal status to spoken Bulgarian
3. Right to education through BGSL
4. Right to information in BGSL
5. Right to use language always and everywhere

### Implementation Requirements
By December 31, 2022:
- All public institutions must provide services in BGSL
- State authorities at central and municipal levels
- Administrative services accessible to deaf

## Historical Founder

### Karl Urbich (German)
- Trained in Eisenach
- Taught deaf in Weimar, Hamburg, Heidelberg
- Met Bulgarian students in Heidelberg
- Learned Bulgarian in one year
- Opened private school (1898) with ~6 pupils
- Funded everything from own pocket for 8 years

## 2023 Amendments

### Strengthening the Act (January 24, 2023)
**Two years after initial adoption**

Key improvements:
1. Bachelor's program in BGSL preparation
2. Interpreter remuneration increased
3. 10% of minimum wage for interpreters
4. Foundation for authentic deaf education

### Sofia University Partnership
"St. Kliment Ohridski" collaboration for:
- Comprehensive undergraduate major
- By end of decade

## Education History

### Boarding Schools
3 boarding schools for deaf (historically)
- Oral methods emphasized
- Speaking/voicing Bulgarian
- Written Bulgarian understanding
- BGSL downplayed or banned

### Cultural Concepts
- "Deaf culture," "Deaf community" relatively new
- Better established in North America/Western Europe
- Growing awareness since 2013 Deaflympics

---

## Media Accessibility

### Requirements
- News and current affairs in BGSL
- Subtitles for all TV broadcasts
- Films must have Bulgarian subtitles for deaf
- On-demand services included

### Film Industry Act (Article 41)
Mandatory subtitles for:
- Foreign films
- Bulgarian films
- Distribution requirement

## Challenges Before 2021

### Historical Isolation
> "For generations, deaf individuals in Bulgaria faced isolation and oppression due to the absence of recognition for their language."

### Interpreter Shortage
- Inadequate trained interpreters
- Lack of certification
- Government support lacking
