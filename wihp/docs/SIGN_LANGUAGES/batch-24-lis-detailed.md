# WIA Talk Sign Language Mapping - LIS (Lingua dei Segni Italiana / Italian Sign Language)
## Batch 24: 20 Signs with Full WIA Talk Component Analysis

**Language:** LIS - Lingua dei Segni Italiana (Italian Sign Language)
**Region:** Italy
**Estimated Users:** 40,000-70,000 deaf signers
**Language Family:** Related to French Sign Language
**Recognition:** May 19, 2021 - Official recognition!

---

## LIS Characteristics

### Historical Background
- **1784:** First school for deaf in Rome (Tommaso Silvestri)
- **1828:** First deaf school in Milan
- **1880:** Milan Conference - oralism adopted (hosted IN Italy!)
- **2021:** Finally recognized as official language!

### Key Features
- French Sign Language influence
- Own grammar distinct from Italian
- Rich non-manual markers
- Regional variations exist

### Organizations
- **ENS** (Ente Nazionale Sordi) - main organization
- **FIADDA** - Family advocacy
- Mason Perkins Deafness Fund
