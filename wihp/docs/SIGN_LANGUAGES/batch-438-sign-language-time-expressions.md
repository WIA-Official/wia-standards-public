### Overview
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|

### Introduction
Sign languages express time in unique ways, often using a spatial timeline where the past is behind the signer and the future is in front. This visual representation of time differs from spoken languages and demonstrates the visual-spatial nature of sign language grammar.

### The Time Line
