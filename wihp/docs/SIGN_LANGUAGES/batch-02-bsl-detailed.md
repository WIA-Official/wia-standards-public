### 1.1 HELLO (안녕)
| 컴포넌트 | 코드 | 설명 |
|----------|------|------|
| HS (손모양) | HS-03 | 펼친 손 (Open hand/5 hand) |
| LC (위치) | LC-02 | 머리 옆 (Side of head) |
| MV (움직임) | MV-06 | 좌우 흔들기 (Wave side to side) |
| OR (방향) | OR-01 | 전방 (Palm facing out) |
| FE (표정) | FE-02 | 미소 (Friendly smile) |

**WIA Code:** HS03-LC02-MV06-OR01-FE02

**출처:** evergreen-life.co.uk, british-sign.co.uk
> "With your palm facing the person you're signing to, wave your dominant hand near the side of your head in one arc. The sign is the same no matter if you're greeting someone or bidding them farewell."

## 2. 숫자 1-10

### BSL 숫자 특징
- **손바닥 방향:** 몸쪽을 향함 (palm facing self)
- **지역 변이:** 6-9는 지역마다 다름 (남부 vs 북부)
- **0-5:** 전국적으로 동일
- **6-9 남부 방식:** 엄지부터 시작하여 손가락 추가
- **6-9 북부 방식:** 새끼손가락부터 시작

**출처:** accessbsl.com, commandinghands.co.uk
> "You sign with your hand facing towards you and holding the hand up at chest height, raising the index finger to indicate the number 1, then raising the index and middle finger together to represent the number 2..."
> "Numbers 0 to 5 are generally the same across different regions in the UK."

| 숫자 | 손모양 | WIA Code | 설명 |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| 1 | 검지 펴기 | HS07-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Index finger extended |
| 2 | 검지+중지 | HS04-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Index + middle |
| 3 | 검지+중지+약지 | HS20-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | 3 fingers extended |
| 4 | 4손가락 (엄지 접음) | HS10-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | 4 fingers, thumb tucked |
| 5 | 5손가락 모두 | HS03-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | All 5 fingers |
| 6 (남부) | 엄지+검지 | HS21-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Thumb bent at knuckle / Thumb+index |
| 7 (남부) | 엄지+검지+중지 | HS22-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Thumb+index+middle |
| 8 (남부) | 엄지+검지+중지+약지 | HS23-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Thumb+3 fingers |
| 9 (남부) | 엄지+4손가락 | HS24-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Thumb+4 fingers (little bent) |
| 10 | 양손 사용 | HS25-LC09-MV00-OR05-FE03 | Various regional signs |

**참고:** 숫자 10은 지역 변이가 가장 많음

## 3. 기본 문장 5개

### 3.1 I LOVE YOU (사랑해)
**구조:** I (나) + LOVE (사랑) + YOU (너)

#### LOVE 사인
| 컴포넌트 | 코드 | 설명 |
|----------|------|------|
| HS (손모양) | HS-02 | 펼친 손 양손 (Flat hands) |
| LC (위치) | LC-07 | 가슴/심장 (Chest/heart) |
| MV (움직임) | MV-11 | 교차 (Cross over) |
| OR (방향) | OR-05 | 내측 (Palms toward body) |
| FE (표정) | FE-01 | 사랑/따뜻함 (Loving) |

**WIA Code (LOVE):** HS02-LC07-MV11-OR05-FE01

**전체 문장 순서:**
1. I: 자신을 검지로 가리킴
2. LOVE: 양손 펼쳐서 가슴에 X자로 교차
3. YOU: 상대방을 검지로 가리킴

**출처:** accessbsl.com, lead-academy.org, johnacademy.co.uk
> "Lift both hands up, palms flat, then cross one over the other in an 'X' shape. This sign represents the word 'love.' Bring both hands to your chest's heart area, maintaining the 'X' shape."
> "In British Sign Language, 'I love you' is usually signed as I (point to self), LOVE (hands cross over chest), YOU (point at the other person)."

### 3.2 WHAT IS YOUR NAME? (이름이 뭐예요?)
**BSL 어순:** NAME - YOU - WHAT?

#### NAME 사인
| 컴포넌트 | 코드 | 설명 |
|----------|------|------|
| HS (손모양) | HS-17 | N 손모양 (검지+중지 펴서 붙임) |
| LC (위치) | LC-02 | 이마 (Forehead) |
| MV (움직임) | MV-17 | 손목 비틀기 (Twist at wrist) |
| OR (방향) | OR-05→01 | 내측→전방 (Palm in → out) |
| FE (표정) | FE-06 | 의문 (Questioning) |

**WIA Code (NAME):** HS17-LC02-MV17-OR05-FE06

**출처:** british-sign.co.uk, evergreen-life.co.uk, accessbsl.com
> "Tips of index and middle fingers ('N' shape) touch forehead. Hand twists forwards at wrist until fingers are facing forwards in front of face."
> "Sign the word 'name' by placing the tips of your index and middle fingers to your forehead. Twist your hand forwards at your wrist until your fingers are facing forwards in front of your face."

#### WHAT 사인
- 검지를 좌우로 흔들며 의문 표정

## BSL vs ASL 주요 차이점

| 항목 | BSL | ASL |
|------|-----|-----|
| 수동 알파벳 | 양손 (Two-handed) | 한손 (One-handed) |
| 손가락 철자 사용 | 적음 (고유명사 주로) | 많음 (일반적) |
| 얼굴 표정 | 중요하지만 손동작 중심 | 매우 중요 |
| 동일성 | 31% 동일, 44% 유사 | - |
| I LOVE YOU | 3단계 (I-LOVE-YOU 각각) | ILY 단일 손모양 |
