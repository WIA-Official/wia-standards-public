🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Time travel has existed only as a dream in science fiction novels and movies. Modern physics has shown the theoretical possibility of temporal displacement through Einstein's general relativity, but there is no systematic physical framework. Energy requirement calculations, wormhole stabilization, and paradox prevention mechanisms are approached differently by each researcher, and without unified standards, safe time travel technology development is impossible. The mathematical foundations to prevent time paradoxes such as the grandfather paradox and bootstrap paradox have not been systematized.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards across accessibility, security, AI, environment, healthcare, time travel, and more—all released as free and open source.
