                    Prove attributes about yourself without revealing the underlying data.
                


                

                    🔐 What is Zero-Knowledge Proof?
                    A cryptographic method to prove you possess certain information (e.g., age > 18)
                    without revealing the actual information (e.g., exact birthdate).
