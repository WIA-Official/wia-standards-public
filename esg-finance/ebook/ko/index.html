ESG Finance

Building Sustainable Financial Systems

WIA-FIN-024 Comprehensive Guide

About This E-Book

Standard: WIA-FIN-024 ESG Finance

Version: 2.0

Published: December 2025

Authors: WIA Standards Committee

License: CC BY-SA 4.0

This comprehensive guide covers Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles in finance, from foundational concepts to advanced implementation strategies.

CHAPTER 1
Introduction to ESG Finance
Understanding ESG principles, history, and the global movement toward sustainable finance. Learn why ESG matters for investors, companies, and society.
CHAPTER 2
ESG Framework & Standards
Comprehensive overview of major ESG frameworks including TCFD, SASB, GRI, CDP, and regulatory requirements across different jurisdictions.
CHAPTER 3
Environmental Pillar
Deep dive into environmental metrics: carbon emissions, climate risk, renewable energy, water management, biodiversity, and circular economy principles.
CHAPTER 4
Social Pillar
Exploring social impact: diversity & inclusion, labor rights, employee well-being, community engagement, human rights, and supply chain responsibility.
CHAPTER 5
Governance Pillar
Corporate governance best practices: board composition, executive compensation, shareholder rights, ethics, transparency, and risk management.
CHAPTER 6
Implementation & Integration
Practical guide to implementing ESG programs: data collection, scoring methodologies, stakeholder engagement, and integrating ESG into business strategy.
CHAPTER 7
Case Studies & Success Stories
Real-world examples of ESG excellence: green bonds, impact investing, corporate transformations, and lessons learned from industry leaders.
CHAPTER 8
Future of ESG Finance
Emerging trends: AI in ESG analysis, blockchain for transparency, climate tech, nature-based solutions, and the path to net-zero by 2050.
