# WIA-FIN-024: ESG Finance Standard

**Version:** 1.2
**Status:** Stable
**Published:** November 2024
**Category:** Finance (FIN)

---

## Changes from v1.1

### New Features

1. **Climate Scenario Analysis**
   - Mandatory 2°C and 1.5°C scenario modeling
   - Physical and transition risk quantification
   - Climate Value-at-Risk (CVaR) disclosure

2. **Social Impact Measurement**
   - Theory of Change frameworks
   - Social Return on Investment (SROI) calculation
   - Stakeholder well-being metrics

3. **Governance Enhancements**
   - Cybersecurity governance requirements
   - AI ethics and governance framework
   - Tax transparency standards

### Climate Scenario Requirements

Organizations SHALL:
- Conduct scenario analysis for at least 2°C and 1.5°C pathways
- Assess both physical risks (acute, chronic) and transition risks
- Quantify financial impact on business model
- Disclose resilience strategies
- Update analysis every 2 years

**Metrics:**
```
Climate_VaR = Expected_Loss_Under_Scenario - Business_As_Usual_Value
```

### Social Impact Framework

**Theory of Change Requirements:**
- Define inputs, activities, outputs, outcomes, impact
- Establish causality between activities and impact
- Set measurable indicators for each stage
- Report annually on impact achieved

**SROI Calculation:**
```
SROI_Ratio = Net_Present_Value_Social_Impact / Investment
```

**Target:** SROI > 3:1 for social programs

### Cybersecurity Governance

**REQUIRED:**
- Board-level cybersecurity expertise
- Quarterly cybersecurity briefings to board
- Incident response and business continuity plans
- Annual cybersecurity risk assessment
- Disclosure of material cyber incidents

**Metrics:**
- Number of data breaches
- Time to detect and respond
- % of systems with MFA enabled
- Employee security training completion rate

### AI Ethics Framework

For organizations deploying AI systems:
- Establish AI ethics committee
- Conduct algorithmic bias assessments
- Ensure AI transparency and explainability
- Implement human oversight mechanisms
- Publish AI ethics policy

### Tax Transparency

**REQUIRED Disclosure:**
- Effective tax rate by jurisdiction
- Country-by-country revenue and tax paid (for large multinationals)
- Tax strategy and governance
- Use of tax havens and transfer pricing policies
