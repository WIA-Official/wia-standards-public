**Version:** 1.1
**Status:** Stable
**Published:** June 2024
**Category:** Finance (FIN)

## Changes from v1.0

### New Features

1. **Nature & Biodiversity Module**
   - TNFD framework integration
   - Biodiversity impact metrics
   - Natural capital accounting

2. **Enhanced Social Metrics**
   - Supply chain transparency requirements
   - Human rights due diligence framework
   - Living wage calculation methodology

3. **Circular Economy Assessment**
   - Product lifecycle analysis
   - Material circularity indicators
   - Waste hierarchy compliance scoring

### Updated Sections

#### ESG Scoring Framework (Updated)

**Environmental Score components:**
- Carbon emissions intensity (12%)
- Renewable energy adoption (8%)
- Water management (5%)
- Waste and circular economy (5%)
- **NEW: Biodiversity impact (5%)**

**Social Score components:**
- Diversity & inclusion (8%)
- Employee well-being (7%)
- Labor practices (6%)
- Community impact (4%)
- **NEW: Supply chain responsibility (5%)**

### New Data Requirements

**Biodiversity:**
- Land use and habitat impact (hectares)
- Threatened species in operating areas
- Ecosystem restoration investments
- Biodiversity action plan

**Supply Chain:**
- Tier 1 supplier ESG assessments (% coverage)
- Supplier diversity spend (%)
- Conflict minerals disclosure
- Modern slavery risk assessments

### Circular Economy Metrics

**REQUIRED:**
- Material circularity rate (%)
- Product take-back and recycling programs
- Design for recyclability (% of products)
- Virgin vs. recycled material ratio

**Calculation:**
```
Circularity_Rate = (Recycled_Materials_Input + Materials_Recovered) /
                   (Total_Materials_Used + Materials_Recovered) * 100
```

### Living Wage Requirement

Organizations SHALL:
- Calculate living wage benchmarks for all operating locations
- Disclose % of employees earning below living wage
- Publish action plan to achieve 100% living wage coverage
- Report annually on progress

### Assurance Expansion

**REQUIRED assurance for:**
- Scope 1 & 2 emissions (limited minimum, reasonable preferred)
- Green bond use of proceeds (reasonable assurance)

**RECOMMENDED assurance for:**
- Key social metrics (diversity, safety)
- Biodiversity impact data
