Executive Summary

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released international standard WIA-AUTO-010 covering vehicle infotainment (IVI) systems, including display technologies, audio systems, navigation integration, smartphone connectivity, voice assistants, and HMI design principles, under MIT license for free. This announcement positions Korea as a leader in vehicle infotainment international standards.

🚨 Current Situation: Why This Standard Is Needed

Modern vehicle infotainment systems provide different interfaces and functions for each manufacturer, causing confusion for drivers. Complex UI/UX causes driver distraction, and features such as smartphone integration, voice control, and multi-display management are not standardized, degrading both usability and safety. Particularly, unclear compliance with NHTSA guidelines poses serious threats to driver safety.

WIA's Solution: WIA-AUTO-010 Vehicle Infotainment

WIA-AUTO-010 provides a complete infotainment framework covering multi-screen layouts, touchscreen interfaces, immersive audio systems, real-time navigation, CarPlay/Android Auto integration, voice control, and HMI safety design.

🏢 About WIA

World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization with a mission to provide equal opportunities to all humanity through technology and certification.

WIA develops over 600 international standards in various fields including accessibility, security, AI, environment, and healthcare, and releases all standards as free open source.

🦀 Tech Stack: WIA's core APIs are developed in Rust. Rust is a systems programming language developed by Mozilla, the only language that achieves both memory safety and zero-cost abstractions.
