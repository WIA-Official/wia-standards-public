Key Features

💱

Automated Market Makers

Standardized AMM algorithms including constant product (x*y=k), concentrated liquidity, and stable swap formulas for efficient token exchanges.

💧

Liquidity Pools

Universal format for pool creation, management, and rewards distribution. Track TVL, APY, and impermanent loss across protocols.

🌾

Yield Farming

Standardized staking mechanisms, reward calculations, and yield optimization strategies. Multi-pool farming and auto-compounding support.

🏦

Lending Protocols

Unified interface for supplying, borrowing, and collateral management. Interest rate models and liquidation mechanisms.

⚡

Flash Loans

Atomic transaction protocols for uncollateralized loans. Arbitrage, liquidation, and collateral swapping capabilities.

🗳️

Governance

Decentralized governance standards including proposal creation, voting mechanisms, and time-locked execution.

🔒

Security Standards

Audit requirements, access controls, emergency pause mechanisms, and best practices for smart contract security.

🌉

Cross-Chain

Bridge protocols and wrapped token standards for seamless asset transfers across different blockchain networks.

🧩

Composability

Money lego standards enabling protocols to integrate and build upon each other, creating complex DeFi strategies.
