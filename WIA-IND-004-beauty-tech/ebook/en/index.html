About This eBook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Beauty Technology Standards (WIA-IND-004). This eBook covers the complete spectrum of modern beauty technology, from AI-powered skin analysis to AR virtual try-ons, personalized recommendations, and smart device integration.

Guided by the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this standard aims to democratize beauty technology and make advanced skincare accessible to everyone, regardless of location, budget, or technical expertise.

Each chapter includes detailed explanations, practical examples, review questions, and forward-looking perspectives to help you understand and implement beauty tech solutions.
