About This Guide

Electronic waste (e-waste) represents one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally, with over 50 million metric tons generated annually. This comprehensive guide provides the technical framework, implementation strategies, and best practices for responsible e-waste management in compliance with international standards.

The WIA E-Waste Management Standard establishes a four-phase approach to transform electronic waste from an environmental liability into sustainable resource recovery, supporting circular economy principles and protecting human health and the environment.
