Chapter 8: Implementation & Certification

8.1 Phased Rollout Strategy

Phase 1 (Years 1-2): Single HVDC link (Gangwon-Wonsan, 500 MW capacity), pilot energy trading program, basic SCADA integration.

Phase 2 (Years 3-5): Additional HVDC terminals (2 GW total capacity), renewable energy sharing, advanced grid services.

Phase 3 (Years 6-10): Full grid integration (10+ GW exchange capacity), Northeast Asia Supergrid connection, carbon-neutral operation.

8.2 Certification Requirements

Level 1: Data format compliance (100% validation pass rate).

Level 2: API integration (functional tests, security audit).

Level 3: Protocol compliance (IEC 61850, DNP3 certification).

Level 4: Full system integration (grid code compliance, operational testing).

8.3 Case Study: Jeju Smart Grid

South Korea's Jeju Island serves as a model with 70%+ renewable penetration, advanced energy storage systems (100+ MW batteries), successful demand response programs. Lessons learned inform mainland integration strategy.

8.4 Success Metrics

Grid reliability (99.99% uptime), renewable energy share (60%+ by 2035), carbon emissions reduction (100+ million tons CO2/year), energy cost reduction (20%+ for consumers), economic development impact ($50B+ annual GDP contribution).
