Power Grid Unification Standard

WIA-UNI-007 Complete Guide

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-UNI-007 Power Grid Unification Standard, designed to enable seamless electricity sharing and renewable energy cooperation between North and South Korea through HVDC interconnections and smart grid technologies.

Built on the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this standard provides the technical foundation for energy security, carbon reduction, and shared prosperity through unified power infrastructure across the Korean Peninsula.
