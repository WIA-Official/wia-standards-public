Chapter 07: Digital Engineering

Digital engineering is transforming aerospace propulsion development, operation, and maintenance. Digital twins, predictive analytics, and AI-driven optimization enable unprecedented insights into engine behavior and performance. This chapter covers digital technologies per WIA-AEROSPACE_ENGINE standards.

7.1 Digital Twin Technology

A digital twin is a virtual representation of a physical engine that evolves throughout its lifecycle. It integrates design data, manufacturing records, operational parameters, and maintenance history to enable real-time monitoring and predictive capabilities.
