## 📊 Content Coverage

### Chapter Topics (8 Chapters)

1. **Aerospace Engine History** - Piston engines → jet revolution → turbofan era → modern GE9X
2. **Turbofan Principles** - Fan, compressor, combustor, turbine, FADEC systems
3. **Turboprop & Turboshaft** - PT6, AE 2100, free power turbines, helicopters
4. **Rocket Engines** - Liquid (Raptor, RS-25), solid (SRB), hybrid, electric propulsion
5. **Materials & Manufacturing** - Nickel superalloys, single crystals, TBC, CMC, 3D printing
6. **Engine Performance** - Thrust, SFC, thermal efficiency, emissions, noise
7. **Engine Certification** - FAR Part 33, EASA CS-E, ETOPS, continued airworthiness
8. **Future Propulsion** - Electric (batteries), hydrogen (ZEROe), SAF, nuclear

### Technical Specifications Included

- FAR Part 33 / EASA CS-E requirements
- ICAO CAEP emissions standards
- Engine architectures (turbofan, turboprop, rocket)
- Materials (superalloys Gen 1-4, CMC, CFRP)
- Performance metrics (SFC, OPR, BPR, NOx)
- Manufacturing (3D printing, precision casting, NDT)
- Future technologies (hydrogen, SAF, open rotor)
