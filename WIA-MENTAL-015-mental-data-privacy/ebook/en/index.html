About This eBook

Mental health data is among the most sensitive information we handle in healthcare. This comprehensive guide provides healthcare professionals, developers, and privacy officers with the knowledge and tools needed to implement robust privacy protections for mental health data. From understanding regulatory requirements to implementing technical controls, each chapter builds upon the last to create a complete framework for mental data privacy.

Through practical examples, real-world case studies, and actionable implementation guidance, you'll learn how to navigate the complex landscape of mental health privacy while maintaining the trust of those you serve.
