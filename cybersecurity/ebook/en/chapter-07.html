7.1 SOC Structure & Roles

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is the centralized unit that deals with security issues on an organizational and technical level. The SOC team is responsible for monitoring, detecting, investigating, and responding to cyber threats.

Role Level Responsibilities
SOC Analyst L1 Tier 1 Alert triage, initial investigation, escalation
SOC Analyst L2 Tier 2 Deep investigation, incident handling, correlation
SOC Analyst L3 Tier 3 Advanced analysis, threat hunting, malware analysis
Detection Engineer Engineering Detection rule development, tuning, automation
Threat Intel Analyst Intelligence CTI integration, actor tracking, reporting
SOC Manager Management Team leadership, metrics, process improvement

7.3 Alert Triage Process

Step Activity Time Target
1. Receipt Alert received in queue Immediate
2. Initial Review Quick assessment of alert details < 5 minutes
3. Context Gathering Enrich with additional data sources < 10 minutes
4. Classification True positive, false positive, or needs escalation < 15 minutes
5. Action Close, escalate, or initiate incident < 30 minutes

7.4 Incident Response Workflow

NIST IR Lifecycle

Incident response follows the NIST lifecycle: Preparation, Detection & Analysis, Containment, Eradication & Recovery, and Post-Incident Activity.

7.5 SOC Metrics & KPIs

Metric Description Target
MTTD Mean Time to Detect < 1 hour
MTTR Mean Time to Respond < 4 hours
MTTC Mean Time to Contain < 24 hours
False Positive Rate % of alerts that are FP < 30%
Alert Volume Alerts per analyst per day < 50
Coverage % of ATT&CK techniques detected > 70%

7.6 SOC Maturity Model

  • Level 1 - Initial: Ad hoc processes, reactive only
  • Level 2 - Managed: Defined processes, basic monitoring
  • Level 3 - Defined: Documented procedures, consistent execution
  • Level 4 - Quantitatively Managed: Metrics-driven, continuous improvement
  • Level 5 - Optimizing: Proactive hunting, automation, innovation

7.7 Chapter Summary

Key Takeaways

  • SOC requires tiered analyst structure with clear responsibilities
  • SIEM, SOAR, and EDR form the core technology stack
  • Alert triage should complete within 30 minutes
  • Incident severity determines response time and escalation
  • KPIs measure SOC effectiveness and guide improvement
