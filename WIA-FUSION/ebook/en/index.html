About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of the WIA-FUSION standard for nuclear fusion energy systems. From the fundamental physics of plasma confinement to practical implementation of AI control systems, this guide provides the technical depth needed to understand and implement fusion energy standards.

The WIA-FUSION standard aims to unify the fragmented landscape of fusion research by providing common data formats, APIs, protocols, and integration guidelines that work across all major reactor types and research institutions worldwide.
