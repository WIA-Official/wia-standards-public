About This Guide

This comprehensive guide explores the WIA-EDU-004 Learning Analytics standard, providing educators, administrators, data scientists, and educational technology professionals with the knowledge and tools to implement privacy-preserving, ethical, and effective learning analytics systems.

Through eight detailed chapters, you'll learn about data collection methods, analytics techniques, predictive modeling, privacy protection, implementation strategies, and real-world case studies that demonstrate the transformative power of learning analytics in education.
