Conformance, Versioning, and Open Governance

Implementations of the clinical-trial-data standard declare their conformance tier (Surface / Verified / Anchored) in the OpenAPI document via the x-wia-conformance-tier extension field. Auditors consume the field to determine review depth without re-running the full test matrix. The conformance package follows the CycloneDX 1.5 SBOM format and is signed using Sigstore (DSSE envelope, Rekor transparency log entry).

Versioning follows Semantic Versioning 2.0.0. Major bumps require at least a 90-day overlap window with the prior major version on every WIA-published reference implementation. Minor bumps are additive only; patch bumps are editorial. Deprecation enters a 12-month sunset window during which the registry marks the version as Deprecated with a migration note.

Issues, errata, and proposals are tracked at github.com/WIA-Official/wia-standards/issues with the clinical-trial-data label. The WIA Standards working group reviews open issues at the start of every minor release cycle and publishes the resulting decision log alongside the release notes.
