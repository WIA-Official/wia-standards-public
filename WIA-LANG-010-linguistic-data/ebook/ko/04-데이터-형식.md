# 제4장: 데이터 형식

## 개요

> **弘益人間 (홍익인간)** - 널리 인간을 이롭게 하라

이 장에서는 WIA-LANG-010 표준의 핵심인 데이터 형식을 상세히 다룹니다. 코퍼스(Corpora), 어휘 데이터베이스(Lexical Databases), 문법 자원(Grammar Resources)의 구조와 사용법을 실제 예제와 함께 설명합니다.

## 1. 코퍼스 형식 (Corpus Format)

### 1.1 기본 구조

```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<linguisticData xmlns="http://wia.org/lang-010/v1.0"
                xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance"
                xsi:schemaLocation="http://wia.org/lang-010/v1.0 wia-lang-010.xsd"
                version="1.0.0">

  <!-- 메타데이터 -->
  <metadata>
    <language iso639-3="kor" name="Korean" script="Hangul"/>
    <title>Korean News Corpus 2025</title>
    <creator>
      <name>Seoul National University</name>
      <email>corpus@snu.ac.kr</email>
    </creator>
    <created>2025-01-15</created>
    <license type="CC-BY-SA-4.0"
             url="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/"/>
    <size documents="1000" sentences="25000" tokens="500000"/>
  </metadata>

  <!-- 코퍼스 본문 -->
  <corpus>
    <document id="doc001">
      <metadata>
        <source>신문사 A</source>
        <date>2025-01-20</date>
        <genre>news</genre>

      <sentence id="s001">
        <text>인공지능 기술이 빠르게 발전하고 있다.</text>

        <!-- 토큰 계층 -->

        <morphology>
          <token ref="t001">
            <morpheme form="인공" lemma="인공" tag="NNG" gloss="artificial"/>

            <morpheme form="기술" lemma="기술" tag="NNG" gloss="technology"/>
            <morpheme form="이" lemma="이" tag="JKS" gloss="NOM"/>
          </token>
          <token ref="t003">
            <morpheme form="빠르" lemma="빠르다" tag="VA" gloss="fast"/>
            <morpheme form="게" lemma="게" tag="EC" gloss="ADV"/>
          </token>
          <token ref="t004">
            <morpheme form="발전" lemma="발전" tag="NNG" gloss="development"/>
            <morpheme form="하" lemma="하다" tag="VV" gloss="do"/>
            <morpheme form="고" lemma="고" tag="EC" gloss="CONN"/>
          </token>
          <token ref="t005">
            <morpheme form="있" lemma="있다" tag="VX" gloss="exist"/>
            <morpheme form="다" lemma="다" tag="EF" gloss="DEC"/>
          </token>
        </morphology>

        <!-- 품사 태깅 -->
        <posTagging tagset="Universal">
          <token ref="t001" pos="NOUN" xpos="NNG"/>
          <token ref="t002" pos="NOUN" xpos="NNG+JKS"/>
          <token ref="t003" pos="ADV" xpos="VA+EC"/>
          <token ref="t004" pos="VERB" xpos="NNG+VV+EC"/>
          <token ref="t005" pos="AUX" xpos="VX+EF"/>
          <token ref="t006" pos="PUNCT" xpos="SF"/>
        </posTagging>

        <!-- 의존 구문 분석 -->
        <dependencies>
          <dep head="t002" dependent="t001" relation="nmod"/>
          <dep head="t004" dependent="t002" relation="nsubj"/>
          <dep head="t004" dependent="t003" relation="advmod"/>
          <dep head="t005" dependent="t004" relation="aux"/>
          <dep head="t005" dependent="t006" relation="punct"/>
        </dependencies>

        <!-- 개체명 인식 -->
        <entities>
          <entity id="e001" type="TECHNOLOGY" start="0" end="4">
            <tokens>t001</tokens>
            <text>인공지능</text>
            <confidence>0.95</confidence>
          </entity>
        </entities>

      </sentence>
    </document>
  </corpus>

</linguisticData>
```

### 1.3 CoNLL-U 호환 형식

Universal Dependencies와의 호환성을 위해 CoNLL-U 형식 변환을 지원합니다:

```
# sent_id = s001

# text_en = AI technology is developing rapidly.

2	기술이	기술	NOUN	NNG+JKS	_	4	nsubj	_	Gloss=technology-NOM
3	빠르게	빠르다	ADV	VA+EC	_	4	advmod	_	Gloss=fast-ADV
4	발전하고	발전하다	VERB	NNG+VV+EC	_	5	aux	_	Gloss=develop-CONN
5	있다	있다	AUX	VX+EF	_	0	root	_	Gloss=exist-DEC|SpaceAfter=No
6	.	.	PUNCT	SF	_	5	punct	_	_
```

  <alignments>

### 3.2 의존 문법 (Dependency Grammar)

```yaml
dependency_grammar:
  language: kor
  version: "1.0"

  # 의존 관계 규칙
  dependency_rules:
    - head_pos: VERB
      dependent_pos: NOUN
      relation: nsubj
      direction: left  # 한국어는 주어가 동사 왼쪽
      conditions:
        - particle: ["이", "가"]
      examples:
        - "학생이 공부한다"
        - "고양이가 달린다"

    - head_pos: VERB
      dependent_pos: NOUN
      relation: obj
      direction: left  # 목적어도 동사 왼쪽
      conditions:
        - particle: ["을", "를"]
      examples:
        - "책을 읽는다"
        - "밥을 먹는다"

    - head_pos: NOUN
      dependent_pos: NOUN
      relation: nmod
      direction: left
      conditions:
        - particle: ["의"]
      examples:
        - "학생의 책"
        - "서울의 날씨"

    - head_pos: VERB
      dependent_pos: ADV
      relation: advmod
      direction: left
      examples:
        - "빨리 달린다"
        - "조용히 말한다"

  # 제약 조건
  constraints:
    - name: "single_root"
      description: "문장은 하나의 root만 가져야 함"
      rule: "count(dependencies.filter(d => d.head == null)) == 1"

    - name: "no_cycles"
      description: "의존 관계에 순환이 없어야 함"
      rule: "!has_cycle(dependencies)"

    - name: "projectivity"
      description: "교차 의존 관계 금지 (한국어는 비교적 투사적)"
      rule: "is_projective(dependencies)"
      strict: false  # 예외 허용

  # 발화 패턴
  valency_patterns:
    - verb: "주다"
      pattern:
        agent: {pos: "NOUN", particle: ["이", "가"], required: true}
        theme: {pos: "NOUN", particle: ["을", "를"], required: true}
        recipient: {pos: "NOUN", particle: ["에게", "한테"], required: true}
      example: "철수가 영희에게 책을 주었다"

    - verb: "가다"
      pattern:
        agent: {pos: "NOUN", particle: ["이", "가"], required: true}
        goal: {pos: "NOUN", particle: ["에", "으로"], required: false}
      example: "학생이 학교에 간다"
```
