Core Features

💓

Heart Rate Monitoring

Continuous heart rate tracking with advanced PPG sensors. Real-time BPM monitoring, resting heart rate analysis, heart rate variability (HRV) measurement, and cardio zone detection for optimized workout performance and cardiovascular health insights.

🏃

Activity Tracking

Comprehensive activity recognition for 50+ exercise types including walking, running, cycling, swimming, and strength training. Automatic activity detection, step counting, distance calculation, calorie burn estimation, and active minutes tracking throughout the day.

😴

Sleep Analysis

Advanced sleep stage detection using accelerometer and heart rate data. Track light sleep, deep sleep, REM cycles, and awake periods. Generate sleep quality scores, identify sleep patterns, and receive personalized recommendations for better rest and recovery.

🏋️

Workout Detection

Intelligent workout recognition and tracking across cardio, strength, sports, and custom activities. Real-time performance metrics, rep counting for strength exercises, interval training support, workout intensity zones, and detailed post-workout analytics and recovery recommendations.

🗺️

GPS Tracking

High-precision GPS, GLONASS, and Galileo satellite tracking for outdoor activities. Route mapping, pace tracking, elevation gain/loss, distance accuracy, and integration with popular fitness apps for comprehensive outdoor workout analysis and route sharing.

Health Insights

AI-powered health analytics combining all sensor data for comprehensive wellness insights. Stress level monitoring, VO2 max estimation, fitness age calculation, training load analysis, recovery recommendations, and personalized health trends with actionable guidance.

4-Phase Architecture

1

Data Format

Standardized sensor data schemas, biometric formats, activity logs, and health metrics structure for universal compatibility across all fitness wearable devices and platforms.

2

API Interface

Comprehensive device APIs, data synchronization protocols, health platform integrations, and developer SDKs for seamless wearable data access and third-party application development.

3

Protocol

BLE and ANT+ communication protocols, data compression algorithms, battery optimization strategies, and real-time streaming protocols for efficient wearable device connectivity and data transmission.

4

Integration

Platform integrations with Apple Health, Google Fit, Strava, health insurance systems, and medical record platforms for comprehensive health data ecosystem connectivity.
