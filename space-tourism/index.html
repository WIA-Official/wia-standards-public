WIA-SPACE-014

Space Tourism

우주 관광 — Standardised passenger spaceflight credentials, safety, accessibility and operations.

Why a tourism-grade standard?

Civil spaceflight is graduating from one-off charters to recurring passenger operations. The standard covers the whole passenger journey: medical eligibility, training records, suit and seat fit, on-orbit duty roster, emergency egress drills, and post-flight debriefing. A WIA-SPACE-014 conformant operator can hand its passenger manifest to any partner operator and have the data interpreted unambiguously, including across language barriers. The same data shape is read by ground rescue teams, insurance carriers and regulators.

About This Standard

  • Comprehensive guide to space tourism industry and operations
  • Coverage of suborbital, LEO, and lunar tourism options
  • Safety regulations, training requirements, and certification standards
  • Analysis of major space tourism companies and their offerings
  • Space hotel concepts and future tourism infrastructure
  • Cost structures and accessibility considerations
  • Environmental and ethical implications of space tourism
  • Future trends: lunar tourism, Mars tours, and beyond
