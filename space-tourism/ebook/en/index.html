Comprehensive Guide to Commercial Space Tourism (2025-2026)

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide provides an in-depth analysis of the commercial space tourism industry as of 2025-2026. It covers all major providers including Virgin Galactic's Delta-class restart, Blue Origin's New Shepard missions, and SpaceX's orbital tourism programs. Each chapter includes real mission data, pricing information, technical specifications, regulatory frameworks, and future projections based on actual industry developments.

WIA Philosophy: 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity)

The WIA-SPACE-TOURISM standard embodies the principle of "弘益人間" (홍익인간) - benefiting all humanity. Space tourism represents humanity's expansion beyond Earth, making space accessible not just to professional astronauts but to ordinary citizens. This democratization of space access drives technological innovation, economic growth, and inspires future generations to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), ultimately benefiting all of humanity.
