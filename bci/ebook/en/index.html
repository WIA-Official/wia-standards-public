WIA-BCI v1.0

Brain-Computer Interface Standards

A comprehensive framework for the development, implementation, and governance of brain-computer interface technologies that prioritize human welfare, safety, and ethical considerations.

About This Standard

The WIA-BCI (World Certification Industry Association - Brain-Computer Interface) standard establishes a unified framework for BCI technology development, ensuring interoperability, safety, and ethical compliance across all implementations.

Mission Statement: To create a world where brain-computer interfaces enhance human capabilities while preserving individual autonomy, privacy, and dignity. Our standards ensure that BCI technology serves humanity's best interests through rigorous safety protocols, ethical guidelines, and transparent governance structures.

Core Objectives

  • Establish universal safety standards for neural interface devices
  • Define ethical guidelines for neural data collection and processing
  • Create interoperability protocols for BCI systems worldwide
  • Protect user privacy and cognitive liberty
  • Promote equitable access to BCI technologies
  • Foster international cooperation in BCI research

Technical Specifications

WIA-BCI Standard Specification v1.0
=====================================

SIGNAL_ACQUISITION:
  sampling_rate: 1000-30000 Hz
  resolution: 16-24 bit
  channels: 1-1024
  latency: <10ms

DATA_FORMAT:
  container: WIA-Neural-Container (WNC)
  compression: lossless
  encryption: AES-256-GCM
  metadata: ISO-11179 compliant

SAFETY_THRESHOLDS:
  max_current: 50μA (cortical)
  max_charge_density: 30μC/cm²
  thermal_limit: 0.5°C rise
  stimulation_frequency: 1-300 Hz

CERTIFICATION_LEVELS:
  - Level 1: Non-invasive consumer devices
  - Level 2: Non-invasive medical devices
  - Level 3: Minimally invasive implants
  - Level 4: Fully implantable systems

Why WIA-BCI Matters

Brain-computer interfaces represent one of the most transformative technologies of our era. Without proper standards, we risk fragmented development, safety incidents, privacy violations, and inequitable access. WIA-BCI provides the foundation for responsible innovation that benefits all of humanity.

Getting Started

To begin implementing WIA-BCI standards in your organization:

  1. Review all chapters thoroughly to understand the complete framework
  2. Assess your current BCI systems against WIA-BCI requirements
  3. Develop a compliance roadmap with your technical and legal teams
  4. Implement safety protocols and privacy protections
  5. Apply for WIA-BCI certification through authorized bodies
  6. Maintain ongoing compliance through regular audits
