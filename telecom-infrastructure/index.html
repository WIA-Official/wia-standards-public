Telecommunications Infrastructure Standard

Purpose: To establish global standards for modern telecommunications infrastructure that ensures reliable, high-speed connectivity for all.

Key Areas:

  • 5G and 6G network architecture and deployment
  • Fiber optic infrastructure standards
  • Cell tower placement and coverage optimization
  • Network resilience and disaster recovery
  • Rural and underserved area connectivity
  • Spectrum allocation and management

Philosophy: 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit All Humanity" through universal, reliable telecommunications access.
