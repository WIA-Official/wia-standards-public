【 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY 】

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the next-generation virtualization technology standard 'WIA-COMP-007: Virtualization Standard' under the MIT license for free public use. This standard provides a complete enterprise-grade virtualization framework including Type-1/Type-2 hypervisors, hardware virtualization, memory management, live migration, and GPU virtualization. It includes TypeScript SDK, CLI tools, and detailed specification documents, available for unrestricted use by developers and enterprises worldwide.

The Chronic Problem of Data Centers: Resource Waste and Inefficiency

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problems with Traditional Server Infrastructure]

Data centers across the Republic of Korea and worldwide suffer from severe resource waste. The average CPU utilization of typical physical servers is only 15-20%, with over 80% of resources sitting idle while consuming power.

Key Issues:

  • Resource Waste: Average 15% utilization per server, 85% resources idle
  • High Costs: Unnecessary hardware purchase and maintenance expenses
  • Power Waste: Continuous power consumption from underutilized servers
  • Space Problems: Data center rack space shortage
  • Management Complexity: Individual management of hundreds of physical servers
  • Lack of Scalability: Weeks required for new workload deployment
  • Disaster Recovery Difficulties: Long recovery times during hardware failures
"Every enterprise has the right to utilize their hardware resources as efficiently as possible. They should be able to safely isolate and run dozens of workloads on a single physical server and migrate them to another host within seconds during failures. Virtualization is not optional—it's essential."
- Sangmin Park, CEO, SmileStory Inc. / WIA

WIA-COMP-007: Complete Virtualization Solution

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_3: Future of WIA Virtualization Solution]

The WIA-COMP-007 standard provides an enterprise-grade virtualization framework that solves these problems:

Core Features:

  • Hypervisor Support: Full support for Type-1 (bare-metal) and Type-2 (hosted)
  • Hardware Virtualization: Utilizing Intel VT-x, AMD-V, EPT/NPT
  • Memory Management: Ballooning, page sharing (KSM), overcommit
  • I/O Virtualization: SR-IOV, device emulation, paravirtual drivers
  • Live Migration: Zero-downtime VM movement
  • Snapshot & Clone: VM state preservation and duplication
  • Resource Allocation: CPU pinning, NUMA awareness, QoS
  • GPU Virtualization: vGPU, GPU passthrough
  • Network Virtualization: Virtual switches, VLANs, overlays

About WIA

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) is an international standards organization dedicated to researching, developing, and distributing next-generation technology standards. Based on the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), WIA releases all technology standards under the MIT license for free use by developers and enterprises worldwide.

WIA is developing over 600 standards, from Rust-based high-performance systems to the TypeScript/JavaScript ecosystem. All standards include production-ready SDKs, CLIs, and detailed documentation.

Major Standard Families: WIA-INTENT (Intent-based Computing), WIA-OMNI-API (Unified API), WIA-COMP (Computing), WIA-CLOUD (Cloud), WIA-AI (Artificial Intelligence), WIA-BLOCKCHAIN (Blockchain), and more
