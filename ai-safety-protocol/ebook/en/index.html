About This Standard

The WIA AI Safety Protocol provides a comprehensive framework for implementing safe, secure, and trustworthy AI systems. Developed by the World Certification Industry Association, this standard is released free and open-source under MIT License to benefit all humanity.

This standard addresses the complete AI safety lifecycle: from risk assessment and security implementation through monitoring, governance, testing, and human oversight. It incorporates guidance from NIST, EU AI Act, ISO standards, and leading industry practices.
