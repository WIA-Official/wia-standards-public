In the digital age, our lives extend far beyond physical possessions. From email accounts to cryptocurrency wallets, from social media profiles to cloud storage, we accumulate vast digital estates that require careful planning and management—especially for what happens after we're gone.

The WIA-DIGITAL_EXECUTOR standard provides a comprehensive framework for digital estate planning, posthumous account management, and the secure transfer of digital assets to designated beneficiaries. This guide covers everything from password vaults and cryptocurrency inheritance to social media legacy and legal frameworks for digital executors.
