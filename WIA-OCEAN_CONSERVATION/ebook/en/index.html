Welcome to Ocean Conservation

Our oceans cover 71% of Earth's surface and contain 97% of the planet's water. They are home to an incredible diversity of life, regulate our climate, produce over half of the world's oxygen, and provide food and livelihoods for billions of people. Yet, our oceans face unprecedented threats from overfishing, pollution, climate change, and habitat destruction.

This comprehensive guide introduces the WIA-OCEAN_CONSERVATION standard, a unified framework for marine conservation efforts worldwide. Whether you're a marine biologist, conservation manager, policy maker, or concerned citizen, this guide will equip you with the knowledge and tools to protect our oceans for future generations.

Through eight detailed chapters, you'll learn about marine ecosystems, conservation strategies, monitoring technologies, enforcement mechanisms, and the critical role each of us plays in ocean conservation. The philosophy of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) reminds us that protecting our oceans benefits everyone - present and future generations alike.

About This Guide

This ebook is part of the WIA-OCEAN_CONSERVATION standard, developed to provide a comprehensive framework for ocean conservation efforts worldwide. Each chapter includes detailed explanations, real-world examples, review questions, and key takeaways to reinforce learning.

Target Audience: Marine biologists, conservation managers, policy makers, environmental scientists, students, educators, and anyone passionate about ocean conservation.

Learning Objectives: By the end of this guide, you will understand the major threats to ocean health, effective conservation strategies, monitoring and enforcement technologies, and how to contribute to marine conservation efforts.

Prerequisites: No prior knowledge of marine biology or conservation is required. This guide is designed to be accessible to all readers while providing depth for professionals.
