WIA Announces Solid-State Battery Standard - The Future of Energy Storage

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has announced the Solid-State Battery standard, a core next-generation energy storage technology. This standard enables revolutionary advances in safety, energy density, and charging speed, redefining the future of electric vehicles and energy storage systems.

Paradigm Shift in Battery Technology

The Republic of Korea is a frontrunner in solid-state battery technology development. WIA's solid-state battery standard encompasses solid electrolytes, lithium metal anodes, ionic conductivity optimization, and safety protocols, surpassing the limitations of conventional lithium-ion batteries. Energy density improves by up to 2x, and fire risk approaches nearly zero.

Key Features:
  • High Energy Density: Up to 2x energy storage capacity compared to lithium-ion
  • Ultra-fast Charging: 80% charge in under 10 minutes
  • Extreme Safety: Solid electrolyte minimizes fire risk
  • Long Lifespan: Over 10,000 charge-discharge cycles
  • Wide Operating Temperature: Stable operation from -40°C to 80°C

Game Changer for Electric Vehicles

Solid-state batteries extend electric vehicle range beyond 1,000km and dramatically reduce charging time. Weight reduction also improves vehicle efficiency, and stable operation in extreme temperatures enables use anywhere in the world. This is the key technology that will accelerate EV adoption.

弘益人間 - Benefit All Humanity

WIA Chairman Yeon Sam-heum stated, "The solid-state battery standard opens the future of safe and sustainable energy storage." He added, "In the spirit of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), this technology will provide safer electric vehicles and clean energy to everyone and contribute to protecting our planet."

"Energy is the foundation of modern civilization. The solid-state battery standard opens the path for humanity to completely move away from fossil fuels toward a sustainable future through safer and more efficient energy storage."

Application Across Industries

Solid-state batteries apply not only to electric vehicles but also to drones, electric aircraft, large-scale Energy Storage Systems (ESS), and portable electronics. The WIA standard provides TypeScript SDK and simulation tools to help manufacturers easily implement and verify the technology.
