WIA-ENE-015 Phase 2: System Integration

Phase 2 Overview

This phase covers the essential requirements and processes for achieving WIA-ENE-015 zero energy building certification. Phase 2 focuses on system integration aspects of the project.

Key Requirements

  • Comprehensive energy modeling demonstrating net-zero annual energy balance
  • High-performance building envelope (R-40+ walls, R-60+ roof, 0.6 ACH50 air tightness)
  • Efficient HVAC systems (SEER 18+, HSPF 10+, or equivalent)
  • 100% LED lighting with controls
  • On-site renewable energy generation (typically solar PV) ≥100% of annual consumption
  • Energy storage system (≥20% of daily solar generation or 4 hours critical loads)
  • Building Energy Management System (BEMS) with comprehensive monitoring
  • Commissioning and performance verification

Documentation

Phase 2 requires comprehensive documentation including:

  • Architectural and engineering drawings
  • Energy modeling reports (PHPP or equivalent)
  • Equipment specifications and submittals
  • Testing and commissioning reports
  • Performance monitoring data
  • As-built documentation

Phase 2 Completion

Successful completion of Phase 2 advances the project to the next stage of WIA-ENE-015 certification. All requirements must be met and documented before proceeding.
