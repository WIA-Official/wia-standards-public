About This E-Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Wearable Fashion technology. This e-book explores the intersection of fashion and technology, providing in-depth knowledge on smart accessories, embedded electronics, and the future of fashion-tech integration.

Based on the WIA-IND-003 standard, this guide serves professionals, designers, engineers, and enthusiasts who want to understand and implement wearable fashion solutions.

Philosophy: 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity) - Every technology we create should serve humanity's collective wellbeing and progress.
