Core Features

🎨

Arts & Performances

Comprehensive cataloging of inter-Korean artistic exchanges including traditional music, dance, theater performances, exhibitions, and contemporary art collaborations with detailed metadata.

⚽

Sports Events

Track unified teams, athletic exchanges, joint training programs, friendly matches, and Olympic cooperation with athlete profiles, statistics, and historical records.

🎵

Music Collaborations

Document musical exchanges from traditional Korean folk music to modern K-pop collaborations, joint concerts, music festivals, and cultural performances across the DMZ.

🎉

Festival Management

Manage joint cultural festivals, traditional celebrations, modern cultural events, and special commemorative occasions with participant tracking and event analytics.

🏛️

Heritage Preservation

Protect and document shared Korean cultural heritage including UNESCO sites, traditional crafts, historical artifacts, intangible cultural properties, and restoration projects.

📚

Educational Exchanges

Support cultural education programs, artist residencies, academic conferences, student exchanges, and collaborative research on Korean cultural traditions.

Use Cases

🎭 Joint Theater Productions

Coordinate inter-Korean theater collaborations, traditional Pansori performances, modern drama productions, and cultural festivals with artist management, scheduling, and audience analytics.

⚽ Unified Sports Teams

Manage joint Korean sports delegations for Olympics, Asian Games, and World Championships including athlete profiles, training schedules, competition results, and media coverage.

🎵 K-Culture Collaborations

Enable music collaborations between North and South Korean artists, joint concerts, music production projects, and cultural festivals promoting shared Korean musical heritage.

🏛️ UNESCO Heritage Projects

Document and preserve shared Korean cultural heritage sites, traditional crafts, and intangible cultural properties with joint conservation efforts and international recognition.

📺 Media & Broadcasting

Facilitate joint documentary productions, cultural program exchanges, historical archival projects, and broadcast cooperation for education and cultural awareness.

🎨 Art Exhibitions

Organize joint art exhibitions, cultural showcases, museum collaborations, and traveling exhibitions featuring traditional and contemporary Korean art from both regions.
