Executive Summary

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) has released the international standard WIA-QUA-001 for quantum chip under free MIT license. This announcement marks Korea's leadership in international standards for this field.

🚨 The Challenge: Why This Standard is Needed

The quantum chip field faces interoperability issues due to lack of standardization. Proprietary vendor systems make integration difficult and create serious compatibility problems. This increases development costs and hinders innovation.

[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_2: Problem Visualization Image]

Yeon Sam-heum, CEO:
"Quantum Chip can no longer be solved independently by each company. Interoperable standards are essential infrastructure to accelerate industry innovation. This is beyond a technology issue — it's a matter of human progress."

WIA's Solution: WIA-QUA-001

WIA-QUA-001 standardizes all core elements of quantum chip, providing complete interoperability and innovative performance.
