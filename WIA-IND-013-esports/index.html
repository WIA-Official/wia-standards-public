Core Features

Comprehensive standards for every aspect of modern e-sports platforms

🏆

Tournament Management

Standardized formats for single/double elimination, round-robin, Swiss system, and custom brackets. Support for seeding algorithms, scheduling, and real-time bracket updates.

Player Statistics

Unified data models for player profiles, match history, performance metrics, and skill ratings. Cross-game analytics with ELO, MMR, and custom ranking systems.

📡

Live Streaming

Protocols for real-time game state broadcasting, spectator overlays, replay systems, and multi-platform streaming integration. Low-latency data sync for commentators and analysts.

👥

Team Management

Standards for team rosters, contracts, transfers, and organizational structures. Integration with player databases, sponsorship tracking, and performance analytics.

Spectator Experience

Interactive viewing features including player POV selection, stat overlays, prediction markets, and social engagement tools. Mobile and desktop optimized interfaces.

🔒

Anti-Cheat & Fair Play

Verification protocols for match integrity, player identity, and hardware/software compliance. Integration with anti-cheat systems, replay analysis, and automated suspicious activity detection.
