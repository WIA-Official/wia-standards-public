## WIA-URBAN-PLANNING Standard

This ebook introduces the WIA-URBAN-PLANNING standard, a comprehensive framework for:

1. **Assessment**: Evaluating current urban conditions
2. **Planning**: Developing integrated sustainable plans
3. **Implementation**: Executing with community engagement
4. **Monitoring**: Tracking progress with KPIs
5. **Adaptation**: Adjusting strategies based on data

The standard is:
- **Universal**: Applicable to cities of all sizes
- **Flexible**: Adaptable to local contexts
- **Evidence-Based**: Grounded in research
- **Technology-Enabled**: Leveraging digital tools
- **People-Centered**: Prioritizing human wellbeing
