Standard Overview

The WIA-PREDICTIVE_MAINTENANCE standard provides a comprehensive framework for implementing intelligent equipment monitoring and predictive analytics to eliminate unplanned downtime, optimize maintenance schedules, and extend asset lifecycles. From sensor specifications to machine learning model requirements, this standard ensures interoperability and quality across predictive maintenance solutions.

Organizations implementing this standard typically achieve 25-40% reduction in maintenance costs, 70-90% decrease in unexpected failures, and significant improvements in overall equipment effectiveness (OEE). The standard supports Industry 4.0 initiatives and enables the transition from calendar-based to condition-based maintenance strategies.

Certification Levels

Bronze Basic condition monitoring, single asset type, threshold-based alerts
Silver Multi-asset monitoring, ML-based anomaly detection, integrated CMMS
Gold Enterprise-wide deployment, RUL prediction, automated work order generation
Platinum Digital twin integration, autonomous maintenance scheduling, prescriptive analytics
