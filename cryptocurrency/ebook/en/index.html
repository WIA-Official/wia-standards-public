About WIA

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) develops open standards for emerging technologies, promoting interoperability, security, and accessibility. Founded on the principle of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), WIA brings together developers, enterprises, and regulators to create standards that serve everyone.

WIA-FIN-003 is part of a broader family of standards including WIA-INTENT (Intent Expression), WIA-OMNI-API (Universal API), WIA-AIR-POWER (Resource Sharing), and WIA-SOCIAL (Social Integration). Together, these standards create a comprehensive framework for the decentralized future.
