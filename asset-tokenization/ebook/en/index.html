About This Ebook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to the WIA-FIN-008 Asset Tokenization Standard. This ebook provides complete coverage of real-world asset tokenization—from foundational concepts to production-ready systems for tokenizing real estate, art, commodities, and securities.

Developed by the World Certification Industry Association (WIA), this standard embodies the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)—benefiting all humanity through accessible, secure, and compliant asset tokenization systems. Whether you're tokenizing real estate, launching a security token offering, or building a fractional ownership platform, WIA-FIN-008 provides proven patterns for success.

About WIA

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) develops open standards for emerging technologies, promoting interoperability, security, and accessibility. Founded on the principle of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), WIA brings together developers, enterprises, and regulators to create standards that serve everyone.

WIA-FIN-008 is part of the broader WIA Finance family including WIA-FIN-002 (Blockchain Finance) and WIA-FIN-003 (Cryptocurrency). Together, these standards create a comprehensive framework for the tokenized economy.
