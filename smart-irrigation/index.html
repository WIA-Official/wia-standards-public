Intelligent Water Management for Agriculture

Global standard for smart irrigation systems, drip irrigation automation, water scheduling, and soil moisture-based control. Conserving water while maximizing crop yields.

💧 What is WIA-AGRI-007?

The WIA-AGRI-007 Smart Irrigation Standard provides a comprehensive framework for implementing intelligent water management systems in agriculture. This standard addresses critical challenges: water scarcity, inefficient irrigation practices, rising energy costs, and the need to maximize crop yields while minimizing water consumption.

Category: AGRI Status: Active Version: 1.0 Color: Lime #84CC16

This standard encompasses evapotranspiration calculation, drip irrigation automation, precision water scheduling, soil moisture monitoring, weather-based control, and water efficiency certification. It provides practical tools, algorithms, and protocols for farmers, irrigation engineers, equipment manufacturers, and water management authorities.
