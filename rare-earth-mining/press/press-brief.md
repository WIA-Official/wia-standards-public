# WIA-ENE-041 — Rare Earth Mining (Press Brief)

**WIA Standards releases canonical Rare Earth Mining envelope shape**

The WIA Standards Committee has published the WIA-ENE-041 standard covering the canonical envelope shape, API surface, protocol exchanges, and ecosystem integration for rare earth mining hosts. The standard composes with the wider WIA Standards family for cross-standard audit transport, identity, and federation.
