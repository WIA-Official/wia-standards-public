Welcome to WIA Digital Twin City Standard

The comprehensive standard for building, managing, and operating digital twin cities. This guide provides detailed technical specifications, implementation guidelines, and best practices for creating interconnected urban digital ecosystems.

Version: 2.0.0 | Release Date: December 2025

Standard ID: WIA-DTC-2025
