# WIA-ENE-003: Carbon Capture & Storage Standard
## PHASE 3: Construction and Integration

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Published  
**Date:** 2025-12-25  
**Philosophy:** 弘益人間 (홍익인간) - Benefit All Humanity

---

## 1. Overview

Phase 3 covers construction, equipment installation, system integration, and commissioning. Duration: 24-36 months. Budget: 70-80% of CAPEX.

## 2. Construction Execution

### 2.1 Site Preparation (Months 1-6)
- Site clearing and grading
- Foundations for major equipment
- Utility installation
- Access roads and laydown areas

### 2.2 Equipment Installation (Months 6-20)
- Major vessels (absorber, stripper)
- Heat exchangers
- CO₂ compressors
- Electrical and instrumentation

### 2.3 Pipeline Construction (Concurrent)
- Right-of-way preparation
- Pipeline welding and coating
- Hydrostatic testing
- Cathodic protection installation

## 3. Quality Assurance/Quality Control

### 3.1 Inspection and Testing
- Material certifications
- Weld inspection (100% for critical welds)
- Pressure testing (all vessels and piping)
- Electrical continuity and insulation testing
- Instrument calibration

### 3.2 Safety Management
- Daily safety meetings
- Incident reporting and investigation
- Safety training for all personnel
- Target: Zero LTIs (Lost Time Injuries)

## 4. Commissioning and Start-up

### 4.1 Pre-Commissioning (Months 20-24)
- System cleaning and flushing
- Leak testing
- Instrument loop checking
- Control system testing

### 4.2 Commissioning (Months 24-28)
- Subsystem functional tests
- Integrated system testing
- Solvent charging
- First CO₂ capture

### 4.3 Performance Testing (Months 28-30)
- Guarantee test runs
- Efficiency verification
- Environmental compliance testing
- Operator training

### 4.4 Reliability Run (Month 30-31)
- 30-day continuous operation
- Achieve 95% availability
- Document all issues and resolutions

## 5. System Integration

### 5.1 Integration with Host Facility
- Flue gas duct connections
- Steam supply integration
- Cooling water systems
- Electrical interconnection

### 5.2 Monitoring Systems
- SCADA implementation
- Data historian configuration
- Alarm and event management
- Compliance reporting systems

## 6. Training and Knowledge Transfer

### 6.1 Operations Training
- 6-month curriculum
- Classroom and hands-on training
- Simulator-based training
- Emergency response drills

### 6.2 Maintenance Training
- Equipment-specific training
- Preventive maintenance procedures
- Spare parts management
- Troubleshooting guides

## 7. Phase 3 Deliverables

- Fully constructed and tested facility
- Performance guarantees demonstrated
- Operating procedures documented
- Trained operations and maintenance staff
- Regulatory inspections passed
- Commercial operations date (COD) achieved

## 8. Handover to Operations

**Requirements for Operational Handover:**
- ✓ All punch list items closed
- ✓ Performance guarantees met
- ✓ As-built drawings delivered
- ✓ Spare parts inventory established
- ✓ Operations and maintenance manuals complete
- ✓ Warranty period commenced
