📖 About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the WIA-FIN-004 Digital Currency Standard, a universal framework for implementing, managing, and regulating digital currencies across all types including CBDCs, stablecoins, e-money, virtual currencies, and cryptocurrencies. Whether you're a central bank, fintech company, payment processor, or developer, this standard provides the technical specifications, best practices, and regulatory guidance needed to build robust digital currency systems.

Complete coverage of CBDCs, stablecoins, e-money, virtual currencies, and more
