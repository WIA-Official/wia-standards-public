🧭 Four Pillars

🚛 Collection

Bin-level events — fill level, weight, pickup time, hauler ID, route segment.

🔄 Sorting

Material composition in percent, contamination score, sort confidence.

♻️ Recycling

End-of-life routing, energy recovery yield, avoided CO₂e.

☠️ Hazardous

UN hazard class, handling reqs, disposal cert hash.
