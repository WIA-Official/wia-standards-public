2.2 Safety Concerns

Physical AI introduces unprecedented safety challenges:

⚠️ Incident Categories

Category Risk Level Examples
Physical Harm CRITICAL Collision, crushing, falling
Property Damage HIGH Object destruction, structural damage
System Failure HIGH Sudden shutdown, uncontrolled fall
Security Breach HIGH Remote hijacking, malicious control
Psychological MEDIUM Fear, distress, over-attachment

📈 Industrial Robot Incident Statistics

  • 2,000+ industrial robot injuries annually (US alone)
  • 40+ deaths from robot incidents per year globally
  • $2.5B annual cost of robot-related workplace injuries
  • 85% of incidents caused by lack of safety protocols

Warning: Consumer embodied AI operates in unpredictable home environments with children, elderly, and pets — far more dangerous than controlled industrial settings.

2.3 Ethical Dilemmas

Embodied AI raises profound ethical questions that no existing framework adequately addresses:

🤔 Key Ethical Questions

Question Stakes
Does embodied AI have rights? Legal personhood, protection from destruction
Who is liable for AI actions? Manufacturer, owner, AI itself?
Can AI refuse dangerous orders? Autonomy vs compliance
How to handle AI "death"? Shutdown, memory erasure, body disposal
Is human-AI romance ethical? Consent, manipulation, social impact

2.5 The Need for Unified Standards

Without comprehensive standards, the embodied AI industry faces:

❌ Risks Without Standards

  • Fragmentation — Every company creates incompatible systems
  • Racing to bottom — Safety sacrificed for speed-to-market
  • Public backlash — One major incident could halt progress
  • Legal chaos — Courts making up rules case-by-case
  • Ethical failures — Harm caused by good intentions
