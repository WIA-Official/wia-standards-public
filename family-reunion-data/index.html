Core Features

🔍

Family Search

Advanced search algorithms to find family members across databases, using names, dates, locations, and historical records with fuzzy matching and multilingual support.

🧬

DNA Matching

State-of-the-art genetic matching technology to identify biological relationships, including siblings, parents, children, and extended family members.

🗺️

Location Tracking

Historical and current location mapping to trace family migration patterns, refugee movements, and settlement histories across borders and time.

📸

Photo Recognition

AI-powered facial recognition to match family photos across decades, identify relatives from old photographs, and create visual family trees.

🔐

Privacy & Security

Military-grade encryption for sensitive family data, GDPR compliance, consent management, and secure data sharing protocols.

🤝

Reunion Coordination

End-to-end support for family reunions including contact facilitation, translation services, travel arrangements, and emotional support resources.

Use Cases

🇰🇷 Korean Separated Families

Support reunification efforts for families separated during the Korean War, managing registration data, DNA samples, and coordinating reunion events with North and South Korean authorities.

🌍 Global Refugee Families

Help refugee families separated during conflicts in Syria, Afghanistan, Ukraine, and other regions to locate and reconnect with loved ones across international borders.

🏛️ Historical Family Tracing

Enable descendants of Holocaust survivors, displaced indigenous populations, and victims of forced migrations to trace their ancestry and find living relatives.

👶 Adoption Searches

Facilitate international adoption reunions, helping adoptees find birth families and providing comprehensive family history documentation with ethical consent protocols.

💔 Missing Persons

Coordinate with law enforcement and humanitarian organizations to maintain databases of missing persons and facilitate family notifications when matches are found.

🏥 Medical Family History

Enable separated family members to share critical medical information, genetic health risks, and hereditary conditions for improved healthcare outcomes.
