Welcome to the Complete Guide

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about implementing family reunion search systems using the WIA-UNI-003 standard. From the fundamentals of separated family data management to advanced DNA matching and photo recognition technologies, this documentation provides essential knowledge for developers, humanitarian workers, and organizations working to reunite families.

The guide is organized into 8 chapters covering the emotional and technical aspects of family reunion, data architecture, search algorithms, DNA matching protocols, privacy considerations, and real-world case studies from Korean separated families, refugee reunions, and international adoption searches.

"Every family deserves to be reunited. Every story deserves to be told. Every heart deserves to heal."

— WIA-UNI-003 Mission Statement
