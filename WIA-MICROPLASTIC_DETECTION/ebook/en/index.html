About This Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on WIA-MICROPLASTIC_DETECTION, the global standard for microplastic detection, analysis, and environmental monitoring. This guide covers everything from fundamental concepts to advanced analytical techniques, providing scientists, researchers, policymakers, and environmental professionals with the knowledge and tools needed to combat microplastic pollution.

Microplastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. With an estimated 51 trillion microplastic particles in our oceans alone, standardized detection and monitoring are crucial for understanding and mitigating this global crisis. This guide presents the WIA standard approach to addressing this challenge.
