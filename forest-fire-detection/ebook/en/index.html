🌲 About This Standard

WIA-ENE-032 Forest Fire Detection Standards represent a comprehensive framework for early detection, monitoring, and response to wildfires using cutting-edge technology and proven methodologies. This standard integrates satellite remote sensing, ground-based sensor networks, artificial intelligence, and coordinated emergency response systems.

With climate change intensifying wildfire risks globally, this standard provides essential guidance for forest services, emergency managers, technology providers, and communities to implement effective fire detection systems that save lives, protect property, and preserve precious forest ecosystems.

This comprehensive guide covers the entire spectrum of forest fire detection—from understanding fire behavior and risk assessment, through satellite and ground-based detection technologies, to API integration, best practices, regional case studies, safety coordination, and future AI-powered prevention strategies.
