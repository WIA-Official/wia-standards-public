# DALL-E Image Prompts for WIA-COMP-016 (Software License Standard)

## Image 1: Main Hero - License Compliance Dashboard
**Style**: Modern, legal, professional
**Color Scheme**: Dark navy background (#0f172a), blue accents (#3B82F6), green compliance indicators
**Mood**: Professional, compliant, confident

**Prompt**:
```
A sophisticated 3D visualization of software license compliance on a dark navy (#0f172a) background. In the center, a large holographic dashboard displays an SPDX document with multiple software packages and their licenses beautifully organized. License badges (MIT, Apache-2.0, GPL-3.0, BSD) float in 3D space, each glowing in blue (#3B82F6). A compatibility matrix shows green checkmarks for compatible license combinations and prevents conflicts. Below, a dependency tree visualization shows hundreds of libraries with their respective licenses color-coded. On the right, a compliance report shows "100% Compliant" in green with automated license scanning results. License text excerpts float elegantly with copyright notices highlighted. A scale representing legal balance glows in the background. Professional, enterprise-grade aesthetic with strong emphasis on clarity and compliance.
```

## Image 2: Problem Visualization - License Compliance Nightmare
**Style**: Chaotic, confusing, legally risky
**Color Scheme**: Muted grays, red legal warnings, dark threatening tones
**Mood**: Risky, confusing, legally dangerous

**Prompt**:
```
A nightmarish scene of license compliance chaos. Center shows a stressed legal team surrounded by stacks of license agreements, each hundreds of pages long with conflicting terms highlighted in red. Multiple computer screens display dependency trees with unknown licenses marked with red warning triangles. A legal department receives cease-and-desist letters for license violations. GPL vs Apache license conflict diagram shows incompatible combinations with big red X marks. Copyright infringement lawsuit papers pile up. Calculator shows mounting legal fees ($500,000+). In background, proprietary software with expired licenses, orphaned dependencies with missing license info, and a confused developer trying to understand LGPL vs GPL differences. Compliance audit report shows "72 violations found" in alarming red. License compatibility checker crashed with error messages. Muted colors with aggressive red legal warnings everywhere. Documentary style showing corporate legal crisis.
```

## Image 3: Solution/Future - Automated License Management Paradise
**Style**: Bright, automated, compliant
**Color Scheme**: Bright blues, greens (compliance), whites, clean gradients
**Mood**: Confident, automated, legally safe

**Prompt**:
```
A beautiful automated license management command center in bright blue ambient lighting. Center shows a confident legal counsel and developer working together at a modern workstation with elegant SPDX compliance dashboard. The screen displays real-time license scanning: all dependencies automatically checked, compatibility verified with green indicators, SBOM (Software Bill of Materials) generated instantly. Left monitor shows automated license header insertion into all source files. Right monitor displays dependency licenses mapped in a beautiful circular graph - all compatible combinations shown in green, any potential conflicts caught early and highlighted for review. Background shows continuous compliance: every PR automatically scanned for license issues, legal notices auto-generated, attribution files created automatically. A timeline shows "From 3 months manual review → 3 minutes automated scan". License templates library available at a click. Everything clean, automated, stress-free. Team members relaxed, confident about legal compliance. Bright blue and green success indicators everywhere. Apple-style minimalist aesthetic celebrating legal clarity and peace of mind.
```

---

**Design Notes**:
- Image 1: Main hero - shows WIA-COMP-016 as comprehensive license management solution
- Image 2: Problem statement - emphasizes legal risks and complexity of license compliance
- Image 3: Future vision - demonstrates confidence and automation of proper license management
- Blue (#3B82F6) is the primary brand color for COMP category
- Green used for compliance and legal safety indicators
- Emphasize SPDX integration, automation, and legal confidence
- Balance technical and legal aspects
