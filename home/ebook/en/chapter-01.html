1.1 The Problem

Current Website Costs
=====================

To create a website today:

1. Domain purchase: $10-50/year
2. Hosting server: $5-100/month
3. SSL certificate: $0-200/year
4. Developer or learning time
5. Ongoing maintenance

= Minimum hundreds/year

Small business reality:
  [!] Dependent on Naver, Instagram, etc.
  [!] Platform changes policy? Adapt or die
  [!] Platform shuts down? Everything lost
  [!] Digital "renter" status


The Landlord Problem
====================

Traditional web hosting:

  You -> Cloud Provider -> Your Website
              |
         Pay rent monthly
         Follow their rules
         Hope they don't close

You never truly own your website.
You're just renting space in someone else's building.


Platform Dependency
===================

Instagram seller:
  [!] Algorithm changes = sales drop
  [!] Account banned = business over
  [!] Platform fee increases = margins shrink
  [!] No portability = trapped

Etsy/Amazon seller:
  [!] 15-30% commission
  [!] Reviews can be manipulated
  [!] No direct customer relationship
  [!] Competing with Amazon itself
