Key Features

🔬

Conformational Ensembles

Generate and analyze multiple protein conformations using AlphaFlow, Boltzmann Generators, and enhanced sampling MD. Predict populations and energy landscapes.

⏱️

Multi-Timescale Dynamics

Capture motions from picoseconds (bond vibrations) to milliseconds (large conformational changes). Unified metrics across all timescales.

🔗

Allosteric Networks

Map communication pathways between distant sites. Identify allosteric sites and predict long-range effects of mutations or ligand binding.

💊

Drug Binding Dynamics

Simulate binding pathways, calculate binding kinetics (kon/koff), predict residence times, and distinguish conformational selection from induced fit.

🌀

Intrinsically Disordered Regions

Characterize IDRs and their functional dynamics. Understand phase separation, liquid-liquid phase transitions, and disorder-to-order transitions.

🔌

Database Integration

Seamless integration with PDB, AlphaFold DB, UniProt, ChEMBL, and BindingDB. Export to standard formats and connect to drug discovery pipelines.
