About This Ebook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to the WIA-FIN-AML Anti-Money Laundering Standard. This ebook provides complete coverage of AML compliance—from foundational concepts to production-ready systems for detecting, preventing, and reporting financial crimes including money laundering, terrorist financing, and sanctions violations.

Developed by the World Certification Industry Association (WIA), this standard embodies the philosophy of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)—benefiting all humanity through transparent, secure, and compliant financial systems. Whether you're implementing KYC processes, building transaction monitoring systems, or ensuring regulatory compliance, WIA-FIN-AML provides proven patterns for success.

About WIA

The World Certification Industry Association (WIA) develops open standards for emerging technologies, promoting interoperability, security, and accessibility. Founded on the principle of 弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity), WIA brings together developers, enterprises, and regulators to create standards that serve everyone.

WIA-FIN-AML is part of the broader WIA Finance family, working alongside standards for blockchain finance, asset tokenization, and cryptocurrency to create a comprehensive framework for secure, compliant financial systems.
