Seawater Desalination

About This Standard

WIA-ENE-052 defines comprehensive standards for seawater desalination systems, covering reverse osmosis (RO), multi-stage flash (MSF), electrodialysis, brine management, energy recovery, and solar desalination technologies. This standard ensures efficient, sustainable, and safe conversion of seawater to fresh water for human consumption and agricultural use.

Key Features

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

High-efficiency membrane-based desalination with energy recovery systems

Multi-Stage Flash (MSF)

Thermal desalination process using flash evaporation stages

Electrodialysis

Ion-exchange membrane technology for brackish water treatment

Brine Management

Sustainable handling and disposal of concentrated brine byproduct

Energy Recovery

Advanced systems to minimize energy consumption and costs

Solar Desalination

Renewable energy-powered desalination for sustainable operation

Water Quality Monitoring

Real-time tracking of TDS, pH, and contaminant levels

Smart Integration

Seamless connection with water distribution systems
