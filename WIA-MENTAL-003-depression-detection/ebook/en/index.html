About This Ebook

This comprehensive guide explores the cutting-edge intersection of artificial intelligence, digital health, and mental healthcare, specifically focused on depression detection and monitoring. Depression affects over 280 million people worldwide, yet many cases go undiagnosed or untreated. This ebook presents evidence-based approaches to leverage modern technology for early detection, continuous monitoring, and improved patient outcomes.

Through eight detailed chapters, you'll learn about the latest AI algorithms, digital biomarkers, clinical assessment tools, and implementation strategies that are transforming mental healthcare. Each chapter includes real-world examples, technical implementations, ethical considerations, and practical guidelines for healthcare professionals, developers, and researchers.

弘益人間 (Benefit All Humanity)

This standard embodies the principle of 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) - "Benefit All Humanity." By making depression detection more accessible, accurate, and timely through technology, we can reduce suffering and save lives globally. Mental health is a fundamental human right, and these tools democratize access to early intervention and continuous care.
