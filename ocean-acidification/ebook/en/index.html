📖 About This Ebook

This comprehensive ebook covers the WIA Ocean Acidification Response Standard (WIA-ENE-054), a global framework for monitoring ocean pH levels, tracking marine ecosystem impacts, and coordinating mitigation efforts. Learn how to implement ocean acidification monitoring systems, integrate with marine research stations, and contribute to protecting our oceans for future generations.
